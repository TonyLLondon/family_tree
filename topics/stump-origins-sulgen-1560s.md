# The STUMP Origins in Sulgen: Multiple Families Established 1560s-1590s

The **STUMP surname** first appeared in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland** during the **1560s-1580s** when multiple patriarchs established families that would continue for over 280 years. This coordinated establishment of STUMP families during the late Renaissance period created one of the most extensively documented Swiss genealogies, spanning from the **1560s through the 1800s** with continuous parish records.
