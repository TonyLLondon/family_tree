### Deaths (Defunciones)

Deaths section for C was examined on pages ~480–490. Key findings:

- No death entry for Juana de Dios Cerpa González (covering ~1918–1932),
  suggesting she survived past the index range.
- Cerpa Alvear, Elba Mercedes appears in both births (~1923) and deaths
  (1926), indicating infant/child mortality.
