# Search progress: Johann Georg Knecht baptism (Bavaria, c. 1815)

**Date:** 2026-04-11  
**Objective:** Locate baptism record for Johann Georg Knecht, born c. 1815 in Bavaria (per 1864 naturalisation HO 1/117/4524)

---

## Evidence summary

### Primary source: 1864 Naturalisation Certificate

**HO 1/117/4524, memorial text (page 3):**
> "That I am a subject of **Wasserlos** in Bavaria and am a coach builder in the employ of the Great Western Railway Company - … forty nine years of age …"

**Facts established:**
- **Origin:** **Wasserlos**, Bavaria (Alzenau district, Unterfranken)
- **Age in 1864:** 49 → **born c. 1815**
- **Arrived UK:** c. 1852 (12 years by 1864)

### Conflicting census evidence

**1881/1891 Census:**
- Birthplace: **Hesse-Nassau**  
- Age inconsistencies: 52 (1881) → born c. 1829; 65 (1891) → born c. 1826

**Assessment:** Naturalisation is contemporary primary evidence where George stated his own origin. Census places were often approximate. Prefer 1815 birth year and Bavaria.
