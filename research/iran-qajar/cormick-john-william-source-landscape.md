## What the memo covers

Structured survey of **John Cormick** (d. 1833) and **William Cormick** (1822–1877): Irish-origin physicians in Qajar Iran; John’s service to **Abbas Mirza**, smallpox vaccination, Persian treatise (*Resāla-ye Taʿlīm*); William’s London training, return as royal/embassy physician, role at the **1848 Báb** trial, **Lion and Sun** (1875), death in Tabriz (1877).

## Source tiers noted in the PDF

- **Persian:** *Donya-e-Eqtesad* and related press/history writing; Dr Mahfouzi Museum material on John’s treatise; Persian Wikipedia entries on William Cormick and the Báb (secondary/tertiary).
- **English:** *Encyclopaedia Iranica* entries (John — Ekbal & Richter-Bernburg; William — Momen); English Wikipedia; Connections BMC blog; Browne’s *Materials* for William’s Báb account; Flannery (*Solas* 2004) cited as print-only.
