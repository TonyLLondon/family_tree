## FamilySearch collection notes

**Bavarian church records:** Over 2,500 collections cataloged. Most are available online or on microfilm. Searching requires knowing the specific parish. The Archives of Bavaria (Bayerisches Hauptstaatsarchiv München) holds most pre-1875 Catholic and Protestant records.

**English probate records:** England and Wales National Probate Calendar 1858-1957 is indexed and searchable on FamilySearch. Images are available but many are restricted to FamilySearch Centers (like George's 1898 probate).

**UK census 1841-1911:** Fully indexed and searchable by name + place + approximate birth year. Should search each census 1861-1891 for George Knecht household at known addresses (Canonbury, Paddington).
