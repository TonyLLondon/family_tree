## 29–64. Birth records (1707–1760, Addobbati / Lanfranchi / Plarinò)

The remaining 36 images are **birth/baptism records** spanning 1707–1760, almost entirely for children named **Addobbati**, **Lanfranchi**, or **Plarinò** born in Zara. They are narrative-format baptismal entries from the Venetian period.

### Summary of coverage:

| Surname | Count | Date range | Parishes |
|---------|-------|------------|----------|
| **Addobbati** | 23 births | 1731–1760 | Zara |
| **Lanfranchi** | 9 births | 1707–1717 | Zara |
| **Plarinò** | 2 births | 1734–1736 | Zara |
| **Carobbi** | 1 birth | 1745 (Segna) | Segna |
| **Castellanich** | 1 birth | 1799 | Zara |

### Key observations from sampled images:

- **Birth Valentino Pietro Addobbati Zara 1732:** Appears to be the earliest Addobbati child in the collection. Parents' names visible — this establishes the founding couple of the Zara Addobbati line in the 1730s.
- **Birth Luigi Addobbati Zara 1744:** Born to Daniele Addobbati and his wife (Lanfranchi?). This Luigi is likely the groom in the 1766 Marriage Luigi Addobbati Plarinò.
- **Birth Simone Sallustio Addobbati Zara 1751:** Additional Addobbati child from the Daniele × Tori (second wife) branch.
- **Birth Simeone Addobbati Zara 1760:** Another generation marker.
- **Birth Santa Lanfranchi Zara 1707:** The oldest image in the collection. Baptismal entry from December 1707. Marginal label "Danca" or "Danze". Multiple entries visible from Dec 1707. Archival labels: "FADED DOCUMENT", "SMEARED INK", "REPAIRED DOCUMENT."
- **Birth Giovanni Battista Carobbi Segna 1745:** Only non-Zara birth in the collection; from **Segna** (Senj). Establishes the Carobbi line's geographic origin outside Zara.

### What these births collectively provide:

Each baptismal entry contains: **child's given names**, **parents** (father + mother including maiden name), **godparents/sponsors**, **legitimacy status**, **baptism date** (sometimes a few days after birth), and **parish**. Across 36 entries they reconstruct:
- The **Daniele Addobbati** family of children (both marriages)
- The **Lanfranchi** family tree in Zara (pre-1720)
- The **Plarinò** sibling set
- Godparent networks linking these families
