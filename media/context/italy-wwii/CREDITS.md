All photographs are Crown Copyright expired (pre-1957) from the Imperial War Museum, via Wikimedia Commons.

- **british-troops-pachino-sicily-1943-iwm.jpg**: British troops advance through Pachino during the Allied invasion of Sicily, 10 July 1943. Major Geoffrey Keating, No. 2 Army Film & Photographic Unit. IWM NA 4411. Public domain.
- **anzio-beachhead-radio-operator-1944-iwm.jpg**: Wireless operator Bombardier S. Williams operates his radio from a shallow dugout on Anzio, 1944. Sgt Dawson, No. 2 Army Film & Photographic Unit. IWM NA 12374. Public domain.
- **gothic-line-aa-gun-bombarding-1944-iwm.jpg**: A 3.7-inch AA gun of 97th Heavy Anti-Aircraft Regiment (London Scottish) bombarding enemy positions on the Gothic Line, 2 September 1944. Sgt Dawson, No. 2 Army Film & Photographic Unit. IWM NA 18197. Public domain.

- **po-crossing-searchlight-mirror-1945-iwm.jpg**: Royal Artillery searchlight crew cleaning the mirror on the south bank of the Po, the pontoon bridge reflected in the glass, 30 April 1945. Alfred Reuben Tanner, Ministry of Information. IWM TR 2854. Crown Copyright expired, public domain.
