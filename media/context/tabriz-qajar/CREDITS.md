Flandin visited Tabriz in 1839–1841 as part of a French diplomatic mission — precisely the years Edward Burgess was living there. His lithographs depict the city Edward knew.

The Baskerville grave photos show the same Armenian cemetery where Edward Burgess was buried in 1855. Howard Baskerville (d. 1909) was interred there fifty-four years after Edward. The Shoghakat Church (1940) stands within the cemetery grounds.
