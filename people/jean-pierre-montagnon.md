# Jean Pierre Montagnon

Jean Pierre Montagnon was born around 1692 and died on **5 March 1737** at Aubonne in the Pays de Vaud. He married **Jeane Elizabeth Cusin** (b. 1685, d. 1753), also of Aubonne.

His son [Jean François Louis Montagnon](jean-francois-louis-montagnon.md), born the same year Jean Pierre died (~1737), married [Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard](jaqueline-suzanne-chenevard.md) in 1761 and is a direct ancestor of [Marc François Stump](marc-francois-stump.md).

## Parentage (unconfirmed)

Jean Pierre's parents have **not been confirmed by any record**. The probable father is [Jean François Montagnon](jean-francois-montagnon-1666.md) (b. ~1667, d. 15 Sep 1732, Aubonne) and the probable mother [Jeane Marechal](jeane-marechal.md) (b. 1665, d. 11 Jul 1742, Aubonne). The connection rests on circumstantial evidence only:

- Jean François was the **only senior Montagnon male** at Aubonne in the 1690s. His own baptism (as "François Montagnon," 22 Dec 1667, father: Jean Montagnon) is the earliest Montagnon event in the Aubonne registers.
- Jean Pierre (b. 1692), a second Jean François (b. 1694, d. 1778), and Jean Michel (b. 1699, d. 1772) all lived and died at Aubonne — the birth spacing (father aged 25, 27, 32) is consistent with a single household.
- Jean Pierre named his own son **François Louis** — combining his father's name "François" with "Louis," a pattern repeated in later generations.
- The Aubonne baptismal registers for the 1690s do not appear to be indexed by FamilySearch, which explains the absence of Jean Pierre's own baptism record.

The connection could be confirmed definitively by viewing the original register images at a FamilySearch Center.
