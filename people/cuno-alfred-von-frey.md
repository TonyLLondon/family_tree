# Cuno Alfred von Frey

Cuno Alfred von Frey was the husband of [Marguerite Emilie Stump](marguerite-emilie-stump.md); the export records their marriage on 11 August 1902 (the family chart also gives 1902). He was son-in-law of [Marc Francois Stump](marc-francois-stump.md) and [Olga Caroline Erbe](olga-caroline-erbe.md) and brother-in-law of [Étienne Stump](etienne-stump.md).
