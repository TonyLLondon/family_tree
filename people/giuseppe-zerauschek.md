# Giuseppe Zerauschek

Father of [Antonio Zerauschek (senior)](antonio-zerauschek-senior.md). The MyHeritage export links him to marriage **24 August 1850** in **Zara** with [Antonia Maria Guerino](antonia-maria-guerino.md) (**F58**) but does **not** carry a birth event in the short INDI block — typical when the researcher started from a marriage backward.

The Fulvia memoir (1996) says Giuseppe was **sent from Vienna to Zara to reorganize the town archives**, travelling in his own horse-drawn carriage — establishing him initially as an **archivist**. The 1878 marriage register of his son Antonio describes Giuseppe's occupation as **Cocchiere** (coachman) — both may be true at different life stages.

His patronymic confirms the GEDCOM patriline: the 1850 birth register of his son Antonio identifies the father as **"Zerauschek [qu.] Biagio, Giuseppe"** — Giuseppe, son of Biagio.

## Life

- **24 August 1850** — Marriage **Zara, Dalmatia** to Antonia Maria Guerino (**F58**). The 1850 marriage register confirms parents: father **Zerauschek Biagio** (*padre defunto* — deceased), mother **Maria Schinkovz** (*madre* — living at time of wedding).
- **BEF 26 Jan 1878** — Deceased. The 1878 marriage register of his son lists the groom's father as **"Biaggio Zerauschek"** (patronymic), **deceased**.
