# Engracia Fernandez

**Spouse:** Fabián Cerpa (1808-1888)  
**Children:** Francisco Cerpa (1859-1895), multiple others documented in Chilean civil records  

## Family

- **Spouse:** Fabián Cerpa (1808-1888), patriarch of documented CERPA family  
- **Children:** Francisco Cerpa → modern Chilean family, plus additional children in Talca region

## Significance

Engracia represents the **maternal line** connecting the documented CERPA lineage. Through her marriage to Fabián Cerpa, she links to the colonial ancestor **Justo Cerpa** (~1775) and the modern Chilean family.

## Evidence

**Multiple Chilean civil death records consistently show:**
- Francisco Cerpa death 1895 (Q241-8HV9): parents "Fabian Cerpa + Engracia Fernandez"
- Additional CERPA family records from Talca region confirm her role as mother
