# Georgina Elizabeth Fensom

**Georgina Elizabeth Fensom** was the eldest daughter of **David Fensom** (I202, labourer, b. ~1836, Harpenden, Hertfordshire) and **Georgiana Talbot Grindlay** (I203), born about **1862** in **Barnsbury**, the Islington district where the Fensom family was rooted for decades. She was a sister of **David William Fensom** (I206/I292, b. Oct 1864), whose daughter [Annie Fensom](annie-fensom-i291.md) married [Alfred Evans](alfred-evans-i101.md) in 1912.

The **1901 Census** found Georgina at age 38, **single**, recorded as an **inmate** of an institution in **Upper Holloway, Islington** — her occupation given as **domestic servant**. Her parents David and Georgiana were living nearby at **13 Eden Grove, Islington** in the same census.

On **31 March 1906** she married **Joseph Barnes** (I212).

## Family

Parents: David Fensom (I202) and Georgiana Talbot Grindlay (I203), married 18 June 1861. Siblings: [David William Fensom](annie-fensom-i291.md) (I206, b. Oct 1864), Ellen Fensom (I207), Fanny Fensom (I209), and others. Husband: Joseph Barnes (I212), married 31 March 1906.
