# James Glasscock (c. 1794–1863)

**James Glasscock** was a painter who spent his life in east London. He was baptised **4 January 1795** at **Stepney**, son of **John Glasscock** and **Sarah** (FMP: R_948638923). He married twice: first **Ann Coupley** on 20 November 1822 at Stepney, and after Ann's death, **Sarah Ann Holles** (b. ~1809, Bethnal Green) on **24 July 1826** at Stepney.

By the **1851 census** the household at **11 Satchwell Rents, Bethnal Green** (HO 107/1541, p. 19, schedule 90) included James (59, painter), wife Sarah (42), son James George (19, cabinet maker journeyman), and daughter [Eliza](eliza-knecht.md) — already widowed at 22 as "Eliza Thorbourn," with a three-year-old daughter Elizabeth Thorbourn.

James died in the **second quarter of 1863** at Bethnal Green (GRO death index; image FMP: BMD/D/1863/2/BK/000671/040 — not yet pulled).
