# Sarah Ann Quaife (~1828–?)

**Sarah Ann Quaife** was a **dressmaker** of **Cranbrook, Kent**, and wife of **[George Pope](george-pope-i296.md)**; she was named as Minnie’s mother on census returns cited under **[Minnie Flora Pope](minnie-pope-i293.md)**.
