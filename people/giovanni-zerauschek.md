# Giovanni Zerauschek (Zio Nini)

Brother of [Antonio Zerauschek](antonio-zerauschek.md). Known in the family as **Zio Nini**. Had **14 children** (seven survived). Ran a **small shipyard** in Zara, continuing the family tradition of boat-building.

After the fall of Zara, he did not leave at the right moment and was forced to stand a **Communist trial**. He served **eight months of hard labour** before being allowed to follow the rest of the family to Italy, eventually settling in **Sardinia** where he continued building boats.

His son **Ferruccio** emigrated to Australia (his own son played in the **national basketball team**). Another son, **Bruno**, went to South America. (Fulvia memoir, 1996.)
