# Giovanni Zerauschek (Zio Nini)

**Zio Nini**—**Giovanni** in the registers—kept the family’s hand on wood and water: a **small shipyard** in Zara, sons enough to people a novella (**fourteen** born, **seven** lived). After the **1945** transfer he missed the window for clean exit, stood a **Communist trial**, and served **eight months of hard labour** before Italy would take him; he finished in **Sardinia**, still building boats.

**Ferruccio** went to **Australia** — Fulvia notes a grandson on the **national basketball team**. **Bruno** crossed to **South America**. The memoir scatters the other names like debris from a busy dock (1996).
