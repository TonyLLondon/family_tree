# Sarah Airey

**Sarah Airey** was born **22 February 1741** at **Kendal**, Westmorland, into the Quaker meeting there. Her father was [Joseph Airey](joseph-airey.md) and her mother [Sarah Grave](sarah-grave.md). She married [Joseph Burgess](joseph-burgess.md) on **6 February 1769** at the Kendal Monthly Meeting and was mother of [Henry Burgess](henry-burgess.md). She lived to **1828**.
