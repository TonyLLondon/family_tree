# Dr. Jerko Machiedo

Physician from **Hvar**, **Croatian Party** deputy in the **Diet of Dalmatia**, **deputy president of the Dalmatian provincial government in Zadar** from **1910**, later **Šibenik** hospital director, returned to Hvar in retirement; significant **WWII** and **post-1945** medical service on Hvar (treating wounded partisans; continued practice). **Paternal uncle** of **[Tatiana Machiedo](tatiana-machiedo.md)** — his brother was **[Juraj-Zorko Machiedo](juraj-zorko-machiedo.md)**.

Standard overviews: [English Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jerko_Machiedo); [Croatian Wikipedia](https://hr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jerko_Machiedo).
