# Ann Braithwaite

**Ann Brathwaite** was born **21 September 1663** at **Swarthmoor**, Lancashire, daughter of [George Brathwaite](george-brathwaite.md) and [Agnes Benson](agnes-benson.md). She married [Joseph Ayrey](joseph-ayrey.md) on **6 October 1692** at the Grayrigg Quaker Meeting, Kendal, Westmorland, and was mother of [Joseph Airey](joseph-airey.md).

The GEDCOM export incorrectly names her father as Richard Braithwaite (the poet's son). The original Quaker register clearly reads "Ann dau of **George** Brathwaite & **Agnes** his wife." George was baptised at Hawkshead in 1637, son of William Brathwaite and Sibell Mackreth; Agnes was daughter of **Barnard Benson** of Longriggs (Quaker marriage register, 1666). The Brathwaites of the Swarthmoor Monthly Meeting may be related to the poet Richard Brathwait's Burneside/Ambleside family — a **James Brathwaite** appears in the same register in 1665 — but the direct line through a son "Richard" is not supported by primary evidence.

## Open questions

- **Brathwaite–poet connection:** Whether George's father William Brathwaite of Hawkshead connects to the poet Richard Brathwait's Burneside/Ambleside family remains unproven. A James Brathwaite appears in the same Swarthmoor register (1658, father also James, mother Catharin), and the poet had a brother James, but this is circumstantial.
- The export's conflicting dates (born 1669 vs christened 8 Mar 1662/3 at Wensley, Yorks) are both wrong — the Quaker register gives 21 Sep 1663 at Swarthmoor.
