# Margaret Mainwaring

**Margaret** (maiden name unknown) was born about **1785** and married **Evan Mainwaring** (b. ~1781), a **mason** in the Swansea area. The couple married and were buried at **St Mary's, Swansea**.

Their daughter [Ann Mainwaring](ann-mainwaring.md) was baptised **16 July 1826** at St Mary's — the register names her parents as Evan (mason) and Margaret. The **1841 census** at Swansea recorded Evan (~56) with children Rachel, John, Martha, and William; Ann (15) was probably already in service.

Margaret died in **Q4 1841** (GRO Vol 20 p. 282) and was buried at **St Mary's, Swansea**. The National Burial Index and Glamorgan Burial Index both record her birth year as ~1785.

## Family

- Husband: Evan Mainwaring (b. ~1781, mason; d. ~1848, Swansea; buried St Mary's).
- Children (from 1841 census and Ann's baptism): [Ann](ann-mainwaring.md) (1826), Rachel, John, Martha, William.
