# Hanss Ulrich Jung (early 1700s)

**Hans Ulrich Jung** was a Swiss man from **Sulgen**, **Thurgau** in the early 18th century. He married [Margreth Mockh](margreth-mockh.md) and became the father of [Anna Margaretha Jung](anna-margaretha-jung.md) (baptized 1731), who later married into the STUMP family as the wife of [Hans Konrad Stump](hs-konrad-stump.md).

## Life

**c. 1700** — Born in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland** (estimated from children's birth dates).

**c. 1725** — Marriage to [Margreth Mockh](margreth-mockh.md).

**5 Apr 1731** — Birth of daughter [Anna Margaretha Jung](anna-margaretha-jung.md) in Sulgen.
