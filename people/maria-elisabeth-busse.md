# Maria Elisabeth Busse

Maria Elisabeth Busse was baptised on **28 March 1779** at the Lutheran church in **Viljandi (Fellin)**, the daughter of [Ernst Gottlieb Busse](ernst-gottlieb-busse.md) and [Anna Christina Schittmar](anna-christina-schittmar.md). Her baptism record names both parents explicitly, resolving the earlier open question about which of Ernst Gottlieb's two wives was her mother.

She married [Johan Jacob Eylandt](johan-jacob-eylandt.md) on **27 December 1799** in Viljandi — the same town where she was born and where her father-in-law [Carl Gustav Eylandt](carl-gustav-eylandt.md) would later die in 1826. The couple moved to **Tallinn**, where they raised at least eight children between 1800 and 1820. Maria Elisabeth died there on **23 February 1823**, aged about 43, when her daughter [Emilie Ida](emilie-ida-eylandt.md) was only eleven.
