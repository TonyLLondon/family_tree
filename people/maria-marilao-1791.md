## Indigenous Heritage & Tribal Affiliation

**MARILAO** is documented in **6,325+ Chilean records** from 1769 onwards, representing one of the most prominent **indigenous surnames** in central Chile.

### Linguistic Analysis
From Mapudungun (Mapuche language):
- **Mari** = "ten" or "complete/perfect"  
- **Lao/Lahuen** = "medicine/healing plant" or "remedy"
- **Combined meaning**: "Woman of the complete medicine" or "Perfect healer"

### Alternative Etymology  
- **Marilao** could derive from "Mariluan" = "ten guanacos" (traditional wealth measure)
- Or from "Mari" + "lawün" = "ten remedies" (medicinal knowledge keeper)
