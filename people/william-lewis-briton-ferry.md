# William Lewis

**William Lewis** lived at **Briton Ferry**, Glamorganshire, on the west bank of the River Neath. He is known from the baptism of his son [David Lewis](david-lewis-briton-ferry.md), born **7 January 1794** at Briton Ferry, and from David's 1812 marriage record which identifies the father.

William's wife was [Catherine Rees](catherine-rees-briton-ferry.md). Their grandson [Lewis Lewis](lewis-lewis.md) (b. 1813) — the doubled Welsh patronymic reflecting the shift from patronymic to fixed surnames — carried the family into the nineteenth-century industrial towns of Port Talbot and Aberavon.
