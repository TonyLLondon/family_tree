# Margreth Mockh (early 1700s)

**Margreth Mockh** was a Swiss woman from **Sulgen**, **Thurgau** who married [Hans Ulrich Jung](hanss-ulrich-jung.md) in the early 18th century. She became the mother of [Anna Margaretha Jung](anna-margaretha-jung.md) (baptized 1731), who later married [Hans Konrad Stump](hs-konrad-stump.md) and continued the STUMP family lineage.

## Life

**10 Jan 1706** — Christened **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. Daughter of [Johannes Mockh](johannes-mockh-1662.md) and [Margaretha Schoenholtzer](margaretha-schoenholtzer.md).

**c. 1725** — Marriage to [Hans Ulrich Jung](hanss-ulrich-jung.md).

**5 Apr 1731** — Birth of daughter [Anna Margaretha Jung](anna-margaretha-jung.md) in Sulgen.
