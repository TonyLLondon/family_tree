# D. Daniele Addobbati

**D. Daniele Addobbati** is the man who planted the Zara branch—one of three brothers who crossed from the Venetian Terraferma, traded cuirassier service for urban oaths, and married the daughter of a Lanfranchi before the council book admitted them as *cittadini originari* in 1733. The initial “D.” is the honorific *don* of Dalmatian registers, not a doctorate; the title that mattered here was *cittadino*.

His first marriage to Margherita Lanfranchi on 22 November 1730 was celebrated under archiepiscopal dispensation: Canon Petrus Bado, acting for Archbishop Zmajević, joined him to the daughter of the late Francesco Lanfranchi while witnesses with names like Armanini and Pilarino knitted the new household into old Zara kindreds. By the early 1730s baptisms in the city register multiply the surname; the formal admission of 1733 merely catches up to a family already in the contado—Turanj registers hint at presence by 1728.

Widowed in early 1749, he married again within weeks—12 March 1749—to Laura Tori, daughter of the late Salustio Tori, *nobile senese*. The Tori arms (vairy argent and gules) and Sienese archives give Tuscan minor nobility to what had been, a generation earlier, artisan-officer stock from Bergamo. From Laura’s father’s name came *Simone Sallustio* in 1751, a thread Sabalich and the parish books draw through later Simon Salustios. Five sons received Zara citizenship attests in the printed tradition; the structured tree presently holds only Luigi from the first marriage, while civic notices and a gallery of baptism images crowd the evidence section with siblings waiting on transcription.

## Life

- **1702** — Born (year from tree; likely Bergamo orbit before Dalmatian settlement).
- **By 1728** — Addobbati in Turanj registers (DAZD inventory); may be Daniele or a brother.
- **22 Nov 1730** — Marriage Zara to [Margherita Lanfranchi](margherita-lanfranchi.md); Canon Petrus Bado; banns omitted by faculty; witnesses Francesco Armanini, Pietro Pilarino.
- **1731** — Two Addobbati births Zara (DAZD inventory).
- **13 Nov 1733** — Admitted as *cittadini originari* with brothers Lorenzo (1705) and Giuseppe (1709).
- **Early 1749** — Death of Margherita (see register image).
- **12 Mar 1749** — Second marriage Zara to Laura Tori (*nobile senese*); Don Pietro Garo, Primicerio; witnesses Giam Battista Lorenzo Adobatti, Girolamo Lantana; scribe Don Giovanni Frangipani.
- **Posterity** — Five sons with citizenship attest per Sabalich; the current tree emphasizes Luigi (b. 1744, first marriage); additional sons from both marriages in parish captures below.

## Family

- Wife (2nd): **Laura Tori**, daughter of the late Salustio Tori; married 12 Mar 1749; son **Simone Sallustio** b. 1751 and others per registers.
- Father: [D. Ioanes Addobbati](d-ioanes-addobbati.md).
- Children: [Luigi Anzolo Alloisio Addobbati](luigi-anzolo-alloisio-addobbati.md); at least four further sons attested in civic tradition (Lorenzo, Daniele, Giuseppe lines in Sabalich); full list pending from images.
- Brothers (not in same tree slice): **Lorenzo Addobbati** (1705); **Giuseppe Addobbati** (1709) — co-admitted 1733.

### Brother Lorenzo's line

Lorenzo married **Anzola Plavino**; son **Zuanne** b. Zara 1731 (DAZD §2.2). Grimani investiture at Nadin (1756); plague deputacy at Dobropolci (1770); son Giuseppe *Capitano delle Craine* (1770). Notary **Angelo Addobbati q.m Lorenzo** (DAZD sig. 56); Dominican **Padre Daniele** (Sabalich p. 504).
