# D. Daniele Addobbati

Father of [Luigi Anzolo Alloisio Addobbati](luigi-anzolo-alloisio-addobbati.md) in **F16** with [Margherita Lanfranchi](margherita-lanfranchi.md). **"D."** = **Don** (honorific) in Dalmatian Catholic registers. Progenitor of the Zara Addobbati branch — one of three brothers who crossed the Adriatic from the Venetian Terraferma in 1733.

## Life

- **1702** — Born (GEDCOM year only; likely Bergamo or northern Italy based on family chain).
- **13 Nov 1733** — Admitted to the Zara council of citizens (*cittadini originari*) alongside brothers **Lorenzo** and **Giuseppe**, sons of Giovanni (Ivan) Addobbati. They arrived as Venetian cavalry officers carrying the 1745 Bergamo testimonial documenting six generations of *cives originarios* lineage from Pietro de Adobatis (1495).
- Settled in Zara; married **Margherita Lanfranchi** (**F16**); father of Luigi Anzolo Alloisio (**I30**).

## Family

- Wife: [Margherita Lanfranchi](margherita-lanfranchi.md) (**F16**).
- Father: [D. Ioanes Addobbati](d-ioanes-addobbati.md) (**F17** lists **@I32@** as **HUSB** and **@I31@** as **CHIL**; **no WIFE** line in **F17** in this export).
- Children include [Luigi Anzolo Alloisio Addobbati](luigi-anzolo-alloisio-addobbati.md) per **F16**.
- Brothers (not in GEDCOM): **Lorenzo Addobbati** and **Giuseppe Addobbati** — co-admitted to Zara *cittadini* 1733.
