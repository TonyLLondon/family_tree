# Margaretha Elisabeth Bischanz

Margaretha Elisabeth Bischanz was the daughter of [Hinrich Bischanz](hinrich-bischanz.md). She married [Georg Busse](georg-busse.md) on **9 February 1744** in Tallinn — her second marriage, as Geni text describes her as "verw. Rippel" (widow Rippel), previously married to **Johann Wilhelm Rippel**, with whom she had children Daniel, Johann Wilhelm, and Christian Ludewig Rippel.

Her name appears in multiple baptism records of children with Georg Busse, spelled variously as "Margr Elis Bischantz," "Margar Elisab Byschantz," and "Margaretha Elisab Bischanz" — standard orthographic variation in Baltic German church registers. Their son [Ernst Gottlieb Busse](ernst-gottlieb-busse.md) was born in 1748.
