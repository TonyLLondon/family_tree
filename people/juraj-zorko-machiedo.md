# Juraj-Zorko Machiedo

Mining engineer; younger brother of **[Dr. Jerko Machiedo](jerko-machiedo.md)**. Father of **[Tatiana Machiedo](tatiana-machiedo.md)** (*Tatjana-Tanja*) and **[Sergije Makiedo](sergije-makiedo.md)**.

## Life (from published genealogy)

Per **Kovačić 2014**, born **1879**, died **Zagreb 1963**. Studied **mining engineering in Graz** (then “Štajerski Gradac”); worked first for the **Dalmatian government**, later **in private practice**.

## Family

- Parents: **Lauro Machiedo** (1851–1918) and **Magdalena “Mandina” Novak** “Bonaparte” (1855–1920, Zadar/Arbanasi) — *no separate `people/` stubs*.
- Siblings: **Jerko**, **Ružica**, **Kreuza** (Kovačić).
- Wife: **[Graziella Nakic d’Osljak](graziella-nakic-d-osljak.md)**.
- Children: **Maksim** (1907–1917, Florence), **Sergije**, **Tatjana-Tanja** (later [Zerauschek](riccardo-zerauschek.md)), **Isabella “Cinci”** (later Capanna).
