# Brynmor Keith Lewis

**Keith** was the **little brother** in the **Lewis** household that produced **[David](david-john-lewis.md)**—born in **Merthyr** in **1934**, nine years after David’s arrival, when Glamorgan was still a landscape of **coal smoke**, chapel debt, and the fragile arithmetic of large families. The 1943 letter David typed from Sicily addresses him directly as the **nine-year-old** “improvement on the other red you had about the house”—affection armoured in teasing. The **1911** fertility return discussed in the coalfield story touches earlier generations; Keith’s own adult trail in this vault is still thin, waiting on census waves and family papers.
