# Anna Marstetter (c. 1645)

**Anna Marstetter** was a Swiss woman from **Sulgen**, **Thurgau** in the mid-17th century. She married [Hans Jung](hanss-jung.md) and became the mother of [Johannes Jung](johannes-jung-1667.md) (baptized 1667), establishing the maternal lineage in Generation 11 that would eventually connect to the STUMP family genealogy.
