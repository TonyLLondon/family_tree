# Keziah Morgan

**Keziah Morgan** was born **7 March 1824** at **Nantyglo**, a hamlet in the parish of Aberystruth, western Monmouthshire, in the heart of the south Wales ironworks valleys. He was [baptised](../media/docs/1824-baptism-keziah-morgan-aberystruth-monmouthshire.jpg) on **4 July 1824** at Aberystruth parish church. The baptismal register gives his father as **Thomas Morgan** (miner, Nantyglo) and his mother as **Charlotte Davies**. Thomas and Charlotte had [married](../sources/corpus/1823-marriage-thomas-morgan-charlotte-davies-aberystruth/transcription.md) at Aberystruth on **5 July 1823** — Keziah was born exactly eight months later.

By the late 1840s Keziah had moved to the Merthyr Tydfil area and married [Jane](jane-morgan-dowlais.md) (b. ~1828, Dowlais). He worked as a **collier** in the Dowlais coalfield and the family lived on **Pond Street**. The [1871 census](../media/docs/1871-census-keziah-morgan-household-dowlais-rg10-5397.jpg) records him at 44, head, collier, born Blaina, with Jane (43) and six children including [Mary](mary-morgan.md) (17).

Keziah disappears from Merthyr Tydfil records after 1871. A Keziah Morgan (b. ~1824) was buried at **Llangeinor** (Ogmore Valley) and a Kezia Morgan (b. ~1823) died Q4 1905 at Bridgend — the same registration district — suggesting he may have moved to the Ogmore coal valleys.
