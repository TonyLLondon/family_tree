# David John Lewis (1857)

**Samuel Lewis's father** (**@I173@**), distinct from his grandson [David John Lewis](david-john-lewis.md) (**@I8@**, b. **1918**).

**Line narrative:** [Lewis in Aberdare and Merthyr — coalfield labour and child mortality](../narratives/lewis-aberdare-merthyr-coalfield.md) — Gelligaer childhood, South Wales Borderers **1082**, Aberdare mining household to **1925**. The GEDCOM bundles civil birth index, parish baptism, decade runs of **1871–1911** census household extracts, **South Wales Borderers** service (**1082**, **1884**), and an **England & Wales** death index pointer (**May 1925**, Merthyr).

## Life

- **~1857** — Birth **Aberdare** area (GEDCOM "ABT 1857"); civil index cites **volume 11A, page 331, line 107** (births).
- **11 Aug 1857** — Baptism **Aberafon, Glamorganshire**; batch **C02533-2** (Wales Births & Baptisms collection text in GEDCOM).
- **1871–1911** — Household snapshots **Gelligaer / Aberdare**, Merthyr district, with parents as heads in **1871** and **1881**, then as head himself from **1891** onward (pieces **RG10/5388**, **RG11/5308**, **RG12/4444**, **RG13/5035**, **RG14/32495** schedule **77**—same schedule as son [Samuel Lewis](samuel-lewis.md) in **1911**).
- **1884** — Military event: **South Wales Borderers**, service number **1082** (GEDCOM).
- **~Feb 1884** — Marriage **Merthyr** to [Catherine Griffiths](catherine-griffiths.md) (**F87**); civil index **volume 11A, page 611**, lines **174** and **330** duplicated in source text (transcription quirk in export).
- **Occupation** — **Coal miner** in **1891** snapshot (same household row as later Samuel narratives).
- **~May 1925** — Death **Merthyr**; civil deaths **volume 11A, page 814, line 110**.
