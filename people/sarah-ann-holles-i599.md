# Sarah Ann Holles (~1809–?)

**Sarah Ann Holles** was born about **1809** at **Bethnal Green**. She married **James Glasscock** (painter of Stepney) on **24 July 1826** at **St Dunstan Stepney**; their line runs to **[Eliza Sarah Glasscock](eliza-knecht.md)** and **[Dorothea Knecht](dorothea-knecht.md)** — hence to **[William Evans](william-evans-i289.md)** and **[Alfred Evans](alfred-evans-i101.md)**.

Narrative detail: [Eliza Sarah Glasscock](eliza-knecht.md).
