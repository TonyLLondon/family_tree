# George Coolbear (~1823–?)

**George Coolbear** (indexed variants **Coalbear** / **Colbear**) was a **journeyman carpenter** from **Hazeleigh, Essex**. He married **Ann Gridley** from **Tillingham**; their son **[George Gridley Coolbear](george-gridley-coolbear-i105.md)** (1844–1892) continues the line to **[May Coolbear](may-coolbear-i100.md)** and the London **Evans** family.

The 1841 census placed teenage George and Ann in **Bicknacre, Danbury**; 1851 has the family at **Butt Lane, Maldon** with young **George Gridley** and siblings. See narrative on [George Gridley Coolbear](george-gridley-coolbear-i105.md).
