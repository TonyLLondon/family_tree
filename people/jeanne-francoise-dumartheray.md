# Jeanne Françoise Dumartheray

Jeanne Françoise Dumartheray was born in **1775** and baptised **25 March 1775** in the Canton de Vaud. Her parents were [Jean Gabriel Nicolas Dumartheray](jean-gabriel-nicolas-dumartheray.md) (father: Jaques Dumartheray) and [Jeanne Elisabeth Borel](jeanne-elisabeth-borel.md) (father: Gabriel Borel), confirmed by the family register ([6JCK-2SH2](https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:6JCK-2SH2)).

She married [Louis Montagnon](louis-montagnon.md) and their daughter [Jeanne Bénigne Montagnon](jeanne-benigne-montagnon.md) was baptised 15 July 1805 in Vaud. Jeanne Bénigne later married [Hans Jacob Stump](hans-jacob-stump-1800.md) at Erlen, Thurgau in 1832.
