# Peter Lewis

Peter was born in Chipata, in Zambia’s Eastern Province, on Bastille Day 1953—British by nationality in the record, African by geography in the birth line. His parents were [David John Lewis](david-john-lewis.md) and [Fulvia Ottilia Antonia Zerauschek](fulvia-ottilia-antonia-zerauschek.md), the Italian–Zadar mother who would later address her 1996 family memoir to him under the fuller form **Peter Martin Lewis**. Older chart labels sometimes shorten the middle name to Morgan; the memoir spelling is the one that matches how the letter opens.

The vault’s narrative for his generation still hangs on that memoir and on the bare vitals the tree imports. His story belongs as much to post-war movement between Wales, Italy, and Central Africa as to any single parish register.
