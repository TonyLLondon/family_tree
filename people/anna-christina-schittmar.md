# Anna Christina Schittmar

Anna Christina Schittmar was born about **1757** in Estonia and married [Ernst Gottlieb Busse](ernst-gottlieb-busse.md). She died around **1790** in **Viljandi County**, after which Ernst remarried. Her mother in the working tree is [Anna Christina](anna-christina.md) (born ~1730, Tallinn); no father is named in the parental union **F147**.

The baptism record of her daughter [Maria Elisabeth Busse](maria-elisabeth-busse.md) -- Viljandi, 28 March 1779 -- names the mother as "Anna Christiane Schittmar," providing primary-source confirmation that Anna Christina was Maria Elisabeth's biological mother and was alive in Viljandi in 1779.

A sibling, **Christina Elisabeth Schittmar**, was baptised on **25 April 1759** at Viljandi -- two years after Anna Christina's estimated birth. The baptism record names the father only as "Schittmar" and the mother as "Anne," consistent with Christian Friedrich Schittmar and Anna Christina (the elder) as parents of both daughters. See [corpus reference](../sources/corpus/fs-estonia-church-books-eylandt-busse/reference.md).
