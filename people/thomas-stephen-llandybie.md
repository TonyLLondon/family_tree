# Thomas Stephen

**Thomas Stephen** lived at **Llandybie**, a rural parish in north-east Carmarthenshire. He is known from the baptism of his son [William Stephen](william-stephen-llandybie.md), baptised **9 May 1790** at Llandybie parish church — the register names the father as Thomas Stephen.

William went on to farm at nearby Llanarthne, where his daughter [Rachel](rachel-james.md) was baptised in 1825. Rachel married [Lewis Lewis](lewis-lewis.md) of Briton Ferry in 1856.
