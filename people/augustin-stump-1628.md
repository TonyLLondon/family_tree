# Augustin Stump (b. 1628)

**Augustin Stump** (baptized 22 December 1628) was the son of [Jacob Stump](jacob-stump-1606.md) and [Barbel Cuntz](barbel-cuntz.md) in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. He lived an exceptional 84 years (1628-1713), married [Barbel Rutershausser](barbel-rutershausser.md), and became the father of [Caspar Stump](caspar-stump-1662.md), representing Generation 13 in the Swiss STUMP lineage during the early 17th to early 18th century.

## Life

**22 Dec 1628** — Christened **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. Son of [Jacob Stump](jacob-stump-1606.md) and [Barbel Cuntz](barbel-cuntz.md).

**c. 1650** — Marriage to [Barbel Rutershausser](barbel-rutershausser.md).

**27 Nov 1662** — Birth of son [Caspar Stump](caspar-stump-1662.md) in Sulgen (Augustin age 33).

**30 Aug 1713** — Died **Sulgen** at age 84. Buried in Sulgen parish after remarkable longevity for the period.
