# Rosina A Martin

**Rosina Martin** was born in **1891** at **Orpington, Kent**, the youngest child and only daughter of **Peter Martin**, a **master chimney sweep**, and **Clara Webb** of Chelsfield. The 1891 Census captures her as an infant at 1 Alfrons Cottages, Frog Lane, Orpington, alongside seven brothers — three of them already chimney sweeps. She married **[Archibald Percy Coolbear](archibald-percy-coolbear.md)** on **28 October 1911**; Archibald was also a chimney sweep, almost certainly a trade connection through the Martin family. Her name sits on **Southend** and **Holloway** next-of-kin lines in her husband's **AIR 79** file. Mother of **[May](may-coolbear-i100.md)** and at least three other children.

## Family

- Father: **Peter Martin** (b. ~1840, Sevenoaks or Woolwich, Kent) — master chimney sweep.
- Mother: **Clara Webb** (b. ~1854, Chelsfield, Kent) — originally Peter's housekeeper; listed as unmarried in 1881 with children surnamed Webb; by 1891 listed as Peter's wife with children re-surnamed Martin.
- Half-siblings (Peter's first marriage to **Catherine Hosten**, m. Q1 1857): James (~1859, Bletchingley, Surrey), Samuel (~1862), Catherine (~1866, Orpington), Emma (~1868, Orpington), Peter Jr (~1870, Orpington).
- Full siblings (Peter & Clara): Arthur (~1877), William (~1879), Albert (~1881), Harry (~1884), Edward (~1888).
- Husband: [Archibald Percy Coolbear](archibald-percy-coolbear.md) (m. 28 Oct 1911).
- Children: Archibald Jr (1912), [May](may-coolbear-i100.md) (1916), Henry Charles (1917), Irene (1921).

**GEDCOM correction:** The GEDCOM incorrectly lists Rosina's parents as Thomas Martin (I229) and Elizabeth Moran (I230), with a birth date of 12 June 1892 in Islington. The 1891 census (taken April 1891) already shows infant Rosina with Peter Martin and Clara Webb in Orpington, and the 1921 census (Rosina's own statement) gives her birth year as 1891 and birthplace as Orpington, Kent. The GEDCOM parents are a different family.

## Open questions

- **1901 Census gap:** The entire Martin family is missing. Peter Martin (chimney sweep) may have died between 1891 and 1901; Clara may have remarried or scattered the children. A death index search for Peter Martin ~1891–1901 in the Bromley/Dartford district would clarify.
- **Peter Martin's first wife:** Died between ~1872 (Peter Jr born) and ~1876 (before Clara arrived). Name unknown. Death record would be in the Bromley/Dartford district.
- **Peter's birthplace discrepancy:** Sevenoaks (1881) vs Woolwich (1891). A baptism or birth record would resolve.
- **Clara Webb's parents:** Born ~1854, Chelsfield, Kent. Not yet traced.
