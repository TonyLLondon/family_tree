# Wife of Abraham Glascock

The structured tree holds **only** the marriage union to **[Abraham Glascock](abraham-glascock-i604.md)** (~1703–~1738); her given name and vitals are still pulled from the **1733 marriage register** and allied Hertfordshire work cited under **[Eliza Sarah Glasscock](eliza-knecht.md)**. She is mother of **[Abraham Glasscock](abraham-glasscock-i602.md)** (bapt. 1735).
