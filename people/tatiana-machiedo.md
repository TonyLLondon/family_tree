# Tatiana Machiedo (née Machiedo; Zerauschek)

Wife of [Riccardo Zerauschek](riccardo-zerauschek.md). The vault previously recorded her only from the 1951 IRO file and Fulvia’s 1996 memoir (birth **16 March 1912**, **Zara**; Dalmatian Italian family; brother described as lawyer / young judge / communist). **Published genealogy ties her to the documented Hvar–Zadar Machiedo lineage** (see Evidence).

## Life (synthesis)

Daughter of **[Juraj-Zorko Machiedo](juraj-zorko-machiedo.md)** and **[Graziella Nakic d’Osljak](graziella-nakic-d-osljak.md)**. Siblings in the same reconstruction: **Maksim** (Zadar 1907 – Florence 1917), **[Sergije Makiedo](sergije-makiedo.md)** (law doctorate, judge, partisan, Yugoslav diplomat and UN official — [HBL](https://hbl.lzmk.hr/clanak/makiedo-sergije)), and **Isabella “Cinci”** (Zadar 1915; later Capanna). Fulvia’s “communist judge” brother is **Sergije** — not an unrelated Machiedo.

**Relationship to Dr. Jerko Machiedo:** **[Dr. Jerko Machiedo](jerko-machiedo.md)** was her **paternal uncle** (brother of Juraj-Zorko); both were sons of **[Lauro Machiedo](lauro-machiedo.md)** and **[Magdalena Novak](magdalena-novak-machiedo.md)** “Bonaparte.”

Marriage to Riccardo Zerauschek and children **Nora**, **Renato**, **Luciano** remain as on [Riccardo’s page](riccardo-zerauschek.md).
