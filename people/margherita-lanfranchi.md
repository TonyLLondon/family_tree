# Margherita Lanfranchi

Full baptismal name **Regina Margaritta**. Wife of [D. Daniele Addobbati](d-daniele-addobbati.md) in **F16** (married **22 Nov 1730**, Zara); mother of [Luigi Anzolo Alloisio Addobbati](luigi-anzolo-alloisio-addobbati.md). Died **1749**, Zara — Daniele remarried (to Tori) that same year.

## Life

- **26 Dec 1715** — Born **Zara, Dalmatia**, daughter of **Francesco Lanfranchi** and **Margaritta Doriceo**. The infant was in danger of dying at birth: an emergency home baptism was performed on **2 Jan 1716** (registered as "2 Geno: 1715" in the Venetian *More Veneto* calendar, where the new year began on 1 March). The curate Don Giorgio Bonicelli baptized her at home "per causa d'imminente necessità" and gave her the name **Regina Margaritta**. Godfather: **Giovanni Maria Canova** (surname confirmed by the 1732 Plarinò × Canova marriage register; previously misread as "Canovia"). The full church ceremonies were deferred and the note was formally entered into the Duomo register on **3 Mar 1716**.
- **22 Nov 1730** — Marriage **Zara** to [D. Daniele Addobbati](d-daniele-addobbati.md) (**F16**). She was 14 years old. The Latin marriage register describes her as "filiam quondam Francisci Lanfranchi" — her father Francesco was already dead (alive at her 1716 baptism, dead by 1730). Canon Petrus Bado officiated under licence from Archbishop Vincenzo Zmajević; banns were dispensed ("Omissis Publicationibus"). Witnesses: Francesco Armanini and Pietro Pilarino. Daniele had arrived from the Venetian Terraferma (Bergamo origin); their marriage predates the formal 1733 *cittadini* admission by three years.
- **1749** — Died **Zara**. The death register image (`Death Margherita Lanfranchi Addobbati Zara 1749.jpg`) records her death; Daniele remarried to Tori later that year.

## Family

- Husband: [D. Daniele Addobbati](d-daniele-addobbati.md) (**F16**).
- Father: **Francesco Lanfranchi**, from the **Diocese of Bergamo** (Lombardy) — confirmed by his 1707 marriage record. Died before Nov 1730 ("quondam" in Margherita's marriage register; alive at her 1716 baptism). The 1707 marriage record names the bride as **Francesca** Doriceo (daughter of the late Giovanni Doriceo), while the 1715 baptism names Margherita's mother as **Margaritta Doriceo** — whether Francesca and Margaritta were the same person (double name, common in the period) or different Doriceo women (sisters?) is unresolved. Santa Lanfranchi's 1707 birth record (Dec 1707, six months after the marriage) names the mother as "Maria Scapallina," but this reading is uncertain (MEDIUM confidence, partially obscured cursive) and may be a misread — see `research/image-based-corrections.md` §28 for the full analysis.
- Mother: **Margaritta Doriceo** — named as mother in the 1715 baptism. Possibly the same person as the **Francesca Doriceo** married in June 1707 (double name). Maternal grandfather: **Giovanni Doriceo** (deceased by 1707). The surname "Doriceo" is confirmed (the earlier uncertain "Canovia" reading was a different person — see Giovanni Maria Canova).
- Children: [Luigi Anzolo Alloisio Addobbati](luigi-anzolo-alloisio-addobbati.md) (I30, b. 1 Oct 1744) per **F16**; likely additional children not yet identified in the registers.
