# Caspar Stumpp

**Caspar Stumpp**—also recorded as **Casper STUMP**—was christened **26 October 1596** at **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**. He married [Anna Schuemperli](anna-schuemperli.md) around 1630-1631 and was father of [Augustin Stumpf](augustin-stumpf.md) and [Blasius Stump](blasius-stump-1631.md). His parents were [Blaesi Stump](blaesi-stump.md) and [Helena Bruewschwyler](helena-bruewschwyler.md), representing the second wife of Blaesi.

## Research Notes

**Corrected Parentage:**
Previous records incorrectly listed Madalena Bruewschwyler as mother. The 1596 christening record clearly shows Helena Bruewschwyler as mother, indicating Caspar was son of Blaesi's second wife.

**Marriage Timing:**
The marriage to Anna Schuemperli occurred around 1630-1631, shortly after his father's death in 1629. Their first child Blasius was born in 1631.

**Generational Bridge:**
Caspar represents the crucial link between confirmed ancestor Blaesi STUMP and the continuing genealogical line through his son Augustin.
