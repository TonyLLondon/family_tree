# George Busse (elder)

George Busse is known only from the baptism records of his children in the Tallinn Lutheran church registers. He and his wife [Anna Margaretha Bohm](anna-margaretha-bohm.md) had at least five children baptised between 1726 and 1739, all in Tallinn: Anna Sophia (1726), [Georg](georg-busse.md) (1727), Friedrich Johann (b. ~1730, d. 1731), Anna Dorothea (1736), and Helena (1739).

His son Georg became a tailor in Tallinn and married Margaretha Elisabeth Bischanz in 1744. Nothing is known yet of George's own birth, death, occupation, or origins — whether he was born in Tallinn or migrated there from elsewhere in the Baltic German world.

## Family

- Wife: [Anna Margaretha Bohm](anna-margaretha-bohm.md).
- Children: Anna Sophia (b. 1726), [Georg Busse](georg-busse.md) (b. 1727), Friedrich Johann (d. 1731), Anna Dorothea (b. 1736), Helena (b. 1739).

## Open questions

- Birth and death dates — a "Georg Buse" was buried 22 March 1757 in Tallinn; this could be the elder George rather than his son.
- Occupation and guild membership in Tallinn.
- Origins — whether the Busse family was established in Tallinn or came from Livland or Germany.
