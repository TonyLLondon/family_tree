# May Coolbear

**May Coolbear** was born **25 January 1916** — daughter of [Archibald Percy Coolbear](archibald-percy-coolbear.md) and [Rosina A. Martin](rosina-a-martin-i104.md), granddaughter of the **Coolbear–Newcomb** line that ties to **RAF** dossiers and **George V** service trails. Her father died in **1928** when she was twelve; by the **1939 Register** (29 September) she was living at **28 Crown Mansions, Lower Holloway, Islington** with her brother Archibald Jr and his wife Rosina (née Angus), her brother Henry, and her sister Irene Barbara. Her occupation was **factory hand packer**. She married [Joseph Evans (c. 1914)](joseph-evans-i99.md) about **August 1940** and was mother to [Joseph (1940)](joseph-evans-i18.md) — the man who would marry **Helia** and father **Jacqueline**.

Her name threads the [Evans · Cerpa Pérez hub](../topics/evans-cerpa-perez-london-chile.md), including the **Coolbear** ascend table and loose **Islington** captures.

## Open questions

- Mother Rosina Martin is absent from the 1939 household — she may have died between 1929 (pension card at 10 Queensland Road) and 1939, or she may have been living elsewhere.
