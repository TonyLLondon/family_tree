# Pietro Plarino

Pietro Plarino was the husband of **Margherita Canova** (daughter of the late **Giovanni Maria Canova**) and father of [Maddelena Plarino](maddelena-plarino.md). He died before 1766: the 1766 marriage register describes the bride as "Maddalena figlia del Sig.r Pietro Plarinò" (no *quondam*, but his absence as a living parent implies he was dead). The surname appears as **Plarinò** (with accent) in both the 1732 and 1766 registers, and as **Pilarino** in the 1730 Daniele Addobbati marriage (where a Pietro Pilarino served as witness—possibly the same man).

## Life

- **19 Oct 1732** — Marriage **Zara** to **Margherita Canova**, daughter of the late Giovanni Maria Canova. Officiated by **Don Pietro Cortese**, with dispensation from the three customary banns by Archbishop **Vincenzo Zmajević** (by written authorization). Ceremony performed in the couple's home ("in Casa di loro habitatione"). Witnesses: **Domenico Bonaielli** and **Giovanni Battista Romani**.
- **22 Nov 1730** — A **Pietro Pilarino** served as witness at the marriage of [D. Daniele Addobbati](d-daniele-addobbati.md) and [Margherita Lanfranchi](margherita-lanfranchi.md). If this is the same man, the Pilarino/Plarinò–Addobbati connection predates both marriages and was a multi-generational social bond (the families intermarried in 1766 when Luigi married Maddalena).
- **BEF 1766** — Died. The 1766 register does not describe him as living.

## Family

- Wife: **Margherita Canova**, daughter of the late **Giovanni Maria Canova**. The surname "Canova" connects to two other records: (1) Giovanni Maria Canova served as godfather at the 1715/16 baptism of [Margherita Lanfranchi](margherita-lanfranchi.md) (where the surname was misread as "Canovia"); (2) the DAZD inventory listed Luigi Addobbati's wife's surname as "Canona" — a conflation of the Plarinò and Canova surnames from this intermarriage. Margherita Canova had a brother **Alberto Canone** (d. before 4 Jul 1769), whose daughters (the "Canona sisters") appointed a procurator after his death (DAZD sig. 49).
- Children: [Maddelena Plarino](maddelena-plarino.md) (b. 1736), **Maria Domenica Plarinò** (b. 1734).
