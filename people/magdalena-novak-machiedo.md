# Magdalena Novak “Bonaparte”

**Mandina** — from a **Hvar shipowning Novak** family, nicknamed **“Bonaparte”** in Kovačić’s article. Married **[Lauro Machiedo](lauro-machiedo.md)** from **1876**; matriarch of the branch that includes **[Dr. Jerko](jerko-machiedo.md)** and **[Juraj-Zorko Machiedo](juraj-zorko-machiedo.md)**.
