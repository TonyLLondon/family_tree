# Evan Mainwaring

**Evan Mainwaring** was born about **1781** in the Swansea area, Glamorganshire. He worked as a **mason** and lived in Swansea, where he married **[Margaret](margaret-mainwaring-i180.md)** (b. ~1785). They were members of **St Mary's, Swansea**, where their children were baptised and both were eventually buried.

Their daughter [Ann](ann-mainwaring.md) was [baptised](../media/docs/1826-baptism-ann-mainwaring-st-mary-swansea.jpg) **16 July 1826** at St Mary's — the register names her parents as Evan (mason) and Margaret. The [1841 census](https://www.findmypast.co.uk/image-viewer?id=GBC/1841/1426/0085) at Swansea recorded Evan (aged ~56) with children Rachel, John, Martha, and William; Ann (15) was probably already in service.

Margaret died in **Q4 1841** (GRO Vol 20 p. 282). Evan survived as a widower until about **1848**, when he too was buried at St Mary's.
