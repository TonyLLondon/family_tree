# Catherine Stump

Family and friends knew her as Kitty. She was born in Richmond in mid-December 1949, at the meeting of two very different family stories: her father’s Tehran-born Stump line and her mother’s Irish and Anglo-Indian White inheritance. The civil register placed the birth in Surrey Northern, but childhood unfolded across Lac Léman—she was raised in Geneva, with French-speaking streets and mountain light a long way from the Thames-side suburb on her certificate.

In August 1974 she married [Ivor Anthony Samuel Lewis](ivor-anthony-samuel-lewis.md) in Hove, Sussex. Album frames from that day show her in beaded gown and champagne tailoring against summer hills; a separate outdoor portrait—white jacket, gold geometry at the throat—became the face the ancestor chart remembers.

Her son [Anthony Robert Lewis](anthony-robert-lewis.md) continues the Lewis branch documented in the tree.
