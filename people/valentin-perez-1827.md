# Valentín Pérez (~1817–1892)

**Birth:** ~1817, Chile (film age **75** at death — index had said 1827)  
**Death:** 5 September 1892, Quirihue, Ñuble, Chile  
**Occupation:** Gañan (agricultural labourer)

Valentín was the son of **Ignacio Pérez** (I702) and **Isabel Mora** (I701), and husband of **Rosario González** (I732). His civil death registration (entry 165) names both parents and his spouse, confirming the Pérez line back through Ignacio.

The informant at his death was **Ramona Perez**, age 43, a laundress (labandera) living on Calle de Freire — likely a daughter or close relative. Witnesses were José B. González and Pascual Santurio.
