# Dorothea Brueschwiler

**Dorothea Brueschwiler** was the wife of [Caspar Stump](caspar-stump-1662.md) (1662–1729) in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. She is named as the mother of [Hans Conrad Stump](hans-conrad-stump-1697.md) in his 1697 baptism record.

## Family

- Husband: [Caspar Stump](caspar-stump-1662.md)
- Son: [Hans Conrad Stump](hans-conrad-stump-1697.md) (baptized 30 May 1697, Sulgen)

## Evidence

- **Son's baptism record:** Hans Conrad Stump, 30 May 1697, Sulgen, parents "Caspar Stump" & "Dorothea Brueschwiler" · FamilySearch (record in Switzerland, Baptisms, 1491–1940)

## Research Notes

The Brueschwiler/Bruewschwyler surname was well-established in the Sulgen STUMP family network. Earlier generations saw Blaesi Stumpp (c. 1560) marry two Bruewschwyler women (Madalena and Helena). Dorothea may have been from the same extended family, indicating a multi-generational marriage pattern between the STUMP and Brueschwiler clans in Sulgen.
