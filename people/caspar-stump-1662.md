# Caspar Stump (b. 1662)

**Caspar Stump** (baptized 27 November 1662) was the son of [Augustin Stump](augustin-stump-1628.md) and [Barbel Rutershausser](barbel-rutershausser.md) in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. He married [Dorothea Brueschwiler](dorothea-brueschwiler.md) and became the father of [Hans Conrad Stump](hans-conrad-stump-1697.md), representing Generation 12 in the Swiss STUMP lineage spanning the mid-17th to early 18th century.

## Life

**27 Nov 1662** — Christened **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. Son of [Augustin Stump](augustin-stump-1628.md) and [Barbel Rutershausser](barbel-rutershausser.md).

**c. 1685** — Marriage to [Dorothea Brueschwiler](dorothea-brueschwiler.md).

**30 May 1697** — Birth of son [Hans Conrad Stump](hans-conrad-stump-1697.md) in Sulgen (Caspar age 34).

**9 Dec 1729** — Died **Sulgen** at age 67. Buried in Sulgen parish.
