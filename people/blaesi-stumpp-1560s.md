# Blaesi Stumpp (c. 1560s)

**Blaesi Stumpp** (born c. 1560) was one of the founding patriarchs of the STUMP family in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. He married two women from the Bruewschwyler family and had at least six children between 1591-1601, making him a central figure in establishing the STUMP presence in Sulgen during the late 16th century. He died during the devastating **1629 plague epidemic** that nearly wiped out the STUMP families.

## Life

**c. 1560** — Born in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland** (estimated from children's birth dates).

**c. 1585** — First marriage to [Madalena Bruewschwyler](madalena-bruewschwyler.md).

**18 Aug 1591** — Birth of daughter Ursula Stumpp with Madalena Bruewschwyler.

**7 Feb 1593** — Birth of daughter Ursula Stumpp with Madalena Bruewschwyler.

**20 Oct 1594** — Birth of son [Jacob Stumpp](jacob-stumpp-1594.md) with Madalena Bruewschwyler.

**c. 1595** — Second marriage to [Helena Bruewschwyler](helena-bruewschwyler.md) (possibly after Madalena's death).

**26 Oct 1596** — Birth of son [Caspar Stumpp](caspar-stumpp-1596.md) with Helena Bruewschwyler.

**6 Feb 1599** — Birth of daughter Ursula Stumpp with Helena Bruewschwyler.

**4 Jan 1601** — Birth of son [Blaesi Stumpp](blaesi-stumpp-1601.md) with Helena Bruewschwyler.

**7 Oct 1629** — Died **Sulgen** during the **European plague epidemic** that devastated Switzerland.
