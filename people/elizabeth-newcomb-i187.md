# Elizabeth Newcomb

**Elizabeth Newcomb** (b. ~1792, Witham / Layer Breton, Essex; d. Q2 1867, Maldon, age 74) was the wife of [William Newcomb](william-newcomb-i186.md) and paternal grandmother of [Alma Newcomb](alma-newcomb-i106.md).

In **1851** she was at Tolleshunt Knights with her husband William, their sons James and Charles, daughter-in-law [Susannah](susannah-south-i185.md), and three grandchildren. By **1861** she was a **widow**, living alone at **King Street, Chelmsford**, described as "formerly a charwoman." Her birthplace is recorded as **Witham** (1861) and **Layer Breton** (1851) — Layer Breton falls within the Witham registration area, so these are consistent.

Her death is registered in the **GRO index** for the April–June quarter of 1867 at Maldon, age 74 (Vol. 4a p. 133). She had returned to the Maldon district from Chelmsford at some point in her final years.

## Family

- Husband: [William Newcomb](william-newcomb-i186.md) (b. ~1793, Essex; d. before 1861).
- Sons: [James Newcomb](james-newcomb-i184.md) (b. ~1823); Charles Newcomb (b. ~1830); probably Thomas Newcomb (m. Q4 1837, Maldon).
