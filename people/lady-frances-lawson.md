# Frances Lawson

Frances Lawson was the daughter of [James Lawson of Nesham Abbey](james-lawson-of-nesham-abbey.md) and [Jane Conyers](jane-conyers.md). She married the poet [Richard Braithwaite](richard-braithwaite-2.md) on **4 May 1617** at **Hurworth**, near Darlington, and bore him **nine children, five of them sons**. She died **7 March 1633**, and Richard memorialised her in his poem *Anniversaries upon his Panarete* (1634), in which he spoke of her merit and his loss in terms that prove he was deeply attached to her.

## Life

- **~1597** — Born at **Neesham** (Neasham), Durham.
- **8 Jan 1593** — Parents [James Lawson](james-lawson-of-nesham-abbey.md) and [Jane Conyers](jane-conyers.md) married at Neesham.
- **4 May 1617** — Married [Richard Braithwaite](richard-braithwaite-2.md) at **Hurworth-on-Tees**, Darlington.
- **7 Mar 1633** — Died (DNB). Richard's *Panarete* elegy published 1634.
