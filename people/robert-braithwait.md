# Robert Braithwait

**Robert Braithwait** was the grandfather of the poet [Richard Braithwaite](richard-braithwaite-2.md). The DNB pedigree states that the poet's great-grandfather, **Richard** (the "squire of Ambleside"), had one son **Robert**, who had two sons — [Thomas](thomas-braithwait-of-worcop.md) and **James** — and five daughters. Robert's wife in the export is [Alice Williamson](alice-williamson.md); his parents are given as [Richard Braithwait](richard-braithwait.md) and [Anne Sandys](anne-sandys.md) (the Anne Sandys connection is from the export/FamilySearch, not confirmed in the DNB).

## Family

- Wife: [Alice Williamson](alice-williamson.md).
- Parents: [Richard Braithwait](richard-braithwait.md) ("squire of Ambleside" per DNB); [Anne Sandys](anne-sandys.md) (export only).
- Children: [Thomas Braithwait, of Warcop](thomas-braithwait-of-worcop.md); **James**; five daughters.

## Open questions

- **Anne Sandys** as Robert's mother (the export/FamilySearch connection) — if she is from the Sandys of Graythwaite/Furness, that would connect to **Archbishop Edwin Sandys** (1519–1588) of York. The DNB does not name Robert's mother.
- Marriage year "~1510" in the export vs reconstructed ages — typical visitation compression.
