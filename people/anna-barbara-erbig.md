# Anna Barbara Erbig

Anna Barbara Erbig was the wife of [Johann Conrad Haag](johann-conrad-haag.md) and mother of [Hans Conrad Haag](hans-conrad-haag.md) (b. 1742, Hüttwilen). Her name appears in records as both "Erbig" and "Erb."

## Family

- Husband: [Johann Conrad Haag](johann-conrad-haag.md)
- Son: [Hans Conrad Haag](hans-conrad-haag.md) (b. 1742, Hüttwilen, Thurgau)
