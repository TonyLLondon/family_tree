# Graziella Nakic d’Osljak

Wife of **[Juraj-Zorko Machiedo](juraj-zorko-machiedo.md)**; mother of **[Tatiana Machiedo](tatiana-machiedo.md)** and **[Sergije Makiedo](sergije-makiedo.md)**.

Kovačić describes her family as **Italian-leaning** (*talijanskih osjećaja*). The noble-style **d’Osljak** segment refers to the **Ošljak** island estate opposite **Preko** (Zadar channel) — not to be confused with unrelated **Nakić** lines elsewhere in Dalmatia without this predicate.

## Family

- Husband: [Juraj-Zorko Machiedo](juraj-zorko-machiedo.md).
- Children (Kovačić): **Maksim** (d. Florence 1917); **Sergije**; **Tatjana-Tanja**; **Isabella “Cinci”** (later Capanna).
