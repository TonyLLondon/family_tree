# Hans Conrad Haag

Hans Conrad Haag was the husband of [Margaretha Freyenmuth](margaretha-freyenmuth.md) and father of [Anna Margaretha Hagg](anna-margaretha-hagg.md); they married on 12 December 1765. He was christened on 24 June 1742 at Huettevilen, Thurgan, Switz. (read **Hüttwilen**, Thurgau) and died on 15 September 1803.
