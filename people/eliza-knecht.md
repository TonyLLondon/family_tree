# Eliza Sarah Glasscock

**Eliza Sarah Glasscock** was born about **1833** in **Ireland** and married [George Knecht](george-knecht.md), a German gas worker, in **Q3 1856** (July–September) at **Islington** (GRO marriage index: Vol 1B, p. 267). Their first child, Francis Henry, was born in 1857 at St Giles — fitting a mid-1856 marriage exactly.

Glasscock is an English surname concentrated in **Essex and Suffolk**. Eliza's Irish birthplace (recorded in the 1871 census) alongside an English surname suggests she may have been born to English parents settled in Ireland, or the family may have had Irish roots despite the English-origin surname.

The **1871 Census** (RG10/263, schedule 316) records her as wife, age 38, at **Pembroke Street, Upper**, St Michael Islington, with six children ranging from Francis Henry (14) to baby Louisa Sarah (1).

## Family

Husband: [George Knecht](george-knecht.md) (m. Q3 1856, Islington). Children (from 1871 census): Francis Henry (~1857), Elizabeth (~1859), [Dorothea](dorothea-knecht.md) (~1861), George William (~1864), Hannah (~1866), Louisa Sarah (~1870).

The **1881 Census** (Paddington, Mary Magdalene parish) records her birthplace as **Middlesex, Paddington** — contradicting the 1871 return's **Ireland**. The discrepancy may reflect changed self-reporting (common among Irish-born residents facing prejudice) or an enumerator error. The English-origin surname Glasscock is consistent with either reading.
