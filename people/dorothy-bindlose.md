# Dorothy Bindlose

**Dorothy Bindlose** was the daughter of **Robert Bindloss of Haulston**, Westmorland. She married [Thomas Braithwait, of Warcop](thomas-braithwait-of-worcop.md), barrister and recorder of Kendal, and was mother of the poet [Richard Braithwaite](richard-braithwaite-2.md) and of Sir Thomas Brathwaite (d. 1618).

The DNB identifies her as "Dorothy, daughter of Robert Bindloss of Haulston, Westmoreland" — the export birthplace "Hailston" is the same place under a variant spelling.

## Life

- **~1544** — Born at **Haulston** (Hailston), Westmorland.
- **1 Sep 1564** — Christened at **Worton**, Kendal (export).
- **~1624** — Death/burial notes point to Westmorland (export).

## Family

- Husband: [Thomas Braithwait, of Warcop](thomas-braithwait-of-worcop.md).
- Father: [Robert Bindloss, of Haulston](robert-bindlosse.md).
- Children: **Sir Thomas Brathwaite** (d. 1618); [Richard Braithwaite, the poet](richard-braithwaite-2.md) (second surviving son).

## Evidence

- **DNB (1885–1900):** "Brathwaite, Richard" — "Thomas… married Dorothy, daughter of Robert Bindloss of Haulston, Westmoreland." Richard's 1611 *Golden Fleece* is dedicated in part to "his uncle, Robert Bindlosse" — Dorothy's brother or nephew.
- **Export:** **treeId** **I198** — unions **F100**, **F102** — [archive/gedcom/Upload for MyHeritage 200929.ged](../archive/gedcom/Upload%20for%20MyHeritage%20200929.ged); FamilySearch **MKFF-J4J**.

## Open questions

- The export calls her father "Sir Robert Bindlosse" of Borwick, Lancashire (b.1511, d.1589). The DNB says "Robert Bindloss of Haulston, Westmoreland" without "Sir." Borwick Hall and Haulston are ~15 miles apart; the Bindloss family held lands in both locations. The "Sir" and Borwick attribution may be a Geni conflation with a later Sir Robert Bindloss (created baronet 1641) or with another branch.
- Mother on F102 not named in any current source.
