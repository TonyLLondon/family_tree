## Indexed mentions (corpus only)

Church indexes associate the name **Isidro Mora** with Isabel Mora in the **1818 marriage** cluster and **1877 burial** cluster for a woman treated as Valentin’s mother in narrative — see [fs-6rq6-zsqw-ignacio-isabel-marriage-1818](../sources/corpus/fs-6rq6-zsqw-ignacio-isabel-marriage-1818/reference.md) and [fs-6y8r-3qpv-isabel-mora-burial-1877](../sources/corpus/fs-6y8r-3qpv-isabel-mora-burial-1877/reference.md). Other Quirihue entries name **Isidro Mora** with **Retamal** (variant indexing) and children in the **1780–1804** window — possible homonym or second union; see burial corpus note.

**Not this man:** indexed **Isidro Mora Alarcón** (`6RQ6-ZNN7`), child of Francisco Mora + Petrona Alarcón in a younger generation.
