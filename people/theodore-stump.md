# Theodore Stump

**Theo** in the family album—**Theodore** in the working tree—was born **1911**, son of **[Étienne](etienne-stump.md)** and **[Henriette](henriette-bottin.md)**, brother to **[Robert](robert-marc-murard-stump.md)** and the younger **Stump** children who crossed from **Qajar Tehran** into **European** schools and exile. The Henderson photograph [Etienne, Henriette, Robert and Theo](../media/albums/henderson/Etienne,%20Henriette,%20Robert%20and%20Theo.jpg) is the human-scale proof beside the chart.

## Family

- Parents: [Étienne Stump](etienne-stump.md); [Henriette Bottin](henriette-bottin.md).
- Siblings: [Robert Marc Murard Stump](robert-marc-murard-stump.md), [Jacques Stump](jacques-stump.md), [Jean Stump](jean-stump.md), [Irene Stump](irene-stump.md).
