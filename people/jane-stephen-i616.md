# Jane Stephen

**Jane Stephen** is the working-tree wife of **[Thomas Stephen](thomas-stephen-llandybie.md)** of **Llandybie**; their son **[William Stephen](william-stephen-llandybie.md)** (bapt. 1790) was father of **[Rachel Stephen](rachel-james.md)** — tying this line into **[Lewis Lewis](lewis-lewis.md)** and the Aberavon **Lewis** descent to **[Tony Lewis](anthony-robert-lewis.md)**.
