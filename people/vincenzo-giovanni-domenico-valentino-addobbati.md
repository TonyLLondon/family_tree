# Vincenzo Giovanni Domenico Valentino Addobbati

Father of [Pietro Paolo Addobbati](pietro-paolo-addobbati.md) with [(Domenica) Vincenza Coltelli](domenica-vincenza-coltelli.md) in **F14** (marriage **25 Feb 1811**, **Zara**). Long **quadruple** given-name stack matches the GEDCOM **NAME** line and **FamilySearch** id **GW1S-CGK**. Styled **Ivan Vicko** in Croatian sources — identified by Granić as one of the two sons of **Lujo (Luigi) Addobbati** formally inscribed as nobles of Nin on **24 June 1804**.

## Life

- **30 Jan 1776** — Born **Zara, Dalmatia**.
- **24 Jun 1804** — Formally inscribed as noble of **Nin** (*Ivan Vicko Addobbati*) alongside brother **Dr. Petar**, sons of Lujo Addobbati. The Nin municipal confirmation is preserved in the Addobbati fonds as **sig. 19** (1 Jul 1804).
- **25 Feb 1811** — Marriage **Zara** to Vincenza Coltelli (**F14**).
- **28 Jan 1839** — Died **Zara, Dalmatia**.

## Family

- Wife: [(Domenica) Vincenza Coltelli](domenica-vincenza-coltelli.md).
- Children include [Pietro Paolo Addobbati](pietro-paolo-addobbati.md) per **F14**.
- Parents: [Luigi Anzolo Alloisio Addobbati](luigi-anzolo-alloisio-addobbati.md); [Maddelena Plarino](maddelena-plarino.md) (**F15**).
- Brother: **Dr. Petar Addobbati** (not in GEDCOM) — also admitted Nin 1804; Knight of the Golden Spur 1786.
