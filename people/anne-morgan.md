# Anne Morgan (Griffiths)

**Anne** — née **Morgan**, daughter of [William Morgan](william-morgan-llanfairarybryn.md) and [Sarah Morgan](sarah-morgan.md) — was born about **1829** at **Llanfair-ar-y-bryn** near Llandovery, Carmarthenshire. Her maiden name was confirmed by her widowed mother Sarah living with the Griffiths family in the 1881 census, and the parental home identified from the 1841 census at **Coedevan**, a farmstead in the parish. The 1871 enumerator wrote "Llan Ferbyn" and the 1881 return has "Llanfair".

She married [Thomas Griffiths](thomas-griffiths.md) of Trecastle, Breconshire, and by 1861 the couple were in **Aberdare**. Their four surviving children — Elizabeth/Eliza, [Catherine](catherine-griffiths.md), Margaret, and David — were all born in Aberdare between ~1860 and ~1869.
