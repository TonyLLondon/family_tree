# Agnes Benson

**Agnes Benson** of Longriggs, Lancashire, daughter of **Barnard Benson**, married [George Brathwaite](george-brathwaite.md) on **1 July 1666** at the Swarthmoor Quaker Monthly Meeting. She was mother of [Ann Braithwaite](ann-braithwaite.md) and three other children.

The Benson and Brathwaite families were closely connected: in 1664, George's sister (a "second daughter of William Brathwaite") married **Barnard Benson son of Francis Benson** at George's own house. George and Agnes already had a daughter (Ann, born 1663) before their own Quaker marriage in 1666.

## Life

- **Born** at **Longriggs**, Lancashire, daughter of **Barnard Benson**. The marriage register (TNA RG6/1085, p. 29) explicitly names her as "Agnes Benson daughter of Barnard Benson." The Hawkshead parish baptisms of "Agnes Benson, father William" (1639 and 1645) are a different family — a William Benson married to Margaret appears separately in the Swarthmoor register (daughter Abigail, 1671).
- **21 Sep 1663** — Daughter [Ann](ann-braithwaite.md) born at Swarthmoor, before the recorded Quaker marriage. Father George, mother Agnes (TNA RG6/1084).
- **1 Jul 1666** — Married [George Brathwaite](george-brathwaite.md) at **Swarthmoor** Monthly Meeting, Lancashire (TNA RG6/1085). The register reads: "George Brathwaite son of William Brathwaite of the parish of Hawkshead in the county of Lancaster and Agnes Benson daughter of Barnard Benson..."
- **2 Jul 1667** — Son **Edward** born at Swarthmoor (TNA RG6/1085). Father George, mother Agnes.
- **4 Jun 1669** — Daughter **Agnes** born at Swarthmoor (TNA RG6/1085). Father George, mother Agnes.
- **10 Feb 1671** — Daughter **Dorothy** born at Swarthmoor (TNA RG6/1085). Father George, mother Agnes.
- **4 Mar 1681** — George buried at Swarthmoor.
- **1684** — Son Edward buried at Swarthmoor, aged only 17 (TNA RG6/1085).
- **1688** — Sister **Rebecka Benson**, daughter of Barnard Benson of Longriggs, buried at Swarthmoor (TNA RG6/1085).
- **1689** — Another sister (name unclear), daughter of Barnard Benson, buried at Swarthmoor (TNA RG6/1085).
- **5 Jul 1694** — Died. Buried **7 Jul 1694** at **Swarthmoor** Quaker burial ground (TNA RG6/1616). The burial record names her as "Agnes the wife of George Brathwaite."

## Evidence

- **Quaker marriage (1666):** TNA RG6/1085, p. 29, Swarthmoor — George Brathwaite son of William Brathwaite of Hawkshead m. **Agnes Benson daughter of Barnard Benson**, 1 Jul 1666. Original register image photographed. FMP `TNA/RG6/MAR/33382/1`.
- **Second marriage on same page (1664):** Barnard Benson son of Francis Benson (Westmorland) m. [?] Brathwaite "second daughter of William Brathwaite" of Hawkshead — "married in the house of George Brathwaite" on [?] day of the 11th month 1664. Shows the two families were intermarrying.
- **Children's Quaker births:** All at Swarthmoor Monthly Meeting (TNA RG6/1085), naming father George, mother Agnes. See [George Brathwaite](george-brathwaite.md) for full citations.
- **Edward's burial (1684):** TNA RG6/1085 — Edward son of George Brathwaite, buried 4th day of the 4th month 1684.
- **Rebecka Benson burial (1688):** TNA RG6/1085 — Rebecka daughter of Barnad Benson of Longriggs, buried 1688. Confirms Agnes's father's name and location.
- **[?] Benson burial (1689):** TNA RG6/1085 — daughter of Barnard Benson, buried 11th day of the 4th month 1689.
- **Quaker burial (1694):** TNA RG6/1616, Lancashire Quarterly Meeting — *Agnes the wife of George Brathwaite*, buried 7 Jul 1694, Swarthmoor. Death date 5 Jul 1694. FMP `TNA/RG6/BUR/49370`.
- **Correction from parish records:** The Hawkshead parish baptisms of "Agnes Benson, father William" (FMP `R_946015326`, `R_946015327`) are NOT this Agnes. The original Quaker register unambiguously names her father as Barnard, not William. A William Benson married to Margaret (with daughter Abigail, 1671) appears in the same Swarthmoor register — a different family.
