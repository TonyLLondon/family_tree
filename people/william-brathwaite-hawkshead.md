# William Brathwaite (of Hawkshead)

**William Brathwaite** (baptised 2 October 1614, Hawkshead – died 25 October 1689, Swarthmoor) married **Sibell Mackreth** on **6 November 1634** at Hawkshead. He was a first-generation Quaker of the Swarthmoor Monthly Meeting and father of [George Brathwaite](george-brathwaite.md).

## Life

- **2 Oct 1614** — Baptised at **Hawkshead**, Lancashire, son of **William (Wm.) Braithwait**. Parish register (FamilySearch batch P00596-1). FMP `R_946014220`. The elder William is also recorded baptising another son Wm. at the same church on 14 Oct 1616, suggesting the first (our William) may in fact have survived and the 1616 entry is a different child or a different William.
- **6 Nov 1634** — Married **Sibell Mackreth** at **Hawkshead**, Lancashire. Parish register (FamilySearch batch M00596-1). FMP Lancashire Banns & Marriages `FS/CUMBRIA/3154704/MAR/0447470/1`.
- **26 Apr 1635** — Son *George* (Geo.) baptised at Hawkshead. This child evidently died in infancy.
- **7 May 1637** — Son [George](george-brathwaite.md) baptised at Hawkshead. The reuse of the name confirms the earlier child's death.
- Became a Quaker. His son George's family appears consistently in the Swarthmoor Monthly Meeting registers from 1663 onward, and William himself was buried in the Quaker burial ground.
- **Buried** at **Swarthmoor** Quaker burial ground — two Quaker burial records for a William Brathwaite at Swarthmoor: **20 Apr 1688** (TNA RG6/1085) and **25 Oct 1689** (TNA RG6/1250). These are either two separate register copies of the same event with transcription variation, or (less likely) a father and son. The 1689 record gives the exact death date as 25 Oct 1689; if this is the same William who married in 1634, he would have been roughly 80 years old.

## Family

- Wife: **Sibell Mackreth** (married 6 Nov 1634, Hawkshead).
- Known children:
 - *George* (bap. 26 Apr 1635, Hawkshead) — died in infancy.
 - [**George**](george-brathwaite.md) (bap. 7 May 1637, Hawkshead) — married Agnes Benson; settled at Swarthmoor.
 - **[?] Brathwaite** (daughter) — described as "second daughter of William Brathwaite" in the Swarthmoor marriage register (1664). She married Barnard Benson son of Francis Benson "in the house of George Brathwaite." This confirms William had at least two daughters as well as son George.
- Possible additional children: Other Braithwait baptisms at Hawkshead with father "Wm." or "Will." include Dorathie (1627), Edward (1628), Agnes (1635), and Edward (1647). Multiple William Braithwaites were active at Hawkshead simultaneously, so attribution of these children to our William is uncertain.

## Ancestry (unproven)

William's father is recorded as "Wm." (William) in the 1614 baptism. The elder William Braithwait had several marriage candidates at Hawkshead:

| Date | Spouse | FMP ID |
|------|--------|--------|
| 14 Aug 1608 | Margaret Braithwaite | `R_855432089` |
| 23 Nov 1611 | Emas Asheburner | `R_855432090` |
| 3 May 1613 | Jenett Jackson | `R_855432091` |

The 1613 marriage to **Jenett Jackson** best fits the timeline (married May 1613, first known child Oct 1614). **In this period** multiple William Braithwaites were active at Hawkshead simultaneously, so the elder William's identity cannot be firmly established from FMP index data alone — original register images would be needed.
