# Marguerite Emilie Stump

Third child of [Marc Francois Stump](marc-francois-stump.md) and [Olga Caroline Erbe](olga-caroline-erbe.md). Marguerite married into a Baltic noble family and relocated from Tallinn to **Latvia**, where she and her husband [Cuno von Frey](cuno-von-frey.md) were last documented in **1910**.

## Life

- **Born 29 November 1872** at 12:15 AM (recorded as "12:15 in the morning") in Tallinn ([EBC 2005](../sources/estonian-biographical-center-stump-report-2005.md)).
- **Baptised 31 December 1872** at home, registered in **Tallinn Dome** congregation. Godparents: Miss **Amalie Weisse**, Mrs **Caroline Pfeiffer** née **Eylandt**, Mrs **Weisse** (wife of a doctor), Mr **Edmund Erbe**, senior teacher **Roselfeldt**, Miss **Valerie Rosenbaum**.
- **Confirmed 21 October 1890** in Tallinn.
- **Holy Communion 11 April 1902** in Tallinn Dome congregation.
- **Married 11 August 1902** in Tallinn Dome congregation to **Cuno Alfred von Frey** ([EBC](../sources/estonian-biographical-center-stump-report-2005.md); matches **F207** marriage date in the working tree).
- **1902** — left the Tallinn Dome congregation and joined a **Lutheran congregation in Valmiera, Latvia** (husband's place of residence).
- **1910** — the Louise Stump obituary lists mourners including *"Kuno Frey and wife in Libaži"* (Limbazi, Latvia), confirming Marguerite was **alive** in 1910. The working tree's death date of "1910" is almost certainly a **last-known-alive date misrecorded as death**; no cause or place of death appears in any source.
