# Frances Ridsdale

Mother of [Edward Burgess](edward-burgess.md). Baptized **10 June 1784** at **Wakefield**, **All Saints**; died **1847** at **Brighton**, **Sussex** (working tree only). She married [Henry Burgess](henry-burgess.md) on **6 December 1804** at **St John's, Wakefield** by licence, with her father **Edward Ridsdale** and **Elisa Ridsdale** as witnesses.

## Life

- **10 Jun 1784** — Baptized **Wakefield**, **All Saints**.
- **6 Dec 1804** — Married [Henry Burgess](henry-burgess.md) by **licence** at **St John's, Wakefield**, Yorkshire. Frances aged 20, resident in this parish; Henry aged 21, resident in Leicester. Witnesses: her father **Edward Ridsdale** and **Elisa Ridsdale**. Archbishop of York licence dated 3 Dec 1804 (Borthwick Institute for Archives).
- **1847** — Died **Brighton**, **Sussex**.

## Family
