# Marc Francois Stump

Swiss-born **Stump** patriline ancestor of [Étienne Stump](etienne-stump.md) (GEDCOM **F70**); therefore **great-grandfather** of [Robert Marc Murard Stump](robert-marc-murard-stump.md). The Findmypast GEDCOM uses the given names **Markus Franz**; this vault keeps the filename **`marc-francois-stump.md`** to match [ancestor-coverage-list.md](ancestor-coverage-list.md) (`@I140@` row).

**Europe-only narrative (Thurgau → Tallinn, Erbe line):** [Stump / Stumpf — Thurgau parishes to Tallinn and the Baltic German Erbe line](../stories/stump-thurgau-tallinn-baltic-line.md).

His wife [Olga Caroline Erbe](olga-caroline-erbe.md) brings a **Baltic German** pedigree—**St. Petersburg** birth, **Tallinn** death, parents [Hermann Eberhard Erbe](hermann-eberhard-erbe.md) and [Emilie Ida Eylandt](emilie-ida-eylandt.md) with deep **Reval / Riga / Rakvere / Gotha** documentation in the merged Geni blocks. That is why Étienne can be **Tallinn-born** and still end up a **Tehran court dentist**: the family had already practiced **long-distance imperial mobility** before the Swiss–Iran thread ever started.

## Life

### Switzerland and naming

- **Christening narrative (Swiss Protestant record):** **10 March 1834**, Evangelisch, **Erlen, Thurgau** (GEDCOM + FamilySearch citation **@S5@** ark `/61903/1:1:FVFK-CQH` — Markus Franz Stump).
- **Tallinn Lutheran evidence:** the [Estonian Biographical Center report (2005)](../sources/estonian-biographical-center-stump-report-2005.md) quotes the **Tallinn Dome** parishioner register with **16 February 1834** at **Yverdon** and a possible second date **10 March 1834** (interpreted there as baptism). **Yverdon** vs **Erlen** and **February vs March** are **not reconciled** in this vault; the report also warns on **Gregorian vs Julian** calendar usage across Switzerland and the Russian Empire.

### Career in Estonia

- **March 1865** — **University of Tartu** examinations (French language and literature, French literary history; minors religion, Bible and Greek history, world history, world geography, arithmetic); **24 March 1865** certificate, toward qualification as a **French teacher** in a **gymnasium** ([EBC 2005](../sources/estonian-biographical-center-stump-report-2005.md)).
- **From 1868** — **Oberlehrer** (senior teacher) of **French** at the **Tallinn government gymnasium**; **Reformed** confession in parish context; wife **Lutheran** (same source).
- **1881** — Holy Communion noted **10 April 1881** for Marc and family in **Tallinn Dome** parishioner register (EBC).
- **1900** — Lived **Tatari** street; title **Kollegienrat** (collegiums councilor); also **Mere 123**. Owned a **house** (report suggests Tatari property while he resided at Mere); tenants included **Magnus Grube**’s bakery (from **May 1894**, still noted **1900**) and pharmacist **Treublut** in the basement (**1897–1900**). **Neighbour Konstantin Mühlberg** sued over a **tinning workshop** (**1900**); further court papers cited in EBC fonds list.

### Marriage and children

- **22 June 1868** — Married **Olga Caroline Erbe** in **Tallinn**; both resident there (EBC; matches **F70**).
- **Children (all five known, per EBC):** [Jean Jacque Stump](jean-jacque-stump.md) (**I398**, d. **1874**); [Arthur Roger Stump](arthur-roger-stump.md) (**I399**, d. **1935** Berlin); [Marguerite Emilie Stump](marguerite-emilie-stump.md) (**I400**, m. [von Frey](cuno-von-frey.md)); [Antoinette Stump](antoinette-stump.md) (**I401**, d. **1881**); [Étienne Stump](etienne-stump.md) (**I16**).

### Death

- **3 April 1903** — Death in **Yverdon** per EBC’s reading of the **1902–1926 Tallinn Dome parishioner** register (with **“Schweiz”** gloss); **no** formal leave from the congregation recorded — interpreted in the report as **death abroad** or incomplete register practice.
- **GEDCOM export** in this repo had **16 April 1903** in **Tallinn** (“Talinn” spelling). Treat **EBC + Tallinn parish book** as the **preferred documentary anchor** until a **Swiss civil death image** is filed; the JSON tree does not carry vital dates (see [family-tree.json](../family-tree.json) `meta.coverage`).
