# Anna Sprenger (c. 1635)

**Anna Sprenger** was a Swiss woman from **Sulgen**, **Thurgau** in the mid-17th century. She married [Thoma Mock](thoma-mock.md) and became the mother of [Johannes Mockh](johannes-mockh-1662.md) (baptized 1661), establishing the maternal lineage in **Generation 12** that would eventually connect to the Jung and STUMP families through subsequent marriages.
