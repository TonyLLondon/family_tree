# Barbel Cuntz

**Barbel Cuntz** was the wife of [Jacob Stump](jacob-stump-1606.md) (1606–c. 1654) in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. She is named as the mother of [Augustin Stump](augustin-stump-1628.md) in his 1628 baptism record.

## Family

- Husband: [Jacob Stump](jacob-stump-1606.md)
- Son: [Augustin Stump](augustin-stump-1628.md) (baptized 22 Dec 1628, Sulgen)

## Evidence

- **Son's baptism record:** Augustin Stump, 22 Dec 1628, Sulgen, parents "Jacob Stump" & "Barbel Cuntz" · FamilySearch FV8P-MBN

## Research Notes

The Cuntz surname appears in the Sulgen parish records. Jacob also had a daughter Madlen (baptized 20 Nov 1627) with wife "Anna Bischoff" — this may indicate Jacob married twice, with Anna Bischoff as an earlier wife or Barbel Cuntz as a second name/second wife. The chronology (Madlen Nov 1627, Augustin Dec 1628) is tight but plausible for the same mother under a variant name.
