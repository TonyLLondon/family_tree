# Conradt Stumpp (c. 1490-1560)

**Conradt Stumpp** (also **Cunrat Stump**) represents the **earliest documented STUMP ancestor** in Swiss records and father of **Blaesi STUMP** (c. 1540-1629), our previously established deepest STUMP ancestor.

## Overview

**Major genealogical breakthrough** identified Conradt as potentially identical to **Cunrat Stump**, who had daughter **Elsbeth** baptized 27 May 1534 in Zürich. **Died 11 August 1560** in Switzerland. This discovery pushes documented STUMP ancestry back to approximately **1500-1510**, representing over **425 years** of Swiss Protestant lineage and establishing the family's presence from the **early Reformation period**.
