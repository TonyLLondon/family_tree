# Georg Busse

Georg Busse was born on **23 October 1727** in **Tallinn (Reval)** and baptised two days later on **25 October** at a Lutheran church in the city. His baptism record names his parents as **George Busse** and **Anna Margaretha Bohm** — a Tallinn couple who had at least five children between 1726 and 1739, including Anna Sophia, Anna Dorothea, Helena, and Friedrich Johann (who died in infancy in 1731).

Georg became a tailor (*Schneider*) in Reval, following what appears to be a family trade. He married [Margaretha Elisabeth Bischanz](margaretha-elisabeth-bischanz.md) on **9 February 1744** in Tallinn — she was a widow (née Bischanz, "verw. Rippel") who brought children from her first marriage to Johann Wilhelm Rippel. The Busse–Bischanz union produced at least three children: Christina David (b. 1745), [Ernst Gottlieb](ernst-gottlieb-busse.md) (b. 1748), and Margaretha Elisabeth (b. 1751).

A "Georg Buse" was buried in Tallinn on **22 March 1757**, having died on **19 March**. If this is our Georg — and the occupation and parish match the Geni data — he died aged just 29, leaving Ernst Gottlieb as a nine-year-old. How Ernst Gottlieb was raised after this early loss, and whether Margaretha Elisabeth remarried again, are open questions.
