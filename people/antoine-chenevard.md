# Antoine Chenevard

Antoine Chenevard was born around **1657** and buried **24 July 1733** at Céligny, Geneva. His father was [Bernard Chenevard](bernard-chenevard.md), the earliest known ancestor of this Chenevard line. Antoine married [Françoise Mestral](francoise-mestral.md) (daughter of Estienne Mestral).

Their children baptised at Céligny include [Benjamin](benjamin-chenevard.md) (b. ~1688), Gabriel (b. 1689), and Françoise (b. 1707). Benjamin married Christine Nerfin and their daughter [Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard](jaqueline-suzanne-chenevard.md) married into the Montagnon family.

The Chenevard surname clusters at Céligny (Geneva) and Jorat-Mézières (Vaud) in Swiss records, with a parallel French branch at Seveux, Haute-Saône (Franche-Comté), suggesting possible cross-border origins.
