# Mary Stephen

**Mary** (surname unknown) was the wife of **William Stephen** (b. 1790, Llandybie, Carmarthenshire), a farmer at **Llanarthne**. She appears on the baptism register of their daughter [Rachel](rachel-james.md), baptised **9 October 1825** at **Llanarthne** — recorded as "Rachel, daughter of William and Mary Stephen."

William married **Margaret Hopkins** at Llanarthne on 18 November 1814, then **Rachel Morley** at Llanarthne on 27 January 1826. Since Mary does not match either of these names, she may have been a wife between them, or the parish clerk may have recorded a variant name. Rachel (the daughter) was born before the second marriage, placing Mary as mother in the period between the two known marriages.
