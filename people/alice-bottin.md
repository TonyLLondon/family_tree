# Alice Jeanne Olga Bottin

Second child of [Julien Bottin](julien-bottin.md) and [Fanny Burgess](fanny-burgess.md), born **~1889** in Tehran. She married **Ladislas Majewski** on **12 September 1914** in **Liège, Belgium**.

## Life

- **~1889** — Born in Tehran, Iran (1901 census gives birth year as 1889, birthplace France; Henderson FBB Timeline gives Tehran).
- **31 Mar 1901** — Enumerated in the 1901 England census at Hammersmith, London, as "Alice Bottin", age 12, born in France (French Subject), living with mother [Fanny](fanny-burgess.md) and siblings [Harriet](henriette-bottin.md), [Edward](edward-bottin.md), [René](rene-bottin.md), and [George](george-bottin.md). Father Julien absent — he was at the Tehran arsenal.
- **12 Sep 1914** — Married **Ladislas Majewski** (son of Léonard and Agniès Josephine Pisart) in Liège, Belgium. Civil registration record names her parents as **Julien Bottin** and **Fanny Burgess**.

## Family

- Parents: [Julien Bottin](julien-bottin.md); [Fanny Burgess](fanny-burgess.md).
- Husband: Ladislas Majewski (m. 1914, Liège).
- Siblings: [Henriette Bottin](henriette-bottin.md), [Edward Bottin](edward-bottin.md), [René Bottin](rene-bottin.md), [George Bottin](george-bottin.md).
