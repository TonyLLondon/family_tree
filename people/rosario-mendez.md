# Rosario Mendez

**Spouse:** Justo Cerpa (~1775)  
**Children:** Fabián Cerpa (1808-1888), Bruno Cerpa (1814-1894), Ignacio Cerpa Mendez (1815-1885)  

## Family

- **Spouse:** Justo Cerpa (~1775, colonial ancestor)
- **Children:** Fabián Cerpa → Francisco CERPA → modern family, plus Bruno and Ignacio Cerpa

## Significance

Rosario represents the **colonial-era CERPA maternal line**, mother of the documented Cerpa patriarch Fabián Cerpa (1808-1888) who appears in multiple Chilean civil death records.

## Evidence

**Multiple CERPA family death records consistently show:**
- Fabián Cerpa death 1888 (Q241-CBB4): parents "Justo Cerpa + Rosario Mendez"
- Bruno Cerpa death 1894 (Q241-8DL9): parents "Justo Cerpa + Rosario Mendez" 
- Ignacio Cerpa Mendez death 1885 (Q241-CYQS): parents "Justo Cerpa + Rosario Mendez"
