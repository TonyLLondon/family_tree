# Sergije Makiedo (Makiedo / Machiedo)

Older brother of **[Tatiana Machiedo](tatiana-machiedo.md)**. **Hrvatski biografski leksikon** summarizes his public career: law studies **Belgrade** and **Zagreb**, doctorate **1936**; trainee **Šibenik** district court; **Vis** municipal court judge from **1940**; joined **partisans** late **1941**; political commissar roles (**Šibenik–Vis**, then **Navy of NOVJ**); mission to **Bari** on patrol boat **Bakar** with engineer **Armando**; post-war **diplomacy** (including **Washington** 1945–49, **Geneva** UN permanent representative 1958–62, **UNDP** to retirement **1976**). Nephew of physician-politician **[Dr. Jerko Machiedo](jerko-machiedo.md)** per HBL.

**Fulvia Zerauschek’s memoir** describes Tatiana’s brother as a **lawyer / young judge** with **communist** persuasion who influenced the family’s wartime choices — that figure aligns with **Sergije**, not a distant namesake.

## Spouse and daughters (Kovačić / HBL)
