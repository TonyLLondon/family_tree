# Hellen Ankettle

**Hellen Ankettle** of **Dingle, Kerry** married [Thomas Roche](thomas-roche.md) of Limerick in **1796** and was mother of [William Roche](william-roche.md) (Crown Solicitor for Limerick). She died on 22 March 1849 "in the 77th year of her age" at **George's Street, Limerick**, where she and Thomas had lived for decades.

Her father was **[John Ankettle](john-ankettle.md)** of Dingle. The Ankettle family had medical and academic connections: Burke's 1833 records a "Doctor Ankettle" (Richard Ankettle, M.D.) who was a medical student at Paris in 1724, a relation of the same family — his daughter Mary-Anne married Thomas's father Stephen Roche as his third wife.

## Life

- **~1772** — Born (died 1849 "in the 77th year of her age").
- **1796** — Marriage at **Limerick** to [Thomas Roche](thomas-roche.md) (Farrar's Index; bride indexed as "Catherine Ankettle," bride's residence: Dingle, Kerry).
- **22 Mar 1849** — Died at **George's Street, Limerick** (*Southern Reporter*, 27 Mar 1849).
