# Henriette Bottin Burgess

**Henriette** is the **French–Anglo–Persian** hinge between the **[Burgess–Bottin](julien-bottin.md)** missionary-merchant world and **[Étienne Stump](etienne-stump.md)**’s **Qajar** surgery—daughter of [Fanny Burgess](fanny-burgess.md) and [Julien Bottin](julien-bottin.md), mother of **[Robert](robert-marc-murard-stump.md)** and his **Tehran-born** siblings. A **French passport *signalement*** in the archive describes her as born at **Téhéran** with the physical detail clerks loved—proof that the **second generation** could be **Persian-native** on paper as well as in memory.

She married Étienne in **1909**; the **1909 album page** with him catches the couple before the Great War redrew borders. She died **26 June 1961** (export date; primary death image still wanted).

The **1886** passport birth and the **1888** export birthday may be one woman twice miscopied—see Open questions.
