# Anna Margaretha Bohm

Anna Margaretha Bohm is known from the baptism records of her children in the Tallinn Lutheran church registers, where her name appears as "Anna Margrath Bohm," "Anna Margr Bohn," and "Anna Margaretha Bohm." She and her husband [George Busse (elder)](george-busse-elder.md) had at least five children baptised in Tallinn between 1726 and 1739.

Her son [Georg Busse](georg-busse.md) (b. 1727) married Margaretha Elisabeth Bischanz and was father of [Ernst Gottlieb Busse](ernst-gottlieb-busse.md), continuing the line into the Eylandt family through Ernst Gottlieb's daughter Maria Elisabeth.
