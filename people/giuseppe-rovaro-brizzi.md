# Giuseppe Rovaro Brizzi

**Giuseppe Rovaro Brizzi** was born and domiciled in **Zara**. He married [Anastasia Castellanich](anastasia-castellanich.md) **25 August 1821** there and was father of [Geralda Rovero Brizzi](geralda-rovero-brizzi.md).

## Life

- **25 Aug 1821** — Marriage **Zara** to [Anastasia Castellanich](anastasia-castellanich.md) (date echoed from daughter’s **1825** birth register).
- Occupation: **Venditore di Sali e Tabacchi al minuto** (retail salt and tobacco seller) per Gerarda's 1825 birth register; by the 1845 marriage register described as **Venditore di Tabacchi all'Ingrosso** (wholesale tobacco seller) — the business grew or was characterised differently over 20 years.
- Address: Zara (Calle name partially legible in 1825 register; wife's address given as Calle S. Michele Nº 590).

## Family

- Wife: [Anastasia Castellanich](anastasia-castellanich.md) (seamstress, *Cucitrice*).
- Children: [Geralda Rovero Brizzi](geralda-rovero-brizzi.md).
