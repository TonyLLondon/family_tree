# Francisco Cerpa

**Francisco Cerpa** married [Ana Delia González](ana-gonzalez-i349.md) in **1909** at Chanco (civil registry marriage entry 59) and was father of [Miguel Cerpa González](miguel-cerpa-gonzalez-i346.md), [Juana de Dios Cerpa González](juana-de-dios-cerpa-gonzalez.md), [René Cerpa González](rene-cerpa-gonzalez-i353.md), and [Narciso Cerpa Gonzales](narciso-cerpa-gonzales-i354.md). The Chanco marriage index confirms his bride's full name as **Ana Delia González**, previously recorded only as "Ana" in the family tree.

The Cerpa surname was well established in Chanco during this period. The same civil registry index records other Cerpa branches: a Horacio Cerpa J. married Ana Rosa Garrido in 1920, and a Genina Cerpa married José Antonio Cáceres A. in 1910 — likely Francisco's relatives.

No death entry for Francisco appears in the Chanco deaths index (covering approximately 1918–1932), so he was alive at least into the early 1930s.
