# Geralda Rovero Brizzi

Geralda Rovero Brizzi was the wife of [Pietro Paolo Addobbati](pietro-paolo-addobbati.md) and mother of [Pietro Pio Addobbati](pietro-pio-addobbati.md) and [Simeone Gilberto Addobbati](simeone-gilberto-addobbati.md). Her baptismal name was **Gerarda Maria** (birth register Entry 107); the formal name line in the working tree uses **Geralda**, a later spelling variant.

## Life

- **12 Jul 1825** — Born **Zara, Dalmatia**, at 1 PM. Baptized **15 Aug 1825** (feast of the Assumption). Baptismal name: **Gerarda Maria**. Legitimate.
- **26 Oct 1845** — Marriage **Zara** to Pietro Paolo Addobbati, at the Collegiate Parish Church. She was **underage** (20, below Austrian majority of 24) and required **Praetor's consent** (granted 14 July 1845, two days after her 20th birthday). Listed as **nubile** (unmarried), Catholic, domiciled in this parish.
- **10 Mar 1908** — Died (marginal annotation **"† 10/III. 1908"** on her birth register entry; the working tree had only "AFT 1890").

## Family

- Husband: [Pietro Paolo Addobbati](pietro-paolo-addobbati.md).
- Parents: [Giuseppe Rovaro Brizzi](giuseppe-rovaro-brizzi.md) (salt and tobacco retailer, *Venditore di Sali e Tabacchi al minuto*); [Anastasia Castellanich](anastasia-castellanich.md) (seamstress, *Cucitrice*; parents married **25 Aug 1821** in this parish). Address: Calle S. Michele Nº 590, Zara.
- Children include [Pietro Pio Addobbati](pietro-pio-addobbati.md) and [Simeone Gilberto Addobbati](simeone-gilberto-addobbati.md).
- Godparents at baptism: **Pietro Battara** (printer, Calle S. Crisogono), **Giovanni Durmanti** (printer, Calle S. Rocco Nº 26), **Antonia Brizzi** (washerwoman — likely a relative of the father).
