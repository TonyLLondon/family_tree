# Dr. Pietro Addobbati

Brother of [Vincenzo Giovanni Domenico Valentino Addobbati](vincenzo-giovanni-domenico-valentino-addobbati.md) (I29); sons of [Luigi Anzolo Alloisio Addobbati](luigi-anzolo-alloisio-addobbati.md) and [Maddelena Plarino](maddelena-plarino.md). Knight of the **Papal Order of the Golden Spur** and **Lateran Count** (1786); pre-eminent lawyer of late-Venetian and Napoleonic Zara; noble of **Nin** (1804).

## Life

- **1769** — Born, Zara (Sabalich p. 506).
- **1786** — During studies at the **University of Padua**, received the order of the **Speron d'Oro** (Golden Spur) and the title of **Conte Lateranense** (Lateran Count) from Bishop Ivan Dominik Stratico. Fulvia's 1996 memoir records that only a hundred knights held the title at any one time and that the miniature of the order was passed down through the family to Peter Martin Lewis.
- **1790** — Doctoral degree by diploma; already signing decrees as *Avvocato* (decree of Angelo Diedo to the villagers of Ugljan).
- **1793** — Member of the **Accademia Economo-Agraria della Capitale della Dalmazia** (under Venetian sovereignty).
- **1794** — *Avvocato criminale* and *Avvocato dei poveri prigionieri* (criminal lawyer and lawyer for poor prisoners).
- **1797** — *Capo e Procuratore dell'ordine civico* during the Extraordinary Civic Congress, amid the fall of the Venetian Republic.
- **1798** — Count Thurn appoints him I.R. *Giudice assessore del Tribunale di I. istanza* (Associate Judge, Tribunal of First Instance) in Zara under the new Austrian administration.
- **24 Jun 1804** — Formally inscribed as noble of **Nin** alongside brother Ivan Vicko (Vincenzo), sons of Luigi. Diploma with imperial arms, arms of Nin, and Addobbati family arms: *"D.r Petri et Joanes Vincentis fratres Addobbati q m Aloysium"*.
- **1806** — Dandolo names him *Membro del Consiglio Comunale* (10 July) and one of four *Amministratori Aggiunti* (28 July).
- **1807** — Under the Kingdom of Italy, Dandolo appoints him *Protetor legale dei carcerati* and *Patrocinator di I. classe per il Distretto di Zara e Contado*.
- **1810** — Praised by his adversary Curnis in a published Venice court case: *"Signor Pietro Addobbati, probo e celebre avvocato di cui basta pronunciar il nome per udirne delle lodi ben giuste."*
- **1812** — Napoleon creates him *Giudice supplente* (Substitute Judge).
- **1815** — Member of the *Deputazione Sanitaria*.
- **1824–1825** — *Primo Assessore dell'Amministrazione Comunale di Zara*; also *Pubblica Beneficenza*, *Consigliere della Fabbrica della Metropolitana Arcivescovile*, and member of the *Commissione di Ripartizione* (1816).
- **4 Nov 1832** — Died, Zara.
