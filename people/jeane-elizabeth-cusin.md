# Jeane Elizabeth Cusin

Jeane Elizabeth Cusin was born around **1685** and buried **4 December 1753** at Aubonne, Vaud. She married [Jean Pierre Montagnon](jean-pierre-montagnon.md) (b. 1692, d. 1737) and was the mother of [Jean François Louis Montagnon](jean-francois-louis-montagnon.md).

She outlived her husband by 16 years. Her name appears in records as both "Cusin" and "Cuzin."

## Family

- Husband: [Jean Pierre Montagnon](jean-pierre-montagnon.md) (b. 1692, d. 5 Mar 1737, Aubonne)
- Son: [Jean François Louis Montagnon](jean-francois-louis-montagnon.md) (b. ~1737, d. 1786)
