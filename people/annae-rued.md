# Annae Rued

**Annae Rued** was the first wife of [Hanssen Stump](hanssen-stump-1580.md) (c. 1580–1647) in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. She is named as the mother of [Jacob Stump](jacob-stump-1606.md) in his 1606 baptism record — the only primary source documenting her.

## Family

- Husband: [Hanssen Stump](hanssen-stump-1580.md)
- Son: [Jacob Stump](jacob-stump-1606.md) (baptized 23 Oct 1606, Sulgen)
- Daughter: Elisabeth Stump (c. 1610, married Uli Muntz 1630)

## Evidence

- **Son's baptism record:** Jacob Stump, 23 Oct 1606, Sulgen, parents "Hanssen Stump" & "Annae Rued" · FamilySearch FV8P-D8C
- **Daughter's marriage record:** Elisabeth Stump married Uli Muntz, 2 Nov 1630, father "Hans Stumpen" · FamilySearch 68VB-LZPG (confirms she had a daughter, though Annae is not named directly)

## Research Notes

The Rued/Rüd surname was established in the Sulgen area. Annae appears only in Jacob's baptism record; no separate birth, marriage, or death record has been found. She was likely Hanssen's first wife, succeeded by Barbel Frue (children from 1623) and Cathrin Bommeli (children from 1627).
