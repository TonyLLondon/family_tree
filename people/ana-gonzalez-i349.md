# Ana Delia González

**Ana Delia González** married [Francisco Cerpa](francisco-cerpa-i348.md) in **1909** at Chanco (civil registry marriage entry 59). She was mother of [Miguel Cerpa González](miguel-cerpa-gonzalez-i346.md), [Juana de Dios Cerpa González](juana-de-dios-cerpa-gonzalez.md), [René Cerpa González](rene-cerpa-gonzalez-i353.md), and [Narciso Cerpa Gonzales](narciso-cerpa-gonzales-i354.md). The family tree previously recorded her name simply as "Ana"; the Chanco marriage index confirms her full name as **Ana Delia González**.

Her children's birth registrations in 1912 are indexed under "Cerpa González," confirming the González maternal surname. No death entry for Ana Delia appears in the Chanco deaths index (covering approximately 1918–1932).
