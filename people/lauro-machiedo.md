# Lauro Machiedo

Head of the **Machiedo** household on **Hvar** in the generation above **[Dr. Jerko Machiedo](jerko-machiedo.md)** and **[Juraj-Zorko Machiedo](juraj-zorko-machiedo.md)**. Kovačić treats him as a central figure in the island’s late‑19th‑century cultural politics (Croatian-national current within a largely Italianate family archive).

## Family (Kovačić 2014)

- Wife from **1876**: **[Magdalena Novak](magdalena-novak-machiedo.md)** “Mandina,” “Bonaparte,” from a prominent Hvar shipowning **Novak** line.
- Children named: **Jerko**, **Juraj-Zorko**, **Ružica**, **Kreuza**.
