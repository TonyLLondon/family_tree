# Maureen Catherine Finbarr White

**Molly** White carried **Anglo-India** in her bones—born **2 June 1919** at **Madras**, christened there **8 June** the same week the peace treaties still shivered across Europe. She was daughter of [Gerald Sebastian White](gerald-sebastian-white.md) and [Mary O’Shea](mary-oshea.md), and in time wife to **[Robert Marc Murard Stump](robert-marc-murard-stump.md)**, the **Tehran-born** son of a Swiss–Baltic dentist who had made **Qajar** teeth fashionable. Their daughter **[Kitty / Catherine](catherine-stump.md)** is the path to [Anthony Robert Lewis](anthony-robert-lewis.md).

Family memory sets the courtship in **wartime Essex**—two **teachers** in the same pressured English spring, continents away from the heat she’d first known. She outlived Robert by nearly a decade, dying **29 August 2006** at **Kingston upon Thames**, Surrey—the Thames-side sprawl a long way from the Coromandel coast, but still inside the same British civil register.
