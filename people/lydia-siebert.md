# Lydia Siebert

Wife of [Arthur Roger Stump](arthur-roger-stump.md). Lydia died during the Russian Civil War period at **Grebblo** in **Novgorod province**, far from her native Tallinn.

## Life

- **Born 6 July 1873** in Tallinn ([EBC 2005](../sources/estonian-biographical-center-stump-report-2005.md)).
- **Married** Arthur Roger Stump in **Moscow** (date unknown; noted in the 1902–1926 Tallinn Dome register of parishioners).
- **Died 11 March 1920** in **Grebblo**, Novgorod province, Russia.
- Obituary published in *Revaler Bote* no. 89-1920. She was mourned by **her siblings** — not by her husband, which is unusual and may indicate Arthur was unreachable at the time (the family was scattered across former Russian Empire territories during the post-1917 upheaval).

## Family

- Children: at least two sons (per Arthur's 1935 obituary).
