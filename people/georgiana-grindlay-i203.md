# Georgiana Talbot Grindlay

**Georgiana Talbot Grindlay** was born on **14 June 1835** in **Middlesex**. She was the daughter of **William Grindley** (I204) and his wife (I208).

She married **[David Fensom](david-fensom-i202.md)** on **18 June 1861** and settled in Islington. Their children included [Georgina Elizabeth](georgina-fensom-i205.md) (~1862), **David William** (Oct 1864), **Annie** (~1870), **Ellen** (I207, ~1875), and **Fanny** (I209, ~1877).

The **1881 Census** found the family at **8 Milton Place, Lower Holloway** — Georgiana recorded as 45, alongside her husband David (house decorator, 46) and all five children. By **1901** the couple were at **13 Eden Grove**, Islington, with David now a general labourer at 65 and Georgiana recorded as 65.

Georgiana died in **1921** in **Edmonton, Middlesex**.
