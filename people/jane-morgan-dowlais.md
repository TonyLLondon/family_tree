# Jane Morgan

**Jane** was born about **1828** at **Dowlais**, Merthyr Tydfil, Glamorganshire. She married [Keziah Morgan](keziah-morgan.md) (b. 7 Mar 1824, Nantyglo, Aberystruth, Monmouthshire), a collier who had moved from the western Monmouthshire ironworks valleys to the Merthyr area by the late 1840s.

The couple settled on **Pond Street** in Dowlais, where they raised at least six children. The [1871 census](../media/docs/1871-census-keziah-morgan-household-dowlais-rg10-5397.jpg) records the household: Keziah (44, head, collier, born Blaina), **Jane** (43, wife, born Dowlais), and children [Mary](mary-morgan.md) (17), Margaret (16), Ann (12), Elizabeth (7), Jane (5), and Samuel (2).

Jane's maiden name remains unknown. The earlier working tree incorrectly listed her as "Amelia" — primary sources (the 1871 census) disprove this.
