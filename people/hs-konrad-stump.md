# Hs. Konrad Stump (b. 1730)

**Hans Konrad Stump** (baptized 30 October 1730) was the son of [Hans Conrad Stump Sr.](hans-conrad-stump-sr.md) and [Elssbeth Straub](elssbeth-straub.md) in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. He married [Anna Margaretha Jung](anna-margaretha-jung.md) in 1766 and became the father of [Hans Ulrich Stump](hans-ulrich-stump.md), continuing the Swiss STUMP lineage through the late 18th century.

## Life

**30 Oct 1730** — Christened **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. Son of [Hans Conrad Stump](hans-conrad-stump-1697.md) and [Elssbeth Straub](elssbeth-straub.md).

**1766** — Marriage to [Anna Margaretha Jung](anna-margaretha-jung.md) (b. 1735).
