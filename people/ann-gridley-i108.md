# Ann Gridley (~1825–?)

**Ann Gridley** of **Tillingham** on the Dengie Peninsula married **George Coolbear**, a Hazeleigh carpenter; their son **[George Gridley Coolbear](george-gridley-coolbear-i105.md)** (1844–1892) heads the Essex branch that reaches **[May Coolbear](may-coolbear-i100.md)**.
