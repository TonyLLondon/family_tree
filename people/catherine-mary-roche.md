# Catherine Mary Roche

**Catherine Mary Roche** is the **Dublin** daughter who followed her **surgeon husband** to **Bombay** and spent her widow decades on **Denny Street, Tralee**—**Kerry** light after **India** heat. Wife of [William O'Byrne White](william-obyrne-white.md); married **8 February 1869** on the **Bombay** side of empire; mother of [Gerald Sebastian White](gerald-sebastian-white.md), who would repeat the pattern as **Madras** registrar.

## Life

- **1841** — Born **Dublin**.
- **8 Feb 1869** — Marriage **Bombay Presidency, India** to William O’Byrne White.
- **1901 / 1911** — Census: **Tralee Urban**, **Denny Street**, wife of household head.
- **1937** — Died **Tralee, Kerry** (export + FamilySearch Irish deaths index).

## Family

- Father: [William Roche](william-roche.md) — Burke’s **1833**: “William, a solicitor in Dublin, married Eliza… daughter and co-heir of — Knight”; son of **Thomas Roche of Limerick**.
- Mother: **Eliza Knight** — co-heiress with sister Catherine; both sisters married **Roche** brothers.
