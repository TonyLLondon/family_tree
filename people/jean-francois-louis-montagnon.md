# Jean François Louis Montagnon

Jean François Louis Montagnon was born around 1737 at Aubonne, Vaud, the son of [Jean Pierre Montagnon](jean-pierre-montagnon.md) (b. 1692, d. 1737) and [Jeane Elizabeth Cusin](jeane-elizabeth-cusin.md) (b. 1685, d. 1753). His father died in March 1737, the same year he was born — he may have been a posthumous child.

On **28 October 1761** he married [Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard](jaqueline-suzanne-chenevard.md) (b. 1732, Céligny, daughter of Benjamin Chenevard + Christine Nerfin) at the Reformed church of Céligny, his wife's home parish. Their first child Jean Gédéon was baptised at Céligny on 9 May 1762, after which the couple settled at **Aubonne**, where the remaining children were born between 1764 and 1776.

He died on **4 February 1786** at Aubonne, aged about 49.

His son [Louis Montagnon](louis-montagnon.md) married Jeanne Françoise Dumartheray and fathered [Jeanne Bénigne Montagnon](jeanne-benigne-montagnon.md), who married [Hans Jacob Stump](hans-jacob-stump-1800.md) in 1832.

**Parentage correction:** An earlier research pass mistakenly identified this man's parents as Jean Michel Montagnon + Catherine Vougaz, based on a burial record (6NQ2-TV9P, d. 1817). That burial is for a different Jean Louis Montagnon (b. 1743, son of Jean Michel) who married Jeanne Françoise Vougaz in 1780. The correct parents (Jean Pierre + Jeanne Cusin) are confirmed by multiple family register entries at Céligny (6NQL-FNTW, 6NQ2-K2PD, etc.).
