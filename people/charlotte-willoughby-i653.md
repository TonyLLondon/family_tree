# Charlotte Willoughby

**Charlotte Willoughby** was born about **1832** in **St Paul's Cray** (also recorded as "St Pancras Cray"), a parish in north-west Kent near Orpington. Her father was **Richard Willoughby**, named on her 1851 marriage record.

On **7 June 1851** she married **[Richard Webb](richard-webb-i652.md)**, an agricultural labourer from nearby Eynsford, at St Paul's Cray church. Their daughter [Clara](clara-webb-i597.md) was born in the same quarter (Q2 1851, Bromley registration district).

By the **1861 Census** Charlotte (29) was living with Richard and Clara at Crocker Hill Road, Crockenhill, in the ecclesiastical parish of All Souls (within Eynsford civil parish). Her birthplace is recorded as **St Pancras Cray** — the same place as her daughter Clara's.
