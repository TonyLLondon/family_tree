# Joseph Burgess

**Joseph Burgess** was born **14 November 1735** at **Leicester** into the Quaker meeting there, son of **John** and **Hannah Burgess**. He married [Sarah Airey](sarah-airey.md) under Quaker discipline on **6 February 1769** at the **Kendal Monthly Meeting**, Westmorland, and was father of [Henry Burgess](henry-burgess.md). He died **26 March 1807** and was buried **29 March 1807**, aged 71, recorded in the Leicester Monthly Meeting burial register.

The Quaker marriage record at Kendal names his father as **John Burgess**, confirmed independently by both his birth register and his burial register — three separate documents in the Society of Friends' registers all naming the same father.
