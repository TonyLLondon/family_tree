# Maria Zuajetto

Maria Zuajetto (also recorded as **Quajetto**) was a Venetian seamstress who settled in Zadar with her husband [Antonio Guerino](antonio-guerino.md), a sailor in the Austrian Imperial Navy. Their daughter [Antonia Maria Guerino](antonia-maria-guerino.md) was born in Zadar on 1 February 1825. Both Maria and Antonio were dead by 1850.

The 1850 marriage register of her daughter records her as **"Quajetto Maria, di Venezia, Cusitrice"** — a seamstress from Venice. Her daughter Antonia Maria also took up the needle trade, listed as a *sarta* (seamstress) at the time of her own marriage.

## Open questions

- **Zuajetto / Quajetto** — the two forms appear in different records. The Venetian dialect may explain the variation (initial Z/Q interchange is common in Veneto dialects).
- **Venice birth and marriage:** Venetian parish or civil records could hold the marriage of Antonio Guerino and Maria Zuajetto/Quajetto, and Maria's own baptism record.
- **Death record:** Deceased before 1850. Death registers in Zadar may contain her burial.
