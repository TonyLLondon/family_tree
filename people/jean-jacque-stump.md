# Jean Jacque Herman Eugen Stump

First child of [Marc Francois Stump](marc-francois-stump.md) and [Olga Caroline Erbe](olga-caroline-erbe.md); died in childhood at age four.

## Life

- **Born 24 June 1869** at 1 PM in Tallinn ([EBC 2005](../sources/estonian-biographical-center-stump-report-2005.md)).
- **Baptised 3 August 1869** at home, registered in **Tallinn Dome** congregation. Godparents: Mr Man Court's secretary **Hermann Erbe**, Mr governor's school principal and state councilor **Dr. Gahlnbäck**, Mrs **von Berg** née **von Sievers** (wife of a doctor), Miss **Pauline Köhler**.
- **Died 7 May 1874** PM in Tallinn; cause of death: **heart disorder**.
- **Buried 10 May 1874** PM, Tallinn Dome congregation.
