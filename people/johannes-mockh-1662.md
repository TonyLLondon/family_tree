# Johannes Mockh (1662-1736)

**Johannes Mockh** (1662-1736) was a Swiss man from **Sulgen**, **Thurgau** who married [Margaretha Schoenholtzer](margaretha-schoenholtzer.md) and became the father of [Margreth Mockh](margreth-mockh.md) (baptized 1706). Through his daughter's marriage to [Hans Ulrich Jung](hanss-ulrich-jung.md), he became the maternal great-grandfather of the STUMP lineage in Switzerland.

## Life

**27 Sep 1661** — Christened **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland** (recorded as "Johanness Mock"). Son of [Thoma Mock](thoma-mock.md) and [Anna Sprenger](anna-sprenger.md).

**c. 1700** — Marriage to [Margaretha Schoenholtzer](margaretha-schoenholtzer.md).

**10 Jan 1706** — Birth of daughter [Margreth Mockh](margreth-mockh.md) in Sulgen.

**15 Mar 1736** — Died **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, age 74 years. Buried at Sulgen.
