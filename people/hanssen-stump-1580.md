# Hanssen Stump (c. 1580)

**Hanssen Stump** (born c. 1580, died 27 May 1647) was the **earliest confirmed direct ancestor** in the STUMP lineage. He lived his entire documented life in **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**, married at least three wives, and fathered at least eight documented children across four decades. Through his son [Jacob Stump](jacob-stump-1606.md) (1606), he is the progenitor of our continuous direct line.

## Life

**c. 1580** — Born in or near **Sulgen**, **Thurgau**, **Switzerland**. His birth predates the start of Sulgen's parish registers (which begin in 1590), so no baptism record exists.

**c. 1604** — Marriage to [Annae Rued](annae-rued.md) (first wife).

**23 Oct 1606** — Birth of son [Jacob Stump](jacob-stump-1606.md) in Sulgen.

**c. 1610** — Birth of daughter **Elisabeth Stump** (estimated from her 1630 marriage).

**c. 1620** — Marriage to **Barbel Frue** (second wife), after death or separation from Annae Rued.

**11 Dec 1623** — Birth of daughter **Margret Stump** with Barbel Frue.

**1 Apr 1627** — Birth of daughter **Catharina Stump** with Barbel Frue.

**15 Mar 1628** — Birth of daughter **Elssbeth Stump** with Barbel Frue.

**c. 1625** — Marriage to **Cathrin Bommeli** (third wife).

**2 Dec 1627** — Birth of son **Hanss Stump** with Cathrin Bommeli.

**29 Apr 1630** — Birth of son **Hanns Stump** with Drin Bommeli (same wife, variant name).

**2 Nov 1630** — Daughter **Elisabeth Stump** married **Uli Muntz**, son of Ulrich Muntz, in Sulgen. The marriage record names the bride's father as "Hans Stumpen."

**3 Mar 1633** — Birth of son **Jacob Stump** with Cathrin Bommeli.

**8 Jun 1643** — A younger son **Hannss Stump** married **Anna Häberlin** (daughter of Jörg Häberli). The marriage record names the groom's father as "Hanss St[ump]."

**27 May 1647** — Died in **Sulgen**. Buried same day.

## Research Notes (2026-04-11)

**Why No Baptism Record Exists:**
The Sulgen Evangelisch-Reformierte Kirchenbuch begins in **1590**. Hanssen was born c. 1580, approximately ten years before systematic parish record-keeping started. His baptism is simply not in the surviving registers.

**Connection to Earlier STUMP Families — Unresolved:**
Multiple STUMP families were established in Sulgen by the 1590s: **Blaesi Stumpp** (children 1591–1601), **Augustin Stumpp** (children 1595–1602), **Andreas Stumpp** (child 1591), **Anstett Stumpp** (children 1596–1604). Hanssen (c. 1580) was likely a son or younger brother of one of these patriarchs, but no record directly names his father. A speculative connection to [Cunrat Stump](cunrat-stump-1500s.md) (c. 1500, Zürich) remains unproven.

**Three Marriages:**
The baptism records reveal three distinct wives across Hanssen's long life (c. 1580–1647): Annae Rued for the earliest children (c. 1604–1610), Barbel Frue for children in the 1620s, and Cathrin/Drin Bommeli for later children (1627–1633). Sequential mortality of wives was common in this period.

**1629 Plague Survivor:**
While five STUMP family members died in the 1629 plague epidemic, Hanssen survived until 1647, suggesting he had some measure of isolation or immunity during the crisis.

**Another Hannss Stumpe (d. 1629):**
A separate "Hannss Stumpe" died 2 Oct 1629 in Sulgen during the plague (FamilySearch 68VT-5XDL). This was likely Hanssen's brother, cousin, or nephew — not our Hanssen who lived until 1647.
