# Sarah Watkins (Morgan)

**Sarah Watkins** was born about **1806**, probably near the border of Brecknockshire and Carmarthenshire. On the 1881 census she gave her birthplace as **Builth, Brecknockshire**; on the 1851 census the enumerator recorded **Llanddaysaint, Carmarthenshire** — these are about 25 miles apart in the upper Usk and Towy valleys.

On **24 October 1831** she married [William Morgan](william-morgan-llanfairarybryn.md) at **Llanfair-ar-y-bryn** parish church, Carmarthenshire. Both parties were already resident in the parish. Their daughter [Anne](anne-morgan.md) (b. ~1829–1830) was born shortly before the marriage.

By 1881 Sarah, now a **widow** aged 75, was living with Anne's family at **11 Clive Street, Aberdare**. The census recorded her as "Mother In Law" to [Thomas Griffiths](thomas-griffiths.md), with the occupation "House Property" — she owned rental property.

Sarah died on **27 June 1887**, aged 82, and was buried at **St Fagan's, Aberdare** — the same parish church where her granddaughter Margaret had been buried twenty-four years earlier.

## Open questions

- **Parents** — maiden name Watkins is confirmed from the 1831 marriage, but the register does not name her father (pre-1837 form). A baptism of Sarah Watkins ~1803–1809 in the Builth or Llanddaysaint area would identify her parents. The **Builth parish registers are not indexed** in any online database (FMP or FamilySearch); the original images on FamilySearch are restricted to FamilySearch Centers. All indexed Brecknockshire "Sarah Watkins" baptisms have been traced to other parishes: Glasbury (22 Mar 1807 and 27 May 1802), Llansanffraid-ar-Wysg (17 Jul 1807), Llanfeugan (28 Feb 1802), Llangatwg (5 Jul 1810). Non-conformist registers at Builth (Horeb Chapel Independent, 1805–1837) are also restricted.
- **Birthplace discrepancy** — 1851: Llanddaysaint, Carmarthenshire. 1881: Builth, Brecknockshire. These are ~25 miles apart. "Llanddaysaint" could be Llanddeusant (a parish on the Black Mountain near the county boundary) — the censuses may reflect different administrative perceptions of the border area.
- **Marriage bond** — a marriage bond for William Morgan × Sarah Watkins (22 Oct 1831, two days before the church marriage) exists on FamilySearch (FS `QGK5-FVXB`, image `3:1:3Q9M-CSJ6-9SDX-M`). Marriage bonds sometimes name the father and parish of both parties, but this image is restricted to FamilySearch Centers.
- **1861 census** — not found.
- **Death certificate** — GRO registration Q2 1887, Merthyr Tydfil district. Would confirm age and name an informant (likely Anne Griffiths née Morgan).
