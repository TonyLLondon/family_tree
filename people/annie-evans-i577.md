# Annie Evans (daughter, b. 1917)

**Annie** was the youngest child and only daughter of [Alfred Evans](alfred-evans-i101.md) and [Annie Fensom](annie-fensom-i291.md), born about **April 1917** in **Islington** and named after her mother. The **1921 Census** found her aged four at **4 Bratton Street, Islington**, the baby of a household of five alongside her brothers [Alfred Charles](alfred-charles-evans-i576.md) (8) and [Joseph David](joseph-evans-i99.md) (6).
