# Alfred Charles Evans

**Alfred Charles** was the eldest child of [Alfred Evans](alfred-evans-i101.md) and [Annie Fensom](annie-fensom-i291.md), born about **April 1913** in **Islington** — ten months after his parents' Christmas Day wedding at St David's Church. The **1921 Census** found him aged eight at **4 Bratton Street, Islington**, attending school full-time alongside his younger brother [Joseph David](joseph-evans-i99.md) (6) and sister [Annie](annie-evans-i577.md) (4).
