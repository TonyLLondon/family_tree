# Antonio Guerino

Antonio Guerino was a Venetian sailor in the Austrian Imperial Navy who settled in Zadar (Zara), Dalmatia. He married [Maria Zuajetto](maria-zuajetto.md), also from Venice, and their daughter [Antonia Maria Guerino](antonia-maria-guerino.md) was born in Zadar on 1 February 1825. Both Antonio and Maria were dead by the time Antonia Maria married in 1850.

The 1850 marriage register of his daughter records him as **"Guerino Antonio, padre defunto, di Venezia, fu Marinajo dell'I.R. marina Austriaca"** — a deceased former sailor from Venice who had served in the Austrian Imperial & Royal Navy.
