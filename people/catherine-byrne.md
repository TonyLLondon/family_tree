# Catherine Byrne

Mother of [William O'Byrne White](william-obyrne-white.md). She connects the **White** surname to **County Wicklow** on her birth side and **County Carlow** on her death side—geographically tight **Leinster** movement typical of small-farmer and professional families before the **Famine** and post-Famine decades.
