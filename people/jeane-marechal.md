# Jeane Marechal

Jeane Marechal was born around 1665 and died on **11 July 1742** at Aubonne in the Pays de Vaud. Her burial record names her as "Jeane Marechal Montagnon" (maiden + married surname, per Vaudois convention) and identifies her husband as **François Montagnon** — i.e. [Jean François Montagnon](jean-francois-montagnon-1666.md) (b. ~1667, d. 1732).

She survived her husband by ten years. She is the probable but **unconfirmed** mother of [Jean Pierre Montagnon](jean-pierre-montagnon.md) (b. 1692), a second Jean François (b. 1694), and Jean Michel (b. 1699) — no record explicitly names her children.

- Probable son: [Jean Pierre Montagnon](jean-pierre-montagnon.md) (b. 1692, d. 1737) — direct ancestor, **unconfirmed**
