# Clara Webb

**Clara Webb** was born about **1854** in **Chelsfield**, a village ~2 miles south of Orpington, Kent. She became housekeeper to the widowed chimney sweep **[Peter Martin](peter-martin-i596.md)** at Alpine Cottage, Frog Lane, Orpington. In the 1881 census she is listed as **unmarried** with surname **Webb**, and her three children with Peter — Arthur, William, and Albert — also carry the Webb surname. By 1891 she is listed as Peter's wife and all children have been re-surnamed **Martin**.

Her youngest child **[Rosina](rosina-a-martin-i104.md)** (b. 1891) married [Archibald Percy Coolbear](archibald-percy-coolbear.md), a chimney sweep like her father.

## Family

- Partner/husband: [Peter Martin](peter-martin-i596.md) (b. ~1840, master chimney sweep).
- Children: Arthur (~1877), William (~1879), Albert (~1881), Harry (~1884), Edward (~1888), [Rosina](rosina-a-martin-i104.md) (1891).

## Evidence

- **1881 Census** — Alpine Cottage, Frog Lane, Orpington (RG 11/859, p. 36, schedule 161): Clara Webb, housekeeper, unmarried, 27, born Chelsfield. Three children surnamed Webb. [Source card](../sources/1881-census-martin-orpington.md).
- **1891 Census** — 1 Alfrons Cottages, Orpington (RG 12/634, p. 61, schedule 171): Clara Martin, wife, 40. Children now surnamed Martin. [Source card](../sources/1891-census-martin-orpington.md).

## Open questions
