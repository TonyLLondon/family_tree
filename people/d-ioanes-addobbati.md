# D. Ioanes Addobbati

GEDCOM **`@I32@`** uses **NPFX Captain** ("**Captain**"); **F17** lists **HUSB @I32@** and **CHIL @I31@** with **no WIFE** in the export — mother of [D. Daniele Addobbati](d-daniele-addobbati.md) unknown in this file. Giovanni (Ivan) Addobbati in the Italian/Croatian forms — father of the three brothers who founded the Dalmatian line.

## Life

- **1676** — Born (GEDCOM year only; Bergamo or Venetian Terraferma).
- **Capitano dei corrazieri** — captain of cuirassiers (Sabalich p. 503). This resolves the GEDCOM prefix "Captain" to a specific heavy-cavalry command in the Venetian military.
- Father of three sons who crossed the Adriatic: **D. Daniele** (1702), **D. Laurentius** (1705), **D. Iosephus** (1709). The sons were present in the Zara area by **1728** (Turanj parish registers per DAZD inventory) and in Zara itself by **1730** (Daniele's marriage), before being formally admitted to the *cittadini originari* on **13 Nov 1733** (Sabalich p. 503). The **1745 Bergamo testimonial** (DAZD **sig. 46**) documents Giovanni (Ivan) as the father of the three brothers in the chain of six generations from Pietro de Adobatis (1495).
