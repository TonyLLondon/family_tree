# René Bottin

Fourth child of [Julien Bottin](julien-bottin.md) and [Fanny Burgess](fanny-burgess.md), born **~1892** in Tehran.

## Life

- **~1892** — Born in Tehran, Iran (1901 census gives birth year as 1892, birthplace France; Henderson FBB Timeline gives Tehran).
- **31 Mar 1901** — Enumerated in the 1901 England census at Hammersmith, London, as "Rene Bottin", age 9, born in France (French Subject), living with mother [Fanny](fanny-burgess.md) and siblings.
- **1919** — Per Fanny's timeline, the sons were in France after serving in the First World War.
