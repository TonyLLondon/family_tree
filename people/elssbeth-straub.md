# Elssbeth Straub (early 1700s)

**Elssbeth Straub** was a Swiss woman from **Sulgen**, **Thurgau** who married [Hans Conrad Stump Sr.](hans-conrad-stump-sr.md) in the early 18th century. She became the mother of [Hans Konrad Stump](hs-konrad-stump.md) (baptized 1730), establishing the maternal lineage in Generation 10 of the Swiss STUMP family.
