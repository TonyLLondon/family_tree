# Hermann Eberhard Erbe

Father of [Olga Caroline Erbe](olga-caroline-erbe.md) and [Eugen Edmund Erbe](eugen-edmund-erbe.md). In the MyHeritage import he is the hinge between **Thuringian Gotha** (his father [Johann Georg Sigismund Erbe](johann-georg-sigismund-erbe.md) was born there and built a career in the **Baltic provinces**) and the **Estonian–Latvian** cities where Hermann himself was born, married, worked, and died.

## Life

- **3 September 1814** — Born **Rakvere** (Wesenberg), a town in the north Estonian interior that was part of the **Russian Empire’s Baltic German administrative world** (historically Livonia / Estland depending on period).
- **27 March 1839** — Married [Emilie Ida Eylandt](emilie-ida-eylandt.md) in **Tallinn (Reval)** (**F140**).
- Occupation in the embedded Geni text: **Domschloßvogt** — a German title usually tied to **cathedral chapter administration**; exact institution (Riga, Tallinn, or another see) must be taken from a primary appointment list or personnel file, not from Geni alone.
- **22 January 1872** — Died **Riga** (Vidzeme). His wife Emilie died **14 July 1872** in **Tallinn**; the six-month gap suggests she may not have accompanied him to Riga at the end, or that he died on business while she remained on the Estonian coast.
