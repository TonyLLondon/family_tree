# Anne Morgan (Griffiths)

**Anne** — née **Morgan**, maiden name confirmed by her widowed mother **Sarah Morgan** living with the Griffiths family in the 1881 census — was born about **1829** at **Llanfair**, Carmarthenshire. The 1871 enumerator wrote "Llan Ferbyn" and the 1881 return has "Llanfair" — likely **Llanfair-ar-y-bryn** near Llandovery, though Llanfihangel-ar-arth is also possible without a certificate.

She married [Thomas Griffiths](thomas-griffiths.md) of Trecastle, Breconshire, and by 1861 the couple were in **Aberdare**. Their four surviving children — Elizabeth/Eliza, [Catherine](catherine-griffiths.md), Margaret, and David — were all born in Aberdare between ~1860 and ~1869.

## Life

- **~1829** — Born **Llanfair**, Carmarthenshire (1871: "Llan Ferbyn, Carmarthenshire"; 1881: "Llanfair, Carmarthenshire").
- **1861** — Age 31, wife of head, Aberdare (RG09/4068).
- **1871** — Age 41, Aberdare (RG10/5405).
- **1881** — Age 52, Aberdare (RG11/5320). Her mother **Sarah Morgan** (75, widow, "house property", born Brecknockshire) in the household. Cousin **John Morgan** (34, coal miner, born Llanfair, Carmarthenshire) also present.

## Family
