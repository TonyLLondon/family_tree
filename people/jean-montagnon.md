# Jean Montagnon

Jean Montagnon is the **earliest known ancestor** of the Montagnon family in Switzerland. He appears only in the baptism records of two children at an unspecified Swiss parish (likely Aubonne): **François** (22 Dec 1667) and **Elisabet** (7 Nov 1670).

Nothing else is known about him — no birth, death, or marriage record has been found. Given the strong Montagnon surname presence in the Isère and Ardèche departments of southeastern France in this era, he may have been a Huguenot refugee who settled in Vaud during the mid-1600s.

## Family

- Son: [Jean François Montagnon](jean-francois-montagnon-1666.md) (bap. 22 Dec 1667, d. 1732, Aubonne)
- Daughter: Elisabet Montagnon (bap. 7 Nov 1670)
