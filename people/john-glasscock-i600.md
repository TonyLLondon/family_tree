# John Glasscock (1773–?)

**John Glasscock** was baptised **14 February 1773** at **St Mary, Whitechapel**, London. His parents were **[Abraham Glasscock](abraham-glasscock-i602.md)** and **Mary** (née Thompson), who lived at **Colchester Street**, Whitechapel. He was the second known child of Abraham and Mary, after Ann Thompson Glascock (b. ~1768), and had a younger brother Abraham Jr. (bapt. 1777, Bethnal Green).

John married twice. His first wife, **Sarah**, was the mother of his son [James](james-glasscock-i598.md) (bapt. 1795, Stepney). After Sarah's death he married **Rebecca Haley** on **28 June 1824** at Stepney.

## Family

- Father: **[Abraham Glasscock](abraham-glasscock-i602.md)** (bapt. 1735, Bishop's Stortford; m. Mary Thompson 1768).
- Mother: **Mary Thompson** (m. 17 Jan 1768, St Botolph Aldgate).
- First wife: **Sarah** (died before 1824).
- Second wife: **Rebecca Haley** (m. 28 Jun 1824, Stepney).
- Son (by Sarah): **[James Glasscock](james-glasscock-i598.md)** (bapt. 4 Jan 1795, Stepney; painter).
- Siblings: Ann Thompson Glascock (b. ~1768), Abraham Jr. (bapt. 1777), Hannah (b. ~1779), Mary Perry (b. ~1779).
