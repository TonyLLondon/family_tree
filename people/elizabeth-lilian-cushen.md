# Elizabeth Lilian Cushen

**Lily** in family speech — **Elizabeth Lilian Cushen** in the registers — was born **21 August 1886** at **Dowlais**, in the Merthyr Tydfil registration district, daughter of [David Cushen](david-cushen.md) and [Mary Morgan](mary-morgan.md).

By the age of fourteen she was in service: the 1901 census found her as a **general servant** on a farm at **Pentrebach** (Bynea Gurman), working for Thomas Kinsey, a farmer, alongside two carters. She was the only woman besides the farmer's wife on the holding.

In **Q1 1908** Lily married **James Lynch** at Merthyr Tydfil — she was twenty-one. The 1911 census records her as **Elizabeth Lynch** with her husband James, just the two of them, still at Merthyr Tydfil. James Lynch died between 1911 and 1917; the circumstances and date of his death have not yet been found.

In **Q4 1917** Lily married [Samuel Lewis](samuel-lewis.md) at Merthyr Tydfil, registered under her married name **Lily Lynch**. The GRO marriage index confirms the cross-reference: "Samuel Lewis married Lily Lynch" (Vol 11A p. 1378). Samuel was the miner who would become assurance man and Carmel deacon. Together they raised **[David John Lewis](david-john-lewis.md)** — the 1918 boy who would earn a Silver Star — and **[Brynmor Keith Lewis](brynmor-keith-lewis.md)** in the long rhythm of Penydarren Street.

She died **suddenly** in **Merthyr** in **November 1972**, a few years after Samuel. The news reached Florence through [Giuliana Zerauschek Rivolta](giuliana-zerauschek-rivolta.md), who passed it to [Mario Zerauschek](mario-zerauschek.md) and his wife; their condolence letter to David and Fulvia (12 Nov 1972) describes "l'improvvisa scomparsa della Signora Lewis."

## Open questions

- **James Lynch's death** — James Lynch (b. ~1879, Merthyr), loco fireman. Death not found in GRO index for south Wales 1911–1918 or in WWI records. Best GRO candidate: Q3 1912, Weardale, Durham (b. ~1881). As a loco fireman he could have died in a railway accident away from home.
- **David Cushen's death** — confirmed Q3 1907, Merthyr Tydfil, age 52, Vol 11A p. 341. Lily married James Lynch six months later.
- **Patrick Cushen and Thomas Cushen relationship** — both from Ireland, both in Merthyr. Patrick (b. ~1811) and Thomas (b. 1821, Cork) are likely brothers. Patrick was an overman, Thomas a labourer. Their family origins in Cork await further research.
