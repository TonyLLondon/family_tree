# Tallinn Dome Church — Stump / Erbe family records (1868–1880)

Nine page images from the Tallinn Dome Lutheran congregation (Toomkirik) registers, downloaded from FamilySearch on 30 March 2026. Collection: **Estonia, Church Books and Synagogue Registers, 1590-1941** (ID 1932350). Registers are in German. Surname transcription errors in the FamilySearch index: Stamp, Slump, Humse, Hamp — all from German Kurrent handwriting of "Stump."
