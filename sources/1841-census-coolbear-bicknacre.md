# 1841 Census — George Coolbear, apprentice at Bicknacre, Danbury

**George Coolbear** (15, born Essex) found as an apparent **apprentice carpenter** living with William and Hannah Stebbing at Bicknacre, Danbury. His future wife **Ann Gridley** (15, farm servant) is recorded on the **next page** of the same enumeration book — they were neighbours in the same hamlet three years before their marriage (~1844).
