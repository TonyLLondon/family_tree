## Summary

Scholarly article on the Italian consular presence in Split (Spalato) as a legal and cultural continuation of the late Venetian civic tradition. Covers the Italian-Dalmatian civic families who staffed the consular, postal, and administrative apparatus in the Habsburg Dalmatian cities; contextualises the I.R. postal assistant role within the broader framework of Italian-speaking professional life in the province.

## Use in this research

Provides context for the Habsburg postal administration's composition in Dalmatia and the role of Venetian civic families — including the *cittadini* class — in staffing it. Useful background for Pietro Pio Addobbati's position as *Ufficiale postale superiore* and for the DAZD reference to a "Pietro Addobbati, I.R. assistente alla Direzione delle Poste."
