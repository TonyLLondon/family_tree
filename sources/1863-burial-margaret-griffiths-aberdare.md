# Burial — Margaret Griffiths, St Fagan's, Aberdare (1863)

Parish burial register entry for **Margaret Griffiths**, buried **2 December 1863** at **St Fagan's, Aberdare**, age 8. Residence: Clive St. She was the eldest daughter of [Thomas Griffiths](../people/thomas-griffiths.md) and [Anne Morgan](../people/anne-morgan.md), born ~1855 in Aberdare. Confirmed by three independent indexes (Glamorganshire Burials, National Burial Index, Glamorgan Burial Index).
