## Indigenous Heritage Significance

**CALLAPAN** surname analysis:
- **Mapudungun etymology**: Calla ("valley") + Pan ("place of") = "Person from the valley clearing"  
- **255+ records** spanning 1724-1897 in Chilean church registers
- **Tribal affiliation**: Picunche (Northern Mapuche)
- **Geographic distribution**: Talca/Maule region - core Picunche territory

**MILLACOYAN** surname analysis:  
- **Pure Mapuche compound name**: Milla ("gold/forest") + Coyan ("gathering place")
- **Traditional indigenous naming pattern** linking individual to ancestral territories
- **Cultural significance**: Indicates ceremonial/spiritual place associations
