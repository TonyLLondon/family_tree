# St David's Church, Islington — Marriage Register (1912)

Microfilm scan of **Pages 6–7** from the register of marriages at **St David's Church, Islington**. Entry 13 records the marriage of **[Alfred Evans](../people/alfred-evans-i101.md)** and **[Annie Fensom](../people/annie-fensom-i291.md)** on **Christmas Day, 25 December 1912**. Both were 19 years old. Witnesses include **David William Fensom** (bride's father).
