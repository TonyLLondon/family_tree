## Summary

Analytical inventory of the Filippi family fonds. The Filippi were a prominent Zara *cittadini* family whose fonds preserve documents of the broader civic network. The inventory records references to **Pietro Addobbati** in documents within the fonds, confirming the Addobbati's legal and business interactions with the Filippi family.

## Use in this research

Evidence of the Addobbati's embeddedness in the Zara civic network. Pietro Addobbati appears as a party or counterparty in Filippi fonds documents, consistent with Angelo Daniele's notarial practice and the family's active involvement in Zara property and legal affairs.
