## Summary

Analytical inventory of the Petricioli family fonds. The Petricioli were one of Zara's most important *cittadini* families; the Addobbati appear among the intermarried civic network in the narrative. The fonds contains a **land register of 1814** bearing **Angelo Daniele Addobbati's notarial certification** — direct evidence of his practice crossing into other families' archives.

## Use in this research

The 1814 land register with Addobbati certification is the most specific cross-archive reference confirming Angelo Daniele Addobbati's notarial activity in the Zara property market. The Petricioli are also listed as a marriage-network family connected to the Addobbati in the broader Zara elite.
