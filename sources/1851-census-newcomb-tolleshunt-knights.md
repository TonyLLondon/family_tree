# 1851 Census — Newcomb household, Tolleshunt Knights

**William Newcomb** (58, labourer) and **Elizabeth** (57, born Layer Breton) heading a multi-generational household at Tolleshunt Knights. Son **James Newcomb** (28, labourer) and his wife **Susannah** née South (27) are living with them, along with three young children: Charles (7), James Jr (5), Sarah A (2). James and Susannah are **Alma Newcomb's parents** — Alma would be born ~4 years later.
