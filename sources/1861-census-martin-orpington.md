# 1861 Census — Peter & Catherine Martin, sheds near Orpington

**Peter Martin** (23, travelling chimney sweeper, born **Bexley**, Kent) and **Catherine** (23, also born **Bexley**) living in sheds near Orpington with their 2-year-old son James (born **Bletchingley**, Surrey). Peter is itinerant — not yet settled at Frog Lane. Both Peter and Catherine come from Bexley parish; she is his first wife who died between 1871 and ~1877.
