# London Gazette — Gerald Sebastian White, O.B.E.

**Supplement to the London Gazette, 31 December 1937 (issue 34469), published 1 January 1938.**

The 1938 New Year Honours list, under **Officer of the Order of the British Empire (OBE) — Civil Division — British India**, records:

> Gerald Sebastian White, Esq., Registrar, High Court of Judicature at Fort St. George, Madras.

The appointment recognised Gerald's service as the senior administrative law officer of the Madras High Court. "Fort St. George" is the formal name for the High Court at Madras (now the Madras High Court, Chennai).
