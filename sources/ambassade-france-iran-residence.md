# La résidence de France en Iran (Ambassade brochure)

Official Ambassade de France en Iran commemorative brochure (300 copies printed in Iran, September 2020) covering the history of the French Residence in Tehran from the 1891 land grant to the present. In French.

**Corpus bundle:** [`corpus/ambassade-france-iran-residence/`](corpus/ambassade-france-iran-residence/) — `original.pdf`, `extracted.pdf.md`, `source.yaml`.

## Key passage — the unnamed arsenal engineer (almost certainly Julien Bottin)

> "Ambassadeur de France en Iran depuis 1881, André de Balloy entreprit la construction de cet édifice devant incarner la relation diplomatique entre la France et l'Iran. Néanmoins, les difficultés rencontrées pour se rendre en Perse compliquèrent la venue d'un architecte. **André de Balloy se fit alors seconder sur place par un ingénieur français, en poste à la direction de l'arsenal de Téhéran.**"

**Translation:** Ambassador de Balloy (in post from 1881) undertook the construction of the residence, but difficulty finding an architect led him to be assisted on the ground by **a French engineer stationed at the directorate of the Tehran arsenal**.

Construction began **10 April 1894**; completed and announced at Christmas 1896.

### Why this is almost certainly Julien Bottin

| Evidence | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Role match | Bottin's contract: *Ingénieur mécanicien de l'Arsenal et des manufactures militaires* |
| Timeline match | Residence construction 1894–1896; Bottin documented in Tehran by 1898 (Onnerfors) and on the Alliance Française committee |
| Nationality match | "un ingénieur français" — Bottin is French (Nice, 1853) |
| Network match | Bottin is named alongside Jean-Baptiste Lemaire (Dr. Tholozan's son-in-law, linked to the same French court circle as ambassador de Balloy's network) in Onnerfors |

Bottin is not named by name in this document. The identification is a strong inference, not a confirmed citation. A match against French diplomatic archives (Quai d'Orsay / MAE) could confirm.
