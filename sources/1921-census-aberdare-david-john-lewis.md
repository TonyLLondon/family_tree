# 1921 Census — 41 Glan Road, Aberdare (David John Lewis household)

Scan of the original household schedule from the **1921 Census of England & Wales**, taken **19 June 1921**. Six persons at **41 Glan Road, Gadlys, Aberdare**: [David John Lewis (1857)](../people/david-john-lewis-1857.md) as widowed head and surface labourer; his son [Samuel Lewis](../people/samuel-lewis.md) with wife [Lily](../people/elizabeth-lilian-cushen.md) (née Cushen); daughter Elizabeth; son Thomas John; and grandson [David John Lewis (1918)](../people/david-john-lewis.md), age 2 years 8 months. All three working men are recorded as **out of work** during the **1921 coal lockout**, employed by variants of **Bwllfa Colliery**.

The schedule is signed by **Samuel Lewis**, not the elderly head.
