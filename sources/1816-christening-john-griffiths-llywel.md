# Christening — John Griffiths, Llywel, 4 Feb 1816

**Summary:** John Griffiths, son of David Griffiths and Margaret, christened 4 February 1816 at Llywel, Breconshire. Earliest known child of the couple, born just 6 weeks after their 22 December 1815 marriage.

**Archive:** FamilySearch extracted index, "Wales, Births and Baptisms, 1541-1907" (collection 1783957).
