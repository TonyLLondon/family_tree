# 1744 baptism — Anzolo Alloisio (Luigi) Addobbati, Duomo di Zara

Baptismal entry in the Metropolitan Church (Sv. Stošija) of Zara for **Anzolo Alloisio Addobbati**, born 1 October 1744 to **D. Daniele Addobbati** (*cittadino*, admitted 1733) and **Margherita Lanfranchi** (b. 26 Dec 1715 Zara). Baptized in November 1744 by Don Carlo Felicinouvich, with **Cristoffo Guardi** as godfather. The baptismal names — Anzolo Alloisio (= Angelo Aloysius) — are the formal-Latin original of the civic-Italian "Luigi" used throughout his adult life and the Croatian "Lujo" of later sources.

The same baptism is independently attested by **DAZD HR-DAZD-342 sig. 52** (a 23 Dec 1783 certified copy issued as "Alvise Anzolo Addobbati, son of Daniele and Margherita") and printed by **Sabalich, *Guida archeologica di Zara* (1897) p. 505**.
