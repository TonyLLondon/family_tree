## Significance for dating the Madras HC directory

The printed court directory shows **"Sir H.O.C. Beasley"** as Chief Justice with G. S. White as Registrar. The knighthood was granted in 1930, so the directory dates to **1930–1937** at the earliest, not merely 1929–1937.
