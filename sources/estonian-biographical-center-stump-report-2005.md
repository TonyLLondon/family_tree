# Estonian Biographical Center — Stump family research (2005)

Commissioned **English** research report (**1 September 2005**, researcher **Fred Puss**, Estonian Biographical Center PLC, Tartu) on the **Stump** family in **Tallinn** (Tallinn Dome Lutheran congregation, Tartu University student file, city address registers, court papers). Aim stated in the PDF: migration to Estonia, names of **Étienne Stump**’s parents and siblings, and when he left — within a fixed time budget.

## Summary (substantive findings)

- **Marc François (Markus Franz) Stump:** Tallinn Dome parishioner registers; **Tartu University** exams **13–24 March 1865** (French and minors) toward **gymnasium** teaching; **from 1868** *Oberlehrer* (senior teacher) of **French** at the **Tallinn government gymnasium**; **Reformed** (wife **Lutheran**). **Residences:** e.g. **Tatari** (1900), **Mere 123**; property on Tatari partly let to **Magnus Grube**’s bakery (from **1894**) and basement to pharmacist **Treublut** (**1897–1900**); **Konstantin Mühlberg** lawsuit (**1900**, fonds ref. in report). **Death:** **3 April 1903** in **Yverdon** (register note; **Switzerland** gloss in parish book) — **conflicts** with some GEDCOM exports that give **Tallinn** and **16 April**. **Birth** in register given as **16 February 1834 Yverdon**, with a possible **10 March 1834** baptism note — **conflicts** with **Erlen / 10 March 1834** from Swiss christening extracts in this vault; calendar (Gregorian vs Julian) discussed in the report.
- **Olga Caroline Erbe:** **22 June 1868** marriage in Tallinn; **11 November 1844** St Petersburg; **15 October 1894** death Tallinn.
- **Children (all listed in report):** Jean Jacque Herman Eugen (d. **1874**); **Arthur Roger** (Moscow, later Berlin d. **1935**); **Marguerite Emilie** (m. **Cuno von Frey**); **Antoinette** (d. **1881**); **Étienne Marc Theodore** — birth **7 March 1880** Tallinn, baptism **6 April 1880**; **1910 obituary** for aunt **Louise Stump** names **Dr Etienne Stump and wife in Teheran**.
- **Further work:** report notes **eight Tallinn real-estate files** for the family; passport application volumes are dense and not fully searched for Étienne’s exit.
