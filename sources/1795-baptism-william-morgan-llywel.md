# Baptism — William Morgan, Llywel, 15 September 1795

**Llywel parish register**, Breconshire. William Morgan, baptized 15 Sep 1795. Father: **Morgan Morgans**. Mother: **Lettuce** (Lettice). FamilySearch batch C08147-1, FMP `R_732790335`.

Identifies William Morgan's parents. Lettuce does not appear on later censuses — William's "Mother" in 1841/1851 is **Anne Morgan** (widow), likely a stepmother following Lettuce's death.
