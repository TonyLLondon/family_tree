# 1881 Census — Martin household, Alpine Cottage, Frog Lane, Orpington

**Peter Martin** (41, master chimney sweep, born Sevenoaks) at Alpine Cottage, Frog Lane, Orpington with **Clara Webb** (27, unmarried, housekeeper, born Chelsfield) and eight children. Four older children surnamed **Martin** from Peter's first marriage; three younger children surnamed **Webb** — Clara's maiden name — because she and Peter are not yet married. By 1891 the Webb children have been re-surnamed Martin and Clara is listed as Peter's wife. **Rosina** (b. 1891) is not yet born.
