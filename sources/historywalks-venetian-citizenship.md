# History Walks Venice — Citizen of the Republic of Venice

## Summary

Accessible plain-language overview of the Venetian citizenship hierarchy: patrizi (nobles, *Libro d'Oro*), *cittadini originari* (*Libro d'Argento*), and the broader citizen class. Explains the three main categories of *cittadinanza*, the role of the *Avogadori di Comun* in verifying claims, and the practical meaning of citizenship status in the republic's social order.

## Use in this research

Background reading for understanding the *cittadini originari* tier occupied by the Addobbati. Pairs with the Ca' Foscari academic chapter ([venetian-cittadini-originari-ch3.md](venetian-cittadini-originari-ch3.md)) for a more detailed institutional account.
