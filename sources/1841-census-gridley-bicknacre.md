# 1841 Census — Ann Gridley, servant at Bicknacre, Danbury

**Ann Gridley** (15, born Essex) found as a **female servant** on John Chapman's farm at the Hamlet of Bicknacre, Danbury. Her only pre-marriage census appearance. She later married George Coolbear (~1844) and named their first son **George Gridley Coolbear** after her maiden name.
