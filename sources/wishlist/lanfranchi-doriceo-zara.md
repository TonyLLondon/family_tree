# Wishlist — Lanfranchi / Doriceo (Zara): Santa 1707 birth contradiction

The 1707 marriage record (`Marriage Lanfranchi Doriceo Zara 1707.jpg`) names the bride as **Francesca Doriceo** (d/o late Giovanni Doriceo), married 4 June 1707 to **Francesco Lanfranchi** (Diocese of Bergamo). Six months later, the Santa Lanfranchi birth entry (8 Dec 1707) names the mother as **"Maria Scapallina"** — a different woman. Francesco is the only securely attested early Lanfranchi male across three independent records. The "Maria Scapallina" reading is MEDIUM confidence in difficult cursive. See `research/image-based-corrections.md` §28 and §28b for full analysis.

**Already in repo:**

- Birth register image: [media/collections/zerauschek/Zerauschek (1)/Birth Santa Lanfranchi Zara 1707.jpg](../../media/collections/zerauschek/Zerauschek%20(1)/Birth%20Santa%20Lanfranchi%20Zara%201707.jpg) — transcribed (Tony, Mar 2026; MEDIUM confidence).
- DAZD sig. 51 (1776): Pietro Lanfranchi, merchant of Calle Larga — proves surname persisted but does not evidence a second male Lanfranchi in 1707.
- Six sibling baptism images (1708–1717): seam damage and archival stickers obscure parent-name lines.

**Options:**

## Priority 2 — Sibling baptisms (1708–1717): mother's name

**What:** Any of the six Lanfranchi sibling baptism images that yields a secure mother's name would resolve whether the mother was consistently "Margaritta/Francesca Doriceo" or genuinely "Maria Scapallina" for the earliest child.

| Image | Known obstacle |

The 1712, 1713, and 1717 entries may have better preservation than the 1708 and 1710 entries and should be examined next.

---

## Priority 3 — DAZD sig. 51: Pietro Lanfranchi letter (1776)

**What:** Physical examination of the 1776 letter from Bergamo mentioning Pietro Lanfranchi (merchant, Calle Larga). The archivist connects him to Margherita Lanfranchi but the exact relationship is unknown. The letter itself may name it.

**Access:** DAZD, Zadar — HR-DAZD-342, Series 4, sig. 51 (1 leaf).

---

## Priority 4 — Francesca vs. Margaritta Doriceo identity

**What:** Determine whether **Francesca** Doriceo (1707 marriage) and **Margaritta** Doriceo (1715 baptism) are the same person (double name) or different women (sisters, both daughters of Giovanni Doriceo).

**Sources to search:**
- Zara baptism registers for Doriceo children (pre-1707) — would show if both "Francesca" and "Margaritta" were baptized as daughters of Giovanni Doriceo.
- Zara death registers (c. 1707–1715) — a death of Francesca Doriceo would prove sequential marriages.
- The 1712/1713/1717 sibling baptisms (Priority 2) — if they name the mother, the name used would clarify the question.
