# David John Lewis — military sources wishlist

Person: [David John Lewis](../../people/david-john-lewis.md) · Narrative: [Sicily to the Po, and the U.S. Silver Star](../../stories/david-john-lewis-italy-1945-silver-star.md)

**Known:** Army number **143587**. Commission 23 Aug 1940. MiD 23 May 1946. Silver Star gazetted 18 Jul 1947. Relinquished commission 31 Oct 1953 (hon. rank of Major). All four London Gazette pages captured as screenshots in `media/military/`.

---

## Priority 1 — buy / subscribe

| Source | What it gives | Cost / access | Status |
|--------|---------------|---------------|--------|
| ***The History of 61 Infantry Brigade May 1944–June 1945*** (Naval & Military Press, ISBN 9781783310876) | Published brigade history covering the period 1st Welch was with 61st Brigade, 6th Armoured Division. Should contain the Argenta Gap, Po crossings, and the Faccenda action (25 Apr 1945) by name. | ~£15–25 from [Naval & Military Press](https://naval-military-press.com/product/the-history-of-61-infantry-brigade-may-1944-june-1945) or secondhand | Not yet acquired |
| **Wartime Memories Project — library subscription** | 6,841 digitised items for the 1st Battalion Welch Regiment, including war diary transcriptions and **two officer nominal rolls** (13 Feb 1943, 26 Sep 1943) that would list Lewis by name and company. | Subscription (modest annual fee) at [wartimememoriesproject.com](https://www.wartimememoriesproject.com/ww2/allied/battalion.php?pid=1400) | Not yet subscribed |

## Priority 2 — TNA / NARA (archive visit or paid download)

| Source | Reference | What it gives |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| **1st Welch war diary** | WO 169 (MEF) / WO 172 (CMF) | Day-by-day battalion record. Would confirm Lewis at specific engagements (Sicily, Garigliano, Anzio, Gemmano, Argenta Gap, Faccenda) and give the 25 April 1945 action in the CO's own words. Not digitised. |
| **Silver Star recommendation** | WO 373 — Foreign Awards to British: From USA, 28 Jul 1942 – 20 Mar 1947 (TNA catalogue [D8682667](https://discovery.nationalarchives.gov.uk/details/r/D8682667)) | The recommendation chain for the Silver Star — who wrote the citation, who endorsed it, any accompanying narrative. |
| **Full GO No. 285** | NARA, HQ U.S. Army Forces Mediterranean Theater, General Orders No. 285, 10 Nov 1945 | The complete general orders volume listing all awards in that batch. Would show whether other 1st Welch or 61st Brigade officers received U.S. decorations for the same action. |

## Priority 3 — promotion gazette notices

Lewis's promotions to Captain and to temporary Major have not been found in the Gazette. Searching supplements from 1942–1944 for "(143587)" or "D. J. Lewis" under Welch R. would locate these. The number block (143584–143593) places him with nine other officers commissioned on the same date; their promotion notices might cluster together.
