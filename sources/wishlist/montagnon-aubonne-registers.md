# Montagnon — Aubonne Reformed church register images (FamilySearch Center visit)

**Goal:** Confirm Jean François Montagnon (b. ~1667, d. 1732) as father of Jean Pierre Montagnon (b. 1692, d. 1737) by viewing the original Aubonne baptismal register for the 1690s.

## Why this is needed

The Jean François → Jean Pierre link is the one unconfirmed step in the Montagnon chain. Strong circumstantial evidence supports it (sole Montagnon patriarch at Aubonne, consistent birth spacing, naming patterns), but no indexed record explicitly names Jean François as Jean Pierre's father. The reason: **Aubonne baptismal registers for the 1690s appear to be unindexed** by FamilySearch — baptisms from the 1660s–1670s are indexed, and records from the 1720s onward are well covered, but the 1690s is a gap.

## What to look for

The images are in FamilySearch collection **4138674** ("Switzerland, Catholic and Reformed Church Records, 1418-1996"). The relevant image ARKs bracket the gap:

| Reference point | Image ARK | Date |
|-----------------|-----------|------|
| Jean François burial | `3:1:3Q9M-CS1X-P9YT-Y` | 15 Sep 1732 |
| Jean Pierre burial | `3:1:3Q9M-CS1X-PSMQ-S` | 5 Mar 1737 |
| Jeane Marechal burial | `3:1:3Q9M-CS1X-PSMS-F` | 11 Jul 1742 |
| François bap (father Jean) | `3:1:3Q9M-C39V-MS39` | 22 Dec 1667 |
| Elisabet bap (father Jean) | `3:1:3Q9M-C39V-MS3P` | 7 Nov 1670 |

The baptism images (`C39V-...` series) are from a different register volume than the burials (`CS1X-...`). Browse the baptism register volume forward from the 1670 entry to find entries from the 1690s. Look for:

1. **Baptism of Jean Pierre Montagnon** (~1692) — should name his father
2. **Baptism of Jean François Montagnon** (~1694) — the second Jean François, who died 1778
3. **Baptism of Jean Michel Montagnon** (~1699) — died 1772
4. Any **family register page** listing Jean François (b. ~1667) with wife Jeane Marechal and children

## Access

Images restricted to **FamilySearch Centers and affiliated libraries**. Nearest locations: search [familysearch.org/en/centers](https://www.familysearch.org/en/centers).
