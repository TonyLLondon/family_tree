## URLs to hit (free, open now)

### 1. Savushun — Internet Archive (borrow)

- **URL:** <https://archive.org/details/savushunnovelabo00dani>
- **What:** Simin Daneshvar, *Savushun: A Novel About Modern Iran*, trans. M. R. Ghanoonparvar (Mage Publishers, 1990)
- **Action:** Borrow (1-hour loan), search for "Stamp" / "Stump" / "Eshtamp" / "dentist". The Persian original uses the phrase «یک دست دندان حسابی پیش دکتر اشتمپ گذاشتن» ("getting a proper set of teeth from Dr. Eshtamp").
- **Why it matters:** Shows Stump's name entered popular Iranian culture; confirms public fame.

### 2. Ebtehaj memoirs — Google Books

- **URL:** <https://books.google.com/books?id=kHPIAwAAQBAJ>
- **What:** ابوالحسن ابتهاج, *خاطرات ابوالحسن ابتهاج* (London, 1991), 2 vols., 900 pp.
- **Action:** Use Google Books search to look for اشتومپ / استمپ in the preview. The passage is about Ebtehaj as a student c. 1916 living with two American women whom Stump visited.
- **Also on:** [Open Library](https://openlibrary.org/works/OL38662880W)

### 3. Elgood — Google Books snippet view

- **URL:** <https://books.google.com/books?id=XFB8-xIyUpAC&q=Stump+dentist>
- **What:** Cyril Elgood, *A Medical History of Persia and the Eastern Caliphate* (CUP, 1951). Hit on **page 536** (Chapter XVIII: "The Sanitary Council and the Quarantine Service", pp 514–537).
- **Action:** Snippet view only — can confirm Stump is named but cannot extract full passage.
- **Also on:** [HathiTrust](https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=inu.30000053313932) (search-only, no page view)

### 4. Mustawfi memoir — Persian PDF sites

- **URLs:**
  - <https://persianpdf.com/book/دانلود-کتاب-شرح-زندگانی-من-اثر-عبدالله/> (10,000 toman)
  - <https://taaghche.com/book/205631/شرح-زندگانی-من-جلد-اول> (vol. 1)
  - <https://fidibo.com/book/174886-شرح-حال-زندگانی-من-جلد-1>
- **What:** عبدالله مستوفی, *شرح زندگانی من* (3 vols., 1927 pp.)
- **Action:** Download vol. 1 (the Mahmoudieh/Stump passages are in vol. 1 per the legacy email). Search for استمپ / دندانساز.
- **Already in repo:** [mustawfi-mahmoudieh-stump.md](../mustawfi-mahmoudieh-stump.md) — legacy email summary of vol. 1 passages.

### 5. Kamareh'i diary — WikiFeqh catalog entry

- **URL:** <https://fa.wikifeqh.ir/روزنامه%20خاطرات%20سید%20محمد%20کمره‌ای‌%20(کتاب)>
- **What:** Catalog/summary of the 2-volume diary (Shirazeh, 2003, 1784 pp.)
- **Action:** Read for content summary; diary not digitised.

### 6. Dr. Esmail Yazdi bio pages

- **URLs:**
  - <https://www.mohammadmossadegh.com/biography/ismail-yazdi/>
  - <https://fa.wikipedia.org/wiki/اسماعیل_یزدی>
- **What:** Background on the author of *Siri dar Tarikh-e Dandanpezeshki-ye Iran*, the book that reproduces Stump's own 1922 article.

### 7. International College of Dentists — early history

- **URL:** <https://icd.org/about-international-college-of-dentists/history/>
- **What:** ICD founded 31 Dec 1927 in Chicago; by 1928 had 250 Fellows. Stump elected 1929 (1308 SH).
- **Action:** Contact ICD archives to request confirmation of Stump's fellowship.
