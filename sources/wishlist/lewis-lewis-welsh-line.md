## Rachel Stephen baptism — RESOLVED

**Baptism found:** Rachel, daughter of William and Mary Stephen, baptised **9 October 1825**, **Llanarthne**, Carmarthenshire (p. 81, No. 648). Father: farmer. FindMyPast record GBPRS/B/871055835/1.

Note: she was born in 1825, not ~1830 as the 1871 census implied — she understated her age by about five years. The register spelling is **Stephen** (no final 's'); the GRO marriage index uses **Stephens**.

## Rachel after 1872

Lewis Lewis died December 1872, buried Aberavon. Rachel was actually **47** (not ~42 — her true birth year was 1825, not ~1830), left with nine children aged ~3–15. Not found on the 1881 census under "Rachel Lewis" near Aberavon/Swansea — may have returned to Carmarthenshire, remarried, or be under a variant spelling. Try broader search with name variants; also search for "Rachel Stephen" in case she reverted to maiden name.

## William Lewis & Catherine Rees (great-grandparents)

David Lewis's parents, named on his 1794 Briton Ferry baptism. The William Lewis × Sarah Bowen marriage at Baglan (22 May 1785) is a **different couple** — wrong bride. The William Lewis × Catherine Rees marriage has not been found at Briton Ferry, Baglan, Neath, or Margam. Try Cadoxton-juxta-Neath, Llantwit-juxta-Neath, or nonconformist registers.
