## Biographical / contextual (Alamir, Aqasi, district)

### 5. Encyclopaedia Iranica — **Eḥtešām-al-Salṭana** (Mahmoud Alamir)

Free online article — not a purchase, but **print if** you want a stable shelf copy: use Iranica citation + PDF print, or cite URL.

- <https://iranicaonline.org/articles/ehtesam-al-saltana-1>

### 6. Haj Mirza Aqasi / ʿAbbāsiyeh context

Any standard **Qajar political biography** or **Tehran urban history** that treats **Muhammad Shah**’s minister and **north Tehran** garden estates may sharpen the **ʿAbbāsiyeh** → **Muhammadieh** thread (Persian WP). Add titles here when one is chosen; no single vault-mandated volume yet.

### 7. Literary memoir of the district (optional)

**Goli Taraghi** grew up in **Mahmoudieh**; her fiction/memoir is cited on Wikipedia as reflecting the neighborhood — optional cultural context, not evidence for Stump.

### 8. Georges Obolensky, *Forever Russian: Memoirs of a Vagabond Prince* (AuthorHouse, 2013)

Memoir by Vera Obolensky's son. Primary source for the **Mahmoudieh guesthouse** (40+ rooms, European business travelers), Vera's marriage to Stump, and the family's flight from Russia. 176 pp. **E-book $3.99** (ISBN 978-1-4817-1476-1). Purchase: [AuthorHouse](https://www.authorhouse.com/en/bookstore/bookdetails/435293-Forever-Russian) · [Google Books](https://books.google.com/books?id=R5W3uYvAhasC) (partial preview) · [Amazon](https://www.amazon.in/Forever-Russian-Memoirs-Vagabond-Prince/dp/1481714775).
