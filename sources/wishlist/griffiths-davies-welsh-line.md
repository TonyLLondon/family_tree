## Margaret Davies death / burial

Margaret was alive and age 75 on the 1861 census (widow, Cwmdare, Aberdare). Not found on 1871 census.

**Candidates (unconfirmed):**
- **Margaret Griffiths**, d. 1874, Builth registration district (covers Llywel), birth 1789 — GRO death index, FS `2JJF-JVK`. Two years off from ~1787; she'd be ~87.
- **Margaret Griffiths**, buried 30 Aug 1859, Battle parish, Breconshire, age 71 (b. ~1788) — FMP `GBPRS/D/886157614/1`. Battle is ~10 miles from Llywel; this would mean she died before the 1861 census, which contradicts the Aberdare census record.

The 1874 Builth death is the stronger candidate (Margaret was alive in 1861; Builth district covers Llywel, where she may have returned). Ordering the GRO certificate (vol/page from the index) would give age at death and informant details.

- **Search:** GRO death index 1861–1880, Builth or Merthyr Tydfil district. Also Llywel/Aberdare parish burial registers.

## Son John Griffiths (1816)

Christened 4 Feb 1816 at Llywel (FS `FM9R-44Q` / `KCV1-DV5`). No further trace — not on 1841 census with the family. May have died young or left Llywell.

- **Search:** Llywel burial register 1816–1840 for a John Griffiths child burial. Or: FMP/FS marriage or census search for John Griffiths b. ~1816 Llywel.
- **Candidate:** John Griffiths, b. 1816, Carmarthenshire, on 1871 census at Ystradgynlais, Glamorgan — FS `V5BG-GX2`. Birthplace "Carmarthenshire" doesn't match Llywel (Brecknockshire), but census birthplace attributions are often imprecise for border parishes. Ystradgynlais is the next parish west of Llywel.
