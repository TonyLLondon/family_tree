# FIBIwiki — Jubbulpore

**URL:** https://wiki.fibis.org/w/Jubbulpore  
**Corpus:** [sources/corpus/fibis-jubbulpore/](corpus/fibis-jubbulpore/)

Families in British India Society (FIBIS) wiki article on Jubbulpore (Jabalpur), the cantonment and city where **Gerald Sebastian White** was born on 12 February 1878.

## Key facts for this vault

- Jubbulpore cantonment established after British occupation 1818
- Brigade headquarters, 5th Division, Southern Army
- Great Indian Peninsula Railway junction opened **1871** — made it one of the most important railway centers in India
- Contemporary sources note the site "unhealthy for Europeans" despite mild climate — hence the need for qualified AMD medical officers
- Snooker reportedly invented at Jubbulpore in 1875 by Colonel Sir Neville Chamberlain (Devonshire Regiment) — confirms active officer presence in Gerald's birth year period
