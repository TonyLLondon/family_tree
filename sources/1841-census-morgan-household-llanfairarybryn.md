# 1841 Census — Morgan household, Coedevan, Llanfairarybryn

**HO107/1386**, folio 8, page 11, schedule 1360 — Coedevan, Rhandir Ganol, Llanfairarybryn, Llandovery, Carmarthenshire.

**William Morgan** (head, 40, b. Carmarthenshire) with wife **Sarah** (35, b. Carmarthenshire — actually Builth, Brecknockshire per 1881 census), elderly **Ann Morgan** (70, probably William's mother), sons **Jinken** (20), **David** (9), **William Jr** (7), and two non-family workers.

Anne Morgan (b. ~1829, their daughter) is absent — likely in service at age ~12.
