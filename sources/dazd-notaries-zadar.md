## Summary

The DAZD Notaries of Zara inventory covers the surviving protocol registers of public notaries who practised in Zara from the medieval period through the 19th century. Each notary has a sub-series entry giving name, dates of practice, and a description of surviving registers.

**Addobbati entry:** **Angelo Daniele Addobbati** — notarial acts, Zara, **1806–1831** (25 years of continuous practice). His register is among the best-dated entries in the series for the early 19th century.

## Use in this research

Establishes Angelo Daniele Addobbati as an active public notary for 25 years spanning the French Illyrian period and the early Habsburg era. Notarial service was one of the most trusted and demanding civic roles in Dalmatian city life — certifying contracts, wills, property transfers, and legal instruments. His register cross-references with the Petricioli and Filippi family fonds, which carry Addobbati-certified documents.
