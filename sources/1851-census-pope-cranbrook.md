# 1851 Census — Pope household, Cranbrook

**George Pope** (26, shoemaker) and **Sarah Ann [Quaife] Pope** (24, dressmaker) with two young daughters at Cranbrook, Kent (Hamlet of Court Lodge). The earliest census for this couple — George is a journeyman shoemaker, not yet a master. A daughter Sarah A (~1849) appears here but not in later censuses, indicating she died young.
