# 1841 Census — Pope household, Cranbrook

Three generations of the Pope family at Cranbrook, Kent. **William Pope** (~40, Kent) and **Sarah Pope** (~35, Kent) with sons George (15) and William (10). An elderly **William Pope** (75) — likely the grandfather — also in the household. This is the earliest census for the Pope line, identifying **George Pope's parents** (I296).
