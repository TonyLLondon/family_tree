# Fulvia Zerauschek — family history memoir (1996)

Seven-page memoir addressed to [Peter Martin Lewis](../people/peter-martin-lewis.md) (PML), dated **18 & 24 September 1996**. Author: Fulvia Zerauschek ("Nonna"), daughter of [Antonio Zerauschek](../people/antonio-zerauschek.md) and [Ester Addobbati](../people/ester-addobbati.md).

Covers **Addobbati**, **Zerauschek**, and **Boara** families of Zara. Key content: Order of the Golden Spur, Napoleon delegation, [Simeone Gilberto Addobbati](../people/simeone-gilberto-addobbati.md)'s death at a ball, [Pietro Pio Addobbati](../people/pietro-pio-addobbati.md)'s seminary expulsion, Addobbati children's occupations, Boara inheritance dispute, [Anna Herenda](../people/anna-herenda.md) biographical detail, Antonio Zerauschek's siblings and business agencies, communist trial, 1943 armistice, post-war Florence exile.
