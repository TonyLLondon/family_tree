# 1911 Census — Annie Fensom, boarder at 17 North Road, Holloway

**Annie Fensom** (18, single, brush maker) boarding on the second floor of 17 North Road, Holloway with **Rose Elizabeth M Watts** (18, brush maker) and her father **John Jas Watts** (64, widower, registration clerk). Annie and Rose worked together at the **Star Brush Factory** on Eden Grove — the same street where the Fensom family had lived for decades. Rose would witness Annie's marriage to Alfred Evans twenty months later.
