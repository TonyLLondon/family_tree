# 1871 Census — Martin household, Frog Lane, Orpington

**Peter Martin** (33, chimney sweep, born Woolwich) at Frog Lane, Orpington with his **first wife Catherine** (31, born **Bexleyheath**, Kent), six children, and a journeyman sweep employee William Deanson. Catherine died between 1871 and ~1877; by 1881 Peter's housekeeper **Clara Webb** had replaced her. Infant William Martin (b. 1871) also died young.
