# 1861 Census — Pope household, Cranbrook

**George Pope** (36, master shoemaker) and **Sarah Ann [Quaife] Pope** (32, dressmaker) with three children at Cranbrook, Kent. All born in Cranbrook. This is the earliest census located for Minnie Flora Pope's parents.
