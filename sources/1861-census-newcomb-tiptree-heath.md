# 1861 Census — Alma Newcomb, granddaughter at Tiptree Heath

**Alma Newcomb** (5, scholar, born Tolleshunt Knights) found as a **granddaughter** in the household of John Cousins (57, ag. lab.) and Elizabeth Cousins (82) at **Tiptree Heath**, Great Wigborough. Alma is not with her Newcomb parents — she is being raised by the Cousins grandparents. The location at Tiptree Heath explains why the 1901 census gave her birthplace as "Tiptree."
