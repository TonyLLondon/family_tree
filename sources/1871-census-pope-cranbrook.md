# 1871 Census — Pope household, Mill Street, Cranbrook

**George Pope** (46, shoemaker master) and **Sarah Ann [Quaife] Pope** (43, dressmaker) with five children at Mill Street, Cranbrook, Kent. All family members born in Cranbrook. This household identifies the parents of **Minnie Flora Pope** (I293), who married David William Fensom in 1886 at Islington.
