# Burial — J Louis Montagnon, 4 February 1786

FamilySearch indexed record from the Reformed church registers at Aubonne, Vaud. This is the burial record of Jean François Louis Montagnon, who married Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | J Louis Montagnon |
| **Born** | 1737 |
| **Died/Buried** | 4 February 1786 |
| **Place** | Aubonne, Vaud, Switzerland |

The record gives no parents, but the birth year (1737) fits Jean François Louis Montagnon, whose father Jean Pierre Montagnon was buried 5 March 1737 at Aubonne — meaning J.F.L. was born the same year his father died. His marriage register (28 Oct 1761 at Céligny, 6NQL-FNTW) identifies his parents as Jean Pierre Montagnon + Jeanne Cusin and his wife's parents as Benjamin Chenevard + Christine Nerfin.

An earlier research pass mistakenly attributed a different burial (6NQ2-TV9P, Jean Louis Montagnon d. 1817) to our ancestor. That 1817 burial is for a different Jean Louis Montagnon (b. 1743, son of Jean Michel Montagnon + Catherine Vougaz) who married Jeanne Françoise Vougaz in 1780. This 1786 burial record corrects the death date for our ancestor.
