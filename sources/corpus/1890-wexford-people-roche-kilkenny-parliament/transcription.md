# Wexford People, 26 April 1890 — "Ramont Castle" / Roche at the 1641 Parliament

From a 3,012-word article on the Roche family history, published also in the *New Ross Standard* and *Enniscorthy Guardian* on the same date:

> ...with the Peers; and **David Roche, of Gleecure**, **John Roche of Castletown**, and **Redmond Roche of Cahirdowgan** in the Commons.

This confirms Burke's 1833: **John Roche of Castletown** (Castletown-Roche, Co. Cork) sat in the **Catholic Parliament** or Confederate Council at Kilkenny during the 1641 rising. He is the earliest confirmed direct-line ancestor — father of [Robert Roche](../../people/robert-roche.md) and great-great-grandfather of [John Lysaght Roche](../../people/john-lysaght-roche.md).

The article continues:

> When Ireton took Limerick in 1651, Alderman Jordan Roche, Edmund Roche, Esq., and David [Roche]...

— showing the Roche family's involvement in the Cromwellian siege of Limerick.
