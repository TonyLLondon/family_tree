# 1987 — Bury Free Press — Bury St Edmunds wins Fulvia Lewis Trophy

**Newspaper:** Bury Free Press, 16 October 1987, page 1
**Reference:** BL/0000762/19871016/001

## Extract (relevant passage)

> BEAUTIFUL Bury St Edmunds has won the coveted Keep Britain Tidy Award in a prestigious national competition. The Fulvia Lewis Trophy was presented to St Edmundsbury Mayor, Clir John Long at the Britain in Bloom awards in London on Wednesday.

## Notes

Front-page illustrated article. Bury St Edmunds won the Fulvia Lewis Keep Britain Tidy Trophy in its first year of entering the Britain in Bloom competition. The trophy was awarded for the greatest effort in litter control among all Britain in Bloom finalists.
