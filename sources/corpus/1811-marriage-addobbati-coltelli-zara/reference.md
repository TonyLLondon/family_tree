# Marriage register — Vincenzo Addobbati & Vincenza Coltelli, 25 February 1811, Zadar

Marriage entry **nº 13** in the Metropolitan parish of **Sv. Stošija (St. Anastasia)**, Zara, on **25 February 1811** at **6 p.m.**, celebrated **in the bride's house** under archiepiscopal dispensation. Officiant: **D. Antonio Can.co Grazovich**. The 1811 register sits across the political hinge of Napoleonic Dalmatia under the **Illyrian Provinces**; Zara remained Catholic and the parish books continued unbroken.
