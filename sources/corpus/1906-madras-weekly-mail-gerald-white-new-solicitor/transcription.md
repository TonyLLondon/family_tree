## Notes

This notice records Gerald Sebastian White's admission as a Solicitor of the High Court of Madras, c. March 1906. It confirms:

- He was previously a **practising Solicitor in the Supreme Court of Ireland**.
- He was **Manager of the firm of Messrs. Haggard and Co., Solicitors** — an established Dublin/London legal firm.

This is the earliest dated evidence of Gerald's arrival in Madras and his transition from the Irish to the Indian bar, preceding his later appointment as Registrar of the High Court (c. 1929–1937).
