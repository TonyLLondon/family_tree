# 1974 — St. Andrews Citizen — Britain in Bloom, Fulvia Lewis trophy

**Newspaper:** St. Andrews Citizen, 31 August 1974, page 11
**Article:** "SCOTCO DISCOUN" [headline garbled by OCR]
**Reference:** BL/0000775/19740831/202

## Extract (relevant passage)

> …shopkeepers and other commercial interests have made special effort. A trophy donated by Mrs Fulvia Lewis, wife of 'Keep Britain Tidy' Director General David Lewis, will be presented to the 'Britain in Bloom' finalist most active in litter prevention.

## Notes

One of the earliest newspaper references to the Fulvia Lewis trophy, confirming it was donated by Mrs Fulvia Lewis and naming her husband David Lewis as Director General of Keep Britain Tidy. The trophy was part of the Britain in Bloom competition, awarded for litter prevention efforts.
