# Baptism register — Anzolo Alloisio (Luigi) Addobbati, 1 October 1744, Zadar

Baptism entry in the **Duomo di Zara** (Sv. Stošija / St. Anastasia, the Metropolitan Church), 1744 baptism register, folio 83 (right-hand page). Born 1 October 1744; baptized at the font in November 1744 by **Don Carlo Felicinouvich, Curate**. The baptismal names were **Anzolo Alloisio** (Angelo Aloysius); "Luigi" is the later civic-Italian rendering used in the citizen books and in his own marriage register entry of 1766. Croatian sources render him as **Lujo**.

Names recorded in the Venetian-Italian orthography of the 1740s Zara registers; the patronymic chain is the same one carried into Luigi's 1762 citizen oath, his 1766 marriage to Maddalena Plarinò, and his sons' 1804 Nin nobility inscription.

## Genealogical position

Luigi sits at **slot 608 (Generation 9, 7×great-grandparent)** on the Lewis ahnentafel (`ancestor-tracker.md`), via the line Tony Lewis → Samuel Lewis → Fulvia Zerauschek → Ester Addobbati → Pietro Pio Addobbati → Pietro Paolo Addobbati → Vincenzo Addobbati → **Luigi Addobbati**. He is the foundational figure of the civic-Italian Addobbati line in Zara: born to the *cittadino* Daniele within a decade of the family's 1733 admission, sworn citizen 1762, husband 1766, *Priore della Casa di Pietà* 1783, head of the *Università dei cittadini* 1786 and again 1797, deputy in the July 1797 sea mission to the incoming Austrian forces.
