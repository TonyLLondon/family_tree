## Notes

Names **"Mr David J Lewis"** as **"the director general of the Keep Britain Tidy Group"** visiting Dundee in April 1985 with the Scottish director Douglas S. Wright. This is one of the latest dated newspaper references to David in the KBT role, consistent with his tenure ending c. 1987 when the Group was renamed the Tidy Britain Group.
