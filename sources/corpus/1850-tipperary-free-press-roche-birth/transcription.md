# Birth — William Roche, Esq., Solicitor

*Tipperary Free Press*, c. August 1850

---

**BIRTHS.**

At Upper Pembroke-street, Dublin, the lady of **William Roche**, Esq., Solicitor, of a **son**.

---

**Notes:**

- Places the Roche family at **Upper Pembroke Street, Dublin** in 1850 — they later moved to **72 Harcourt Street**.
- This son born 1850 could be **Dr Antony Roche** (later Professor of Pathology at Catholic University School of Medicine).
- William described as "Solicitor" — not yet Crown Solicitor (that appointment came in 1861).
