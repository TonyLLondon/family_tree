## Notes

### Celsus White (I412) — dentist on Denny Street

**Celsus White** (I412), age 30, born 1881 in **Tipperary** — confirmed by both census and GEDCOM. His qualification **LDSI** (Licentiate in Dental Surgery of Ireland) is recorded in the occupation column. He is the second son of [William O'Byrne White](../../../people/william-obyrne-white.md) and [Catherine Mary Roche](../../../people/catherine-mary-roche.md) to pursue a professional career: his elder brother **William Xavier** (I410) became a solicitor in Maryborough, and **Gerald** (I12) became a solicitor-registrar in Madras. The father was a surgeon (LRCPI LRCSI) — three professional sons from one retired Army medical officer.

Celsus has set up practice at **27 Denny Street** — the same street as his parents' household (Form B 22, likely a neighbouring house). He had his own surgery and his own household with two servants, indicating a successful early practice.

### Catherine White (wife) — recently married, local

Catherine, age 21 (born ~1890), is from **Kerry** — a local marriage for Celsus. They married approximately 1910 (1 year married). She has had 1 child born alive, 1 still living — this is **Declan**.

Her maiden name is not recorded on the census form. Marriage records (civil registration or Tralee parish registers ~1910) would supply it.

### Declan White — infant son

**Declan**, age not recorded (probably newborn or under 1 month at census date, 2 April 1911). Born in Kerry. His education column reads "Cannot read" — the standard entry for an infant. The blank age field and the marriage duration of 1 year suggest he was born in early 1911, very shortly before the census.

### Two servants — prosperous household

The household employs **two** female servants:

- **Ellen Walsh** (21, Kerry) — General Domestic Servant, local hire.
- **May Clifton** (23, Dublin) — described as **Nurse (Domestic)**. A nursemaid brought from Dublin to care for the newborn Declan. Hiring a dedicated children's nurse from outside Kerry indicates that Celsus could afford specialist domestic staff — consistent with a professional dentist's income.

### Relationship to parents' household

Celsus's parents [William O'Byrne White](../../../people/william-obyrne-white.md) (67) and [Katie White](../../../people/catherine-mary-roche.md) (69) are recorded at **Denny Street** on the same census night under Form B 22 — five houses along the street. Sister [Kathleen](../../../people/william-obyrne-white.md) (29) remains with the parents. The two Denny Street households represent a family compound: the retired surgeon-father and the practising dentist-son side by side.
