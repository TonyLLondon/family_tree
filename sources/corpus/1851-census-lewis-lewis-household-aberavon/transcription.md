Lewis Lewis was a widower at 38 with six children. His first wife Mary (present on the 1841 census) had died between c. 1848 and March 1851. The family had moved from Briton Ferry/Baglan to Aberavon between 1841 and 1844. Henry (14) was already working as a lister in the tin works. The children's birthplaces trace the family's migration: Ann and Henry born in Baglan, Eliza in Briton Ferry, then Mary, Gwen, and Sarah in Aberavon.
