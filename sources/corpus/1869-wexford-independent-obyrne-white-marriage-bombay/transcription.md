# Marriage of William O'Byrne White — Wexford Independent, 13 March 1869

**Source:** Wexford Independent, 13 March 1869. Also Cork Examiner, 19 March 1869. Via British Newspaper Archive / FindMyPast.

**FMP search:** [Wexford Independent](https://www.findmypast.co.uk/search-newspapers/results?names=o%27byrne+white&newspaper=wexford%20independent&startdate=1869-03-13&enddate=1869-03-13&exactdate=true) · [Cork Examiner](https://www.findmypast.co.uk/search-newspapers/results?names=o%27byrne+white&newspaper=cork%20examiner&startdate=1869-03-19&enddate=1869-03-19&exactdate=true)

## Transcription (Wexford Independent)

> MARRIAGES.
>
> February 8, at Bombay, India, by the Right Rev. Doctor Leo Meurin, Vicar Apostolic of Bombay, Wm. O'Byrne White, Esq., Royal Medical Staff, third son of the late Mathew White, Esq., M.D., of Killoughter and Ballinaliitch, co. [Carlow?]

## Transcription (Cork Examiner variant)

> At Bombay, India, Staff Assistant-Surgeon Wm. O'Byrne White, third son of the late M. E. White, Esq., M.D., of Killoughter and Ballinaliitch, co. [Carlow/Wexford?]

## Notes

- Confirms the marriage date as **8 February 1869** at Bombay, officiated by **Bishop Leo Meurin SJ**, Vicar Apostolic of Bombay (a Jesuit prelate who served 1867–86).
- William is described as **"third son of the late Mathew White, Esq., M.D., of Killoughter and Ballinaliitch"** — confirming his father Matthew Esmond White was deceased by this date and had medical qualifications (M.D.), and placing the family seat at **Killoughter** (likely Killoughter, Co. Wicklow) and **Ballinaliitch** (unidentified; possibly Ballinvalley or similar in Carlow/Wexford).
- William's rank: **Staff Assistant-Surgeon** (Cork Examiner) / **Royal Medical Staff** (Wexford Independent).
- The bride (Catherine Mary Roche) is not named in the snippet — the full notice may contain her details.
