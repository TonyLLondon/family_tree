## Notes

- **George G Coolbear** (I105) — now 46, his occupation has shifted from "bill poster & hawker" (1881) to **general dealer**. A general dealer was a shopkeeper or itinerant trader selling a variety of goods. Age 46 gives birth year ~1845, consistent across all censuses.
- **Alma Coolbear** (I106, née **Newcomb**) — her birthplace is now specified as **Tolleshunt Knights, Essex**, a small village ~7 miles east of Maldon on the Blackwater estuary. Age 37 confirms the ~1854 birth year, consistent with the 1881 census (27). This means she was definitively ~14 when Ellen Alice (b. ~1868) was born — far too young to be Ellen Alice's biological mother in most scenarios.
- **Ellen Alice**, **Charles**, and **George Jr** from the 1881 household are all absent — they've left home by 1891 (Ellen Alice would be ~23, Charles ~20, George Jr ~15).
- **Five new children** appear since 1881:
  - **Harold A Coolbear** (~1882, Maldon)
  - **Alice E Coolbear** (~1884, Maldon)
  - **Lily R Coolbear** (~1886, Maldon)
  - **Jubilee Victoria Coolbear** (~1888, Maldon) — named for Queen Victoria's **Golden Jubilee** (20–21 June 1887). She was born ~1888, suggesting the family celebrated the Jubilee and used it as a naming inspiration shortly after.
  - **Ernest W Coolbear** (~1890, Maldon)
- **Archibald Percy Coolbear** (I103) would be born in **March 1892**, ~11 months after this census. George Gridley died in **1892** — the same year his youngest son was born. Alma was left a widow at ~38 with at least seven young children.
- George Gridley died having fathered at least **11 children** across ~24 years (Ellen Alice ~1868 to Archibald 1892).
