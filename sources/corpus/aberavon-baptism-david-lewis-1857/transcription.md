## Notes

This is a FamilySearch index entry only — no register image is available. The original source is the parish register at Aberavon (Aberafon), Glamorgan. The spelling "Aberafon" in the original matches the form used in the GEDCOM export (I173).

**Father confirmed as Lewis Lewis.** This matches the [1871 census](../1871-census-aberavon-lewis-lewis-household-rg10-5424/transcription.md), which places Lewis Lewis (age 58, Porter, born Briton Ferry) at Aberavon with wife Rachel and eldest son David (age 13).

**Mother's maiden name:** The index gives only "Rachael" with no surname. The GEDCOM records her as Rachel James (I428), but this is **wrong** — GRO MarriageFinder confirms the maiden name as **Stephen(s)**, and the Llanarthne baptism register (9 Oct 1825) records her as **Rachel Stephen**, daughter of William and Mary Stephen, farmer. The 1871 census records her as Rachel Lewis (married name).

**Aberavon vs Aberdare:** The baptism place is Aberavon (modern Port Talbot area, on the coast), not Aberdare (inland, Cynon Valley). David consistently reported "Aberdare" as his birthplace on later censuses (1891, 1901, 1911) — an apparent confusion between two similarly-named Glamorganshire towns. The actual birthplace was Aberavon.
