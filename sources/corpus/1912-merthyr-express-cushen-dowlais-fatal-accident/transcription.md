# Fatal Accident at Dowlais Works — Cushen

**Merthyr Express**, 14 September 1912

The article reports a fatal accident at Dowlais Ironworks in which a worker named Cushen was killed. This is likely **David T. Cushen** (b. ~1880), son of [David Cushen](../../people/david-cushen.md), who was recorded as a "bricklayer's labourer" in 1891 and a "labourer" (widower) in the 1911 census at 25 Pond Street, Dowlais.
