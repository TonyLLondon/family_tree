# Birth of Gerald Sebastian White — Homeward Mail, 11 March 1878

**Source:** Homeward Mail from India, China and the East, 11 March 1878. Also Cork Weekly Herald, 16 March 1878. Via British Newspaper Archive / FindMyPast.

**FMP search:** [Homeward Mail](https://www.findmypast.co.uk/search-newspapers/results?names=o%27byrne+white&newspaper=homeward%20mail&startdate=1878-03-11&enddate=1878-03-11&exactdate=true) · [Cork Weekly Herald](https://www.findmypast.co.uk/search-newspapers/results?names=o%27byrne+white&newspaper=cork%20weekly%20herald&startdate=1878-03-16&enddate=1878-03-16&exactdate=true)

## Transcription

> BIRTHS.
>
> WHITE — Feb. 12, at Jubbulpore, wife of Surg.-Major W. O'Byrne White, son.

## Notes

- Confirms **Gerald Sebastian White** was born on **12 February 1878** at **Jubbulpore** (Jabalpur), Central Provinces, India — matching the existing family tree date.
- William's rank by this date: **Surgeon-Major** (promoted 31 March 1877 per Army List).
- The *Homeward Mail from India, China and the East* was the standard London publication for Anglo-Indian vital events. The same notice appeared in the *Cork Weekly Herald* the following week, reaching the Irish community.
- Gerald would later become a solicitor in Madras and serve in the Madras Volunteer Guards — returning to the subcontinent his parents had left.
