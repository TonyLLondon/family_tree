# Family register — Gabriel Nicolas Dumartheray × Jeanne Elisabeth Borel, 4 August 1767

FamilySearch indexed family register entry from the Canton de Vaud church records.

This record identifies the parents of both Jean Gabriel Nicolas Dumartheray and his wife Jeanne Elisabeth Borel (who appears across records as "Jeanne Louise Borel," "Louise née Borel," "Elisabeth Borrel," "Louise Boret" — all variant spellings/given names for the same person).
