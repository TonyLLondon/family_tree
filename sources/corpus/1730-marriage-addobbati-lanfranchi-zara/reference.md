# Marriage register — D. Daniele Addobbati & Margherita Lanfranchi, 22 November 1730, Zadar

Marriage entered in **Latin** in the Metropolitan parish of **Sv. Stošija**, Zara, on **22 November 1730**. **Canon Petrus Bado** officiated under licence from **Archbishop Vincenzo Zmajević**. Banns were dispensed (*Omissis Publicationibus*). The bride was **fourteen years old**.
