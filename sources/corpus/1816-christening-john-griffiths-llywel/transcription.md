## Notes

### First known child

John Griffiths was christened at Llywel on 4 February 1816, just **six weeks** after his parents' marriage on 22 December 1815. He is the earliest known child of David and Margaret.

### Parish church, not Saron Chapel

This christening appears to be from the Llywel **parish church** (Church of England), not from the Saron Independent Chapel. The later children (Thomas, 1828 and Thomas, 1830) were baptised at Saron Independent (TNA RG4/1725). This suggests the family moved between CofE and Congregationalist worship, or that David and Margaret attended the established church earlier in their marriage before becoming committed Independents.

### No further trace

John Griffiths is not found in the 1841 census household at Tresgob, Llywell, where only David, Margaret, and Thomas (11) were living. By 1841 John would have been ~25. He may have died young, married and left home, or migrated.
