# Death register — Casimiro Carobbi, 5 April 1866, Zadar

Death record of **Casimiro Carobbi** in **Zara**, **5 April 1866**. Casimiro had been born in **Senj** (Italian: Segna), the strategic Adriatic port on the Croatian Military Border long settled by Italian merchant and military families, and married **Marianna Billicich** in **Kotor** (5 May 1820), settling thereafter in Zara, where his daughter Vittoria Elisa was born in 1828.
