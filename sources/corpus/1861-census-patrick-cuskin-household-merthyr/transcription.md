Patrick has moved from Trevethin to Merthyr Tydfil. Wife **Ellen is absent — she died between 1851 and 1861**. Michael listed as "Wife" in the transcription — clearly a transcription error; he is the eldest son (23, Ireland).

Note: **Michael Walsh** (45, Ireland) is a boarder. In 1881, Patrick Walsh (33, inn keeper, Ireland) married Patrick Cushen's daughter Ann at the Royal Oak Inn. Michael Walsh may be Patrick Walsh's father — the Walsh–Cushen families knew each other from the Irish community before the marriage.

Catherine (7) appears for the first time — the youngest child, possibly born after the family moved from Trevethin. The surname shifts mid-page from "Cuskin" to "Caslin."
