The earliest record of the Cushen family in Wales. **Patrick** (b. ~1811) and **Thomas** (b. ~1826/1821) are in the same household cluster at Trevethin — brothers who emigrated together from Ireland. Patrick's wife is Helen/Ellen; their son Michael (3) was born in Ireland, meaning they arrived ~1839–40. Mary (1) is the first child born in Wales. Thomas is listed as 15 (1841 census rounded ages 15+ to nearest 5). A cluster of Irish families (Carrolls, Conolys) surround them — a typical Irish migrant lodging group near the Pontypool ironworks.
