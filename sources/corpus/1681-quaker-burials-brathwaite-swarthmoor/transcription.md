# Quaker Burial Register — Brathwaite & Benson Burials, Swarthmoor, 1681–1689

**Source:** TNA RG6/1085 (pp. 49–50) and RG6/1250 (p. 103). Burial entries for the Brathwaite and Benson families across three register pages.

---

## George Brathwaite — buried 4 March 1681

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0060` (p. 49, burials 1681–1684)

George Brathwaite of [Colthouse?]... was buried the fourth day of the first month Anno Domini 1681

---

## Edward Brathwaite — buried 1684

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0060` (same page as above)

Edward son of George Brathwaite of [Hawrey?]... was buried the fourth day of the fourth month Anno Domini 1684

Edward was born 2 Jul 1667 and died aged approximately 17.

---

## George Brathwaite (son of Rowland) — buried 12 March 1687

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0061` (p. 49, burials 1687–1689)

George son of Rowland Brathwaite of [?] was buried the twelfth day of the first month Anno Domini 1687

**Note:** This is a DIFFERENT George Brathwaite — son of Rowland, not our George (son of William) who was buried in 1681.

---

## Rebecka Benson — buried 1688

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0061` (same page)

Rebecka daughter of Barnad Benson... in Longriggs was buried... Anno Domini 1688

Agnes Brathwaite's sister. Confirms the Benson family's location at **Longriggs**.

---

## William Brathwaite — buried 1689

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0061` (same page)

William Brathwaite of [?]... was buried the [twentieth?] day of the second month Anno Domini 1689

This is George's father — William Brathwaite of Hawkshead, who married Sibell Mackreth in 1634.

---

## [?] Benson — buried 1689

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0061` (same page)

[?] daughter of Barnard Benson was buried the eleventh day of the fourth month Anno Domini 1689

Another of Agnes's siblings.

## RG6/1250 register (second copy)

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1250/0/0108` (p. 103, burials 1690–1691)

This page from the Swarthmoor Monthly Meeting's second register copy covers 1690–1691 burials. The William Brathwaite burial (recorded as 25 Oct 1689 in FMP) may appear on the preceding page of this register. Visible entries include Thomas Rawlinson, Robert Pennington (Hawkshead), Rowland Parke (Skelwith), Robert Green (Grasmere), Mary Pennington, Margarett daughter of Edward Bawrey/Awrey, Charles Satterthwaite.
