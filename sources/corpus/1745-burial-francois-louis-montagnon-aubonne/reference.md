## Significance

François Louis died young (no birth year given but clearly an infant or child based on the dates). The record explicitly names **Pierre Montagnon** (= Jean Pierre Montagnon, b. 1692, d. 1737) as his father. Jean Pierre had already died in 1737, so his widow Jeane Elizabeth Cusin would have been the surviving parent.

This record confirms Jean Pierre had a son named François Louis — and his naming pattern (combining "François" and "Louis") may echo family names: "François" from his father Jean François, and "Louis" perhaps a favored name (his other son was Jean François **Louis**).
