## Notes

This local newspaper notice records Archibald Percy Coolbear's enlistment in the Essex Regiment, placing him at **Long Sutton, Lincolnshire** at the time of his recruitment — distant from his Maldon birthplace and later Southend residence. His enlistment date was **29 December 1914** per the RAF service file (AIR 79/1122); this notice appeared one week later.

The "Long Sutton" location is unexpected — his wife Rosina was from Orpington, Kent, and his mother Alma lived in Little Totham, Essex. He may have been working as a chimney sweep in the Long Sutton area when he enlisted.
