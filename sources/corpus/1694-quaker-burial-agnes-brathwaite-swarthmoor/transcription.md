# Quaker Burial Register — Agnes Brathwaite, Lancashire Quarterly Meeting, 1694

**Source:** TNA RG6/1616, p. 42. Image: `TNA/RG6/1616/0/0048`.

Two-page spread showing burials from 1692 (left) and 1694 (right). This is the Lancashire Quarterly Meeting register — a higher-level compilation from the individual monthly meetings.

---

## Agnes Brathwaite — buried 7 July 1694

**Right column, 1694 burials:**

Agnes the wife of George Brathwaite of [Swarthmoor?]... dyed the [?] days... was buried... the [thirtieth?] day of the... month Anno Domini 1694

FMP transcription gives: death 5 Jul 1694, burial 7 Jul 1694, Swarthmoor. Spouse's first name: George.

Also visible on the same page: George Brathwaite — "George Brathwaite late of [?]... dyed the [29th?] days... was buried the thirtieth day of the... month... 1694." This may be a different George or possibly a re-recording.

**Note:** Agnes survived her husband George (buried 4 Mar 1681) by thirteen years. She is identified as "the wife of George Brathwaite," confirming the identification of this Agnes née Benson as the same woman.
