## Significance

David Lewis was the father of [Lewis Lewis](../../people/lewis-lewis.md) (bapt. 14 Mar 1813, Briton Ferry). David married [Ann Davies](../../people/lewis-lewis.md) at Briton Ferry on 23 May 1812.

David's father is recorded as **Will Lewis** (William Lewis), who also had a son **William Lewis** (bapt. 25 May 1783 at Briton Ferry, with mother **Mary**). This means William Lewis married first Mary David (10 Sep 1780, Briton Ferry) and later Catherine Rees — David was from the second marriage.
