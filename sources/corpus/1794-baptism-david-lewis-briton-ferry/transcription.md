# Baptism of David Lewis, Briton Ferry, 7 January 1794

**Register:** Births & Baptisms at Briton Ferry, Anno Domini 1794  
**Reference:** P/70, Pages 28–29  
**Date of Baptism:** 7 January 1794

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Child's Name | David |
| Surname | Lewis |
| Father's Name | Will[iam] Lewis |
| Mother's Name | Catherine Rees |

The same page records burials for the year 1794 and a marriage (Aba[…] Dodson and Margaret Doris, married by banns, 27 April). The register was maintained by Wm. Thomas, Curate, and Wm. Miles, Churchwarden.

David Lewis was the son of William Lewis and Catherine Rees of Briton Ferry. He later married Ann Davies at Briton Ferry on 23 May 1812 and was the father of Lewis Lewis (baptised 14 March 1813).
