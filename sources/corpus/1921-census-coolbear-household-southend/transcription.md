## Notes

- **Archibald Percy Coolbear** (I103) — two years after RAF discharge, he is back to **chimney sweeping** plus two additional trades: **carpet beating** and **chair caning**. He is self-employed ("Own Account"). His age 29 confirms birth year 1892 and birthplace Maldon, Essex — consistent with all other records.
- **Rosina Coolbear** née Martin (I104) — her birthplace is now confirmed as **Orpington, Kent**, and birth year as **1891**. She is occupied with "Domestic Duties" — running a large household of 10 including four young children, a brother-in-law, and three visitors.
- **Archibald Coolbear Jr** — eldest son, born 1912 in **Rochford, Essex**. Born ~11 months after the Oct 1911 marriage. His birthplace of Rochford suggests the couple initially settled in the Rochford district before moving to Southend.
- **May Coolbear** (I100) — confirmed born **1916**, **Southend, Essex**. The GEDCOM had "MAR 1916" — this confirms the year and gives the specific town.
- **Henry Charles Coolbear** — second son, born 1917, Southend. Born while Archibald was serving in France (he was hospitalised with dysentery Jul 1916, evacuated to England Nov 1916, so may have been home on medical leave when Henry Charles was conceived).
- **Irene Coolbear** — baby daughter, born 1921, Southend. No age in years given (infant).
- **Edward Martin** — Rosina's brother, 31, born 1889, Kent. Works as a stoker at Powling Ltd Fat Factory in Eastwood, Essex. His presence confirms the Martin family connection and his Kent origins align with Rosina's Orpington birthplace.
- **Annie Maria Hedley** — visitor, 43, born **Germany** (1878). Nationality noted as British on the census form. Herbert John Hedley (18, born Essex) is likely her son. A German-born woman with British nationality in 1921 — she may have married an Englishman named Hedley before the war.
