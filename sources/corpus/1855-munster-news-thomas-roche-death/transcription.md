# Death of Thomas Roche, Esq.

*Munster News*, c. 19 December 1855

---

**DEATHS.**

On Saturday morning, the 15th instant, at his house in **George's-street**, after a short illness, in his **eighty-seventh year**, **Thomas Roche, Esq.**, elder brother of **William Roche, Esq.**

---

**Notes:**

- **Thomas Roche** (treeId I341): died **Saturday 15 December 1855** at George's Street, Limerick, "in his eighty-seventh year" — born ~1769.
- Described as "elder brother of William Roche, Esq." — his brother William was MP for Limerick and had predeceased him.
- Died at the same George's Street house where his wife Hellen had died in 1849.
