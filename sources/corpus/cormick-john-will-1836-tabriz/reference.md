## Context

Dr John Cormick was physician to Crown Prince Abbas Mirza at Tabriz, Persia. He was a colleague and predecessor of Edward Burgess in the British community at Tabriz. Cormick died in Persia; his will was proved in London on 6 August 1836.

**Relevance to Burgess family research:**  
The will may name executors, beneficiaries, or witnesses from the Tabriz British circle, potentially connecting to Edward Burgess or his associates. The executors and witnesses (Cooper, Becher) may have been part of the consular or merchant community that Burgess later joined.
