## Significance

Mary (later known as **Mary Ann Prichard**) married [Lewis Lewis](../../people/lewis-lewis.md) at Briton Ferry on 28 June 1834. She was buried at Aberavon on 10 July 1849, aged 44 (the register overestimates — she was actually 41 based on this baptism). Her parents Henry Prichard and Gwenllian [married](../1807-marriage-henry-prichard-gwenllian-harries-neath/transcription.md) at Neath St Thomas in November 1807, with Gwenllian's maiden name as **Harries**.
