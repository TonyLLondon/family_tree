# Marriage register — Giuseppe Rovaro Brizzi & Anastasia Castellanich, 25 August 1821, Zadar

Marriage of [Giuseppe Rovaro Brizzi](../../people/giuseppe-rovaro-brizzi.md) and [Anastasia Castellanich](../../people/anastasia-castellanich.md) on **25 August 1821** in Zara — date and parish cross-attested by the 1825 birth register of their daughter [Geralda Rovero Brizzi](../../people/geralda-rovero-brizzi.md), which records "matrimonio del 25 Agosto 1821" in this parish.
