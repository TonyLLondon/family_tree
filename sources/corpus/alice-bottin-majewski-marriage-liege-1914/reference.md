## Significance

This record provides independent confirmation that **Julien Bottin** and **Fanny Burgess** had a daughter named **Alice Jeanne Olga**, corroborating the Henderson FBB Timeline and the 1901 English census (where she appears as "Alice Bottin", born ~1889 in France). The full given names — Alice Jeanne Olga — were previously unknown.

The marriage in Liège establishes the Bottin family's Belgian connection and explains the absence of the family from the 1911 England census.
