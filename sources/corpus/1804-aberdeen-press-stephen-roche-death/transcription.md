# Death of Stephen Roche, Esq.

*Aberdeen Press and Journal*, 7 March 1804

---

At Limerick, in his **80th year**, **Stephen Roche, Esq.** the **oldest merchant** in that city.

---

**Notes:**

- **Stephen John Roche** (treeId I358): died ~February 1804 at Limerick, "in his 80th year" — born 5 December 1724 (Burke's 1833), consistent with age 79.
- "The oldest merchant in that city" — confirms his long mercantile career in Limerick spanning over four decades.
- Burke's 1833 gives death date as **12 February 1804**; this Aberdeen notice published 7 March 1804, ~3 weeks later.
