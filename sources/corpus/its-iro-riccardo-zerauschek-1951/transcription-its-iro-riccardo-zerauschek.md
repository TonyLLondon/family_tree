## Document 5: IRO Preliminary Information / Interview Report — image copy 4.png

Trieste, 17.8.51
ZERAUSCHEK, Ricardo — M
17.6.1896 at Zara, now YU

**Prel. Inf.:** the applicant is an Italian from Zara, terr. annexed to YUGO. On 10.6.40 he was resident at Zara. Came to Trieste on 1.4.1947. The subject got a temporary permitt from Municipality (Gradski narodni odbor) at Zara due to continue his singing learn in Italy. When he came to Trieste did not returne more to YUGO. Opted to retain Ital. citizenship in Trieste. Has permanent residence in Trieste. At present jobless.

1938 the subject lived at Zara with his brother who had a representative of Ford, Firestone, Zenith etc.. applicant worked as procurator.

1944 escaped from Zara to Losin Grande, to not be mobilized by Germans in YUGO. When the danger grew up, he went with his family on

2.45 to Silba. There was till

4.45 when returned to Zara. The subject got a job as singer with Opera at Split and Zagreb.

4.47 he got a permitt and left for Trieste. He motived his applies for this permitt with pretext to go in Italia to continue his singing-studies. Came to Trieste. Still at present jobless.

## Document 6: IRO Interview — Continuation / Political Statement — image copy 5.png

none

Came to Trieste on 1.4.1947. The subject was always contrary to every regime and dictature. He hated the Faschism and Nacism as well as communism. He has been born as free man and wants to be and to live in freedom. Is contrary the comm. regime, because it has taken away the principales rights of peoples as catholic he could not join with antireligious and communist

NO organisations. He was impossible to live under communist dictatorship and to go away he had requested the permitt to leave the country.

The subject is happy to stay in a democratic country and does not want to go again under comm. terror. He cannot live in a county where a man has fear to speak freely his mind.

USA, Canada

Signed: Riccardo Zerauschek
