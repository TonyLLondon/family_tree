## Notes

- **William Morgan** (head, age 40, b. ~1801) — identified as the father of [Anne Morgan](../../people/anne-morgan.md), who was born ~1829 in Llanfair, Carmarthenshire. William is a farmer at Coedevan, a farmstead in the Rhandir Ganol area of the parish.
- **Sarah Morgan** (wife, age 35, b. ~1806) — the 1881 census gives her birthplace as **Builth, Brecknockshire**, not Carmarthenshire. The 1841 census only recorded whether a person was born in the same county; Sarah was living in Carmarthenshire so was counted as "born in county," though her actual birthplace was in a different county. This is a common 1841 census inaccuracy.
- **Ann Morgan** (age 70, b. ~1771) — listed without a relationship label. Likely William's mother. FMP transcription records sex as Male, which is a transcription error; the name "Ann" and age 70 clearly indicate a woman. She would be a potential Gen 8 ancestor.
- **Jinken Morgan** (age 20, b. ~1821) — "Jinken" is a form of **Jenkin**, a common Welsh name. No relationship given. Could be an older son of William and Sarah (Sarah would have been ~15), William's younger brother, or another Morgan relative from the same parish.
- **David Morgan** and **William Morgan Jr** — sons, ages 9 and 7. Anne's brothers.
- **William Davies** (23) and **Mary Williams** (18) — farm servants or workers with different surnames.
- [Anne Morgan](../../people/anne-morgan.md) (b. ~1829) does not appear in this household. At about 12 years old she was probably away in domestic service, a common arrangement in rural Wales.
