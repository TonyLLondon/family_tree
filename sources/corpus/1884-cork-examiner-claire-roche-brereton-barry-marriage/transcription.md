# Barry — Roche Marriage

*Cork Examiner*, 30 January 1884

---

**MARRIAGES.**

**BARRY — ROCHE** — Jan 28, at the church of St. Kevin, Harrington street, Dublin, Ralph Brereton Barry, B.L., 20, Upper Fitzwilliam street, eldest son of the late James Barry, Esq., Limerick, to **Claire**, daughter of **William Roche**, Esq., **Crown Solicitor** for Limerick, 72, Harcourt street.

---

**Notes:**

- **Claire Roche** is another daughter of William Roche (treeId I137).
- Groom Ralph Brereton Barry was a Barrister-at-Law (B.L.), later County Court Judge R. W. Brereton Barry, K.C. (died 1920).
- Marriage at St. Kevin's Church, Harrington Street, Dublin — the same parish church used by the Roches.
