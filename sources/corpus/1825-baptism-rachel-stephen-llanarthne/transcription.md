| By whom the Ceremony was performed | Tho[mas] Williams |

Rachel Stephen was baptised at Llanarthne, a rural parish in Carmarthenshire about 10 miles east of Carmarthen town. Her father William Stephen was a farmer. She later married Lewis Lewis of Aberavon in Q4 1856 (GRO, Neath district). On the 1871 census she gave her birthplace as "Carmarthen" and her age as 41 (implying ~1830), five years younger than her actual baptism date of 1825.
