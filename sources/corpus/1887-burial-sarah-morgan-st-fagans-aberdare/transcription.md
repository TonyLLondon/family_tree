## Notes

- Sarah's death was registered in the **Merthyr Tydfil** registration district, which encompassed Aberdare at this date.
- Age 82 at burial (b. ~1805) is consistent with the 1841 census (age 35, b. ~1806) and 1881 census (age 75, b. ~1806). The one-year discrepancy is normal for age rounding.
- St Fagan's, Aberdare is the same parish church where Sarah's granddaughter [Margaret Griffiths](../../people/anne-morgan.md) (~1855–1863) was buried in 1863.
- [Sarah Morgan](../../people/sarah-morgan.md) was a widow by 1881, living with her daughter [Anne Griffiths](../../people/anne-morgan.md) at 11 Clive Street, Aberdare. She likely continued living there until her death.
