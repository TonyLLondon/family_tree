## Significance

This record confirms the death date and location of **Blaesi STUMP** (I165), our deepest confirmed direct ancestor in the STUMP line. The burial date of 7 October 1629 provides precise chronological anchor for Generation 13 of our ahnentafel chart.

The location Sulgen, Thurgau confirms the family's multi-generational presence in this Swiss parish, consistent with baptismal records of his children found in the same location between 1591-1631.
