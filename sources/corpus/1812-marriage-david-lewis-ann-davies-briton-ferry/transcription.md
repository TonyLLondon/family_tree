# Marriage of David Lewis and Ann Davies, Briton Ferry, 23 May 1812

**Register:** Marriages solemnized in the Parish of Briton Ferry in the County of Glamorgan  
**Reference:** P/70/CW/8, Page 23, No. 93  
**Date of Marriage:** 23 May 1812

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Groom | David Lewis |
| Groom's status | Bachelor |
| Groom's parish | Baglan |
| Bride | Ann Davies |
| Bride's status | Spinster (?) |
| Bride's parish | Briton Ferry |
| Married by | Banns |
| Officiant | Edw. Thomas, M[inister?] |
| First witness | Rees Powell (by mark) |
| Second witness | Morgan Evans (by mark) |

David Lewis of Baglan married Ann Davies of Briton Ferry. Both signed by mark. The witness Morgan Evans also appears as a witness at the 1834 marriage of their son Lewis Lewis to Mary Ann Prichard at the same church, twenty-two years later.

David was 18 years old at the marriage (baptised 7 January 1794); their first child Lewis Lewis was baptised at Briton Ferry on 14 March 1813, less than ten months after the wedding.
