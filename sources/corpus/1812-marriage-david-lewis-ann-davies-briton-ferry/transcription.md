## Significance

This marriage took place ten months before the [baptism of Lewis Lewis](../../people/lewis-lewis.md) on 14 March 1813 at the same church. It identifies Lewis Lewis's mother's maiden name as **Davies**. David Lewis was [baptised](1794-baptism-david-lewis-briton-ferry/transcription.md) at Briton Ferry on 7 January 1794, son of William Lewis and Catherine Rees.

The witness **Morgn Evans** also witnessed the [1834 marriage](../1834-marriage-lewis-lewis-prichard-briton-ferry/transcription.md) of Lewis Lewis × Mary Ann Prichard — a continuity of family connections at Briton Ferry across two generations.
