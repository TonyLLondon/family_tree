# Mr. Thomas Roche — House in Patrick Street

*Limerick Gazette*, April–September 1806 (repeated advertisement)

---

**LANDS AND HOUSES.**

MR. **THOMAS ROCHE**,

Will Set or Sell the Interest of his **HOUSE**, In **Patrick-Street**,

Its situation being so well known, is an excellent one for business.

Limerick, April 9.

---

**Notes:**

- **Thomas Roche** (treeId I341) advertising the sale or letting of his house on Patrick Street, Limerick, running from April to September 1806.
- Patrick Street was one of Limerick's main commercial thoroughfares.
- Thomas later moved to (or also had) **George's Street**, where he and Hellen died in 1849 and 1855 respectively.
- The repeated advertisement over six months suggests either difficulty selling during the economic uncertainties of the Napoleonic era, or a deliberate long marketing campaign.
