**Note:** This marriage shows intermarriage between the Brathwaite and Benson families. The bride was George's sister ("second daughter of William Brathwaite"). The marriage took place at George's house, confirming he was already an established householder at Swarthmoor by 1664 — before his own Quaker marriage to Agnes in 1666. Their daughter Ann had already been born in 1663.
