## Significance

This baptism record extends the **Jung genealogy** back to Generation 11, identifying the parents of **Johannes Jung** (1667-1726), who became the father of [Hans Ulrich Jung](../../../people/hanss-ulrich-jung.md) and grandfather of [Anna Margaretha Jung](../../../people/anna-margaretha-jung.md).

### **Five-Generation Jung Lineage:**
1. **Hanss Jung** × **Anna Marstetter** - Generation 11 (mid-1600s)
2. **Johannes Jung** (baptized 1667) - Generation 10  
3. **Hans Ulrich Jung** (c. 1700) - Generation 9
4. **Anna Margaretha Jung** (1731-1807) - Generation 8
5. **Hans Ulrich Stump** (1767-1807) - Generation 7 (via marriage to STUMP family)

### **Deep Swiss Roots:**
The Jung family presence in Sulgen extends back to at least the 1640s (based on Hans Jung's likely birth date), demonstrating multi-generational stability in this Thurgau parish.

### **Name Patterns:**
The baptism shows "Johanness Jung" (a variant of Johannes), consistent with Swiss German naming traditions. The father "Hanss Jung" follows the pattern of naming the eldest son after the father (Johannes after Hans).
