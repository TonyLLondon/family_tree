# Baptism register — Regina Margaritta Lanfranchi, born 26 December 1715, Zadar

Birth and baptism record of **Regina Margaritta Lanfranchi** in the **Duomo di Zara** (Sv. Stošija). The infant was in danger of dying at birth, so an **emergency home baptism** was performed on **2 January 1716** by **Don Giorgio Bonicelli**, "per causa d'imminente necessità". The note is registered as "2 Geno: 1715" under the Venetian *More Veneto* calendar (where the year began on 1 March). The full church ceremonies were deferred and the entry was formally entered into the Duomo register on **3 March 1716**.
