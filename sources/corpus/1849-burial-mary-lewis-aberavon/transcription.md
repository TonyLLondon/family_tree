| By whom the Ceremony was performed | R. Morgan |

Mary Lewis, née Prichard, wife of Lewis Lewis, was buried at Aberavon aged 44, having lived at Port Talbot. Her age of 44 places her birth at about 1805. The 1841 census had given her age as 30 (rounded down per the census convention for that year); if she was actually 36 in 1841, the birth year aligns at ~1805.
