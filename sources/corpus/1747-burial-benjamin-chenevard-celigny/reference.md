# Burial — Benjamin Chenevard, 2 December 1747

FamilySearch indexed record from the Reformed church registers of Céligny, Geneva. This is the burial record of Benjamin Chenevard, father of Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Benjamin Chenevard |
| **Born** | c. 1688 |
| **Died/Buried** | 2 December 1747 |
| **Place** | Céligny, Geneva, Switzerland |
| **Father** | Antoine Chenevard |

Benjamin married Christine Nerfin on 9 March 1720 at Céligny. Their daughter Jaqueline Susanne (baptised 1732) married Jean François Louis Montagnon in 1761. Benjamin died when Susanne was about 15 years old.
