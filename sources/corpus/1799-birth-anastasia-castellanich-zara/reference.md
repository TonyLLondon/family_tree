# Birth register — Anastasia Castellanich, Zadar, 1799

## Likely parents (working hypothesis)

The collection holds the **1796 marriage register entry of Castellanich × Paletta** in this same Zadar parish run (`1796-marriage-castellanich-paletta-zara`). Given (a) the rarity of the surname combination in 1790s Zara and (b) the close fit between a 1796 marriage and a 1799 daughter, the 1796 couple are most likely Anastasia's parents — but this requires confirmation by reading both register entries side by side and matching the father's given name across the two records.
