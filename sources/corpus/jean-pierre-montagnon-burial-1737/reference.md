## Significance

This burial record extends the **MONTAGNON lineage** back another generation beyond what was previously documented:

### **Five-Generation MONTAGNON Lineage:**
1. **Jean Pierre MONTAGNON** (1692-1737) - Generation 1 (subject)  
2. **Jean François Louis MONTAGNON** (~1737-1786) - Generation 2 (son)
3. **Louis MONTAGNON** - Generation 3 (grandson)  
4. **Jeanne Bénigne MONTAGNON** (1805-?) - Generation 4 (great-granddaughter)
5. **Marc François STUMP** (1834-1903) - Generation 5 (great-great-grandson)

### **Geographic Consistency:**
The Aubonne location confirms the family's multi-generational presence in the Vaud region, consistent with other MONTAGNON records spanning from the 1690s through the 1800s.

### **Chronological Framework:**
- Birth year 1692 places Jean Pierre in the late 17th century
- Death/burial 1737 aligns with the birth of his son Jean François Louis (~1737)
- Extends confirmed MONTAGNON ancestry back ~135 years from Jeanne Bénigne's birth
