# Marriage register — Simeone Vincenzo Boara & Vittoria Elisa Carobbi, 8 August 1849, Zadar

Marriage in the **Collegiate Parish Church of Sv. Šime** (San Simeone), **Zara**, **8 August 1849** — the union from which descend the Boara children of late-Habsburg Zara: Evelina, Artemia, Riccardo, Bate (Battista), Casimiro, Carmela, Alfonso, and Ottilia (the youngest, born 1865, who married Pietro Pio Addobbati in 1890).
