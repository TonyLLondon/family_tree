## Research Methodology Success

This extension demonstrates the continued effectiveness of **iterative parent-searching via death records**:

1. **Civil registration** (post-1880) provides detailed parent information
2. **Catholic church records** extend into colonial and early republican periods
3. **Burial records** consistently capture family relationships
4. **Geographic specificity** helps distinguish between common surnames

The same methodology successfully extended **CERPA**, **PÉREZ**, and **ESCOBAR** lines, confirming its reliability for Chilean genealogical research.

The **Elqui-Coquimbo-Illapel** geographic concentration suggests potential involvement in Chile's **colonial and early republican mining economy**:

- **La Serena** (Elqui region): Colonial administrative and mining center
- **Illapel** (Coquimbo region): Historical gold and copper mining area

This pattern is consistent with **internal Chilean migration** from northern mining regions toward central administrative and agricultural areas during the 19th century.
