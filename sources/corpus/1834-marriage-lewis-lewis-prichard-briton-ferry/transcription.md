# Marriage of Lewis Lewis and Mary Ann Prichard, Briton Ferry, 28 June 1834

**Register:** Marriages solemnized in the Parish of Briton Ferry in the County of Glamorgan in the Year 1834  
**Reference:** P/70/CW/9, Page 20, No. 59  
**Date of Marriage:** 28 June 1834

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Groom | Lewis Lewis |
| Groom's status | Bachelor |
| Groom's residence | Aberavon |
| Bride | Mary Ann Prichard |
| Bride's status | Spinster |
| Bride's residence | Briton Ferry |
| Married by | Banns |
| Officiant | Edw. Thomas, P. Curate |
| First witness | Thomas Williams |
| Second witness | Morgan Evans |

Both parties signed by mark. Lewis Lewis was described as "of the Parish of Aberavon," suggesting he had already moved for work, while Mary Ann Prichard was of Briton Ferry. The marriage took place at Briton Ferry parish church (the bride's parish, as was customary).
