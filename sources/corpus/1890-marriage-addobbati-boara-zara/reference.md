## Genealogical position

This marriage produces [Ester Addobbati](../../people/ester-addobbati.md), Fulvia Zerauschek’s mother — slot **18 × 19** on the Lewis ahnentafel. The four witnesses define the Addobbati civic milieu in late-Habsburg Zara: a Lieutenancy accountant kinsman, a serving k.k. officer in Vienna, an imperial councillor, and a local notable.
