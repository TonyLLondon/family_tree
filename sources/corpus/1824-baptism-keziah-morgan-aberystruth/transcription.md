**Image:** [Aberystruth baptism register, 1824](../../media/docs/1824-baptism-keziah-morgan-aberystruth-monmouthshire.jpg)

**Notes:** The register entry reads "Keziah, born 7th March, daughter of Thomas & Charlotte" — but the 1871 census records Keziah Morgan as **male**, head of household, collier. The register column says "daughter" which may be a scribal error, or the child's sex was ambiguous at baptism. The family lived at **Nantyglo**, a hamlet in Aberystruth parish near Blaina, consistent with the 1871 census birthplace "Blaina, Monmouthshire."

The same register page records other families at Nantyglo, all miners — Morgan, Charles, Davies, Williams — the typical ironworks and mining community of the western Monmouthshire valleys.
