# Dublin Evening Post — Catholic Petition, 1792

**Date:** 12 May 1792 (also 10 May 1792)
**Newspaper:** Dublin Evening Post
**FMP:** [article link](https://www.findmypast.co.uk/search-newspapers/article/BL/0000435/17920512/001)

## Transcription (partial, from OCR)

> ... **Stephen Roche John, city of Limerick, [Philip] Roche John, city of Limerick**, George [Ryan], co. Tipperary, Robert Hardiman, Loughrea, co. Galway, John [...], city of ...

## Notes

A Catholic petition to the Lord Lieutenant, listing prominent Catholic gentlemen across Munster and Connacht. Both **Stephen Roche John** (1724–1804) and his brother **Philip Roche John** (~1740–1797) are listed as signatories from Limerick. Also listed in the Belfast News-Letter, 3 January 1792.

George Ryan of Inch, Co. Tipperary was the husband of Mary Roche, Stephen's daughter by his first wife Margaret Meade — confirming the family network.
