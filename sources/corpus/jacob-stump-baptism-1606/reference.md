## Significance

This baptism record potentially establishes **Jacob Stump** (1606-c.1654) as the father of **Augustin Stump** (baptized 1628), extending the STUMP genealogy back to **Generation 15** (early 17th century).

## Research Decision

**Chosen Line:** **Hanssen Stump** × **Annae Rued** as parents

**Reasoning:**
1. **Chronological fit:** Hanssen Stump died 1647-1656, giving adequate lifespan for Jacob's generation
2. **Baschli timing issue:** Baschli Stump died September 1629, just 9 months after Augustin's baptism (Dec 1628), suggesting Baschli was likely older generation
3. **Family pattern consistency:** Multiple children spanning 1602-1608 for Baschli suggests he was established earlier

**Uncertainty:** This determination requires further verification through additional records or DNA evidence.
