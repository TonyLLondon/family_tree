## Significance

Henry and Gwenllian's daughter **Mary [Ann] Prichard** was [baptised](../1808-baptism-mary-prichard-neath/transcription.md) at Neath St Thomas on 4 July 1808 (born 23 Jun 1808), confirming these are her parents. Mary married [Lewis Lewis](../../people/lewis-lewis.md) at Briton Ferry on 28 June 1834 and died 10 July 1849 at Port Talbot.

This record reveals Gwenllian's maiden name as **Harries** — a new surname in the ancestral tree.
