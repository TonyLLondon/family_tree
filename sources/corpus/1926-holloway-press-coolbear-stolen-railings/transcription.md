## Notes

Post-war court appearance at North London, September 1926. Confirms:

- **Full name:** Archibald Percy Coolbear.
- **Age 34** — consistent with birth in March 1892.
- **Address: 20 Queensland Road, Holloway** — matches the Ministry of Pensions card (P.F. 23 M.) which gives widow Rosina's address as **10 Queensland Road, Holloway, N.4** in 1929. The family was established in the Queensland Road area of Holloway by this date.

This places Coolbear in north London by 1926, having moved from Southend-on-Sea (his RAF discharge address in 1919). He died in Hendon in 1928, two years after this court appearance.
