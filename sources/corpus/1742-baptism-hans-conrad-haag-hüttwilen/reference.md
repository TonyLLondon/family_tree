# Baptism — Hans Conrad Haag, 24 June 1742, Hüttwilen

FamilySearch indexed record from the Swiss church registers. This is the baptism record of Marc François Stump's maternal great-grandfather (Anna Margaretha Hagg's father).

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Hans Conrad Haag |
| **Baptised** | 24 June 1742 |
| **Place** | Hüttwilen, Thurgau, Switzerland |
| **Father** | Johann Conrad Haag |
| **Mother** | Anna Barbara Erbig (Erbin) |

This record establishes the parents of Hans Conrad Haag (I182), who married Margaretha Freyenmuth and fathered Anna Margaretha Hagg (I145). The father Johann Conrad Haag had other children including Anna Barbara Haag (b. 1713, d. 1741), indicating he was active in the Hüttwilen parish from at least the early 1710s.
