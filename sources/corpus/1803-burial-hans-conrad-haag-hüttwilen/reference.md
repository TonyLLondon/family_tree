# Burial — Hans Conrad Haag, 15 September 1803, Hüttwilen

FamilySearch indexed record from the Swiss church registers. This is the burial record of Marc François Stump's maternal great-grandfather.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Joh Conrad Haag |
| **Born** | 1742 |
| **Buried** | 15 September 1803 |
| **Place** | Hüttwilen, Thurgau, Switzerland |

Hans Conrad Haag died at roughly sixty-one, just four years before his son-in-law Hans Ulrich Stump was buried at nearby Sulgen in December 1807. The burial place at Hüttwilen is consistent with his baptism there in 1742.
