## Working hypothesis

That gives the chain:

- **1776** Paletta × Pettessich — likely *maternal grandparents* of Anastasia Castellanich
- **1796** Castellanich × [Paletta] — likely *parents* of Anastasia Castellanich
- **1799** birth of Anastasia Castellanich
- **1821** Anastasia × Giuseppe Rovaro Brizzi

Confirmation requires matching the Paletta given names across the 1776 and 1796 entries and connecting the same Paletta to the 1799 Castellanich baptism.
