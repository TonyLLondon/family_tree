## Notes

- Thomas's birthplace "Lampeter, Brecknockshire" — in 1861 given as "Llanworted" (Llanwrtyd). These are different settlements in Brecknockshire. The discrepancy may reflect an imprecise local name or enumerator error; both place him firmly in rural Brecknockshire.
- Thomas's occupation has changed from mining to **Moulder** (iron moulder/caster). His son David (23) also works as a moulder — likely at the Nantyglo ironworks or nearby.
- **Mary** (wife, b. ~1803) born at **Cadirkstone**, Glamorganshire. This may be "Cadoxton" (Cadoxton-juxta-Neath) or another Glamorganshire hamlet.
- Ann (~1826), the eldest daughter from 1841, is absent — probably married.
- Two younger daughters have appeared: Sarah (~1842) and Mary (~1845), born after the 1841 census.
