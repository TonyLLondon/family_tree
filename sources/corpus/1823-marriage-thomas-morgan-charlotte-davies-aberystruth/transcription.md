## Significance

This marriage took place eight months before the [baptism of their son Keziah Morgan](../1824-baptism-keziah-morgan-aberystruth/transcription.md) on 4 July 1824. It confirms Charlotte's maiden name as **Davies**, correcting the family tree which previously had her surname unknown.
