# Death Registration — infant Cerpa Villegas, 1 November 1900, Chanco

FamilySearch indexed record from the Chilean civil registration. An unnamed infant boy who died in 1900, registered in Chanco. The parents confirm Francisco Cerpa's first marriage to Bersabé/Betsabe Villegas — predating his second marriage to Ana González (mother of Miguel Luis Cerpa González).

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Cerpa Villegas (unnamed) |
| **Born** | 1900 |
| **Died** | 1 November 1900 |
| **Place** | Chanco, Cauquenes, Maule, Chile |
| **Father** | Francisco Cerpa |
| **Mother** | Betsabe Villegas |
| **Gender** | Male |

This record, together with the 1890 birth of Abraham del Carmen Cerpa (also Francisco × Bersabé Villegas, Chanco), establishes that Francisco Cerpa had at least two children by his first wife across a decade. The date of Bersabé Villegas's death and Francisco's subsequent marriage to Ana González are not yet determined.
