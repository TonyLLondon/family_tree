# Quaker Birth Register — Brathwaite Children, Swarthmoor, 1667–1671

**Source:** TNA RG6/1085, pp. 8, 18, 19. Three register pages covering George & Agnes Brathwaite's children born after their 1666 marriage.

---

## Edward Brathwaite — 2 July 1667

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0016` (p. 8, births 1666–1667)

Edward son of George Brathwaite & Agnes his wife of [Hawrey?] was borne there the second day of the fifth month anno Domini 1667

**Also on this page:** Benjamin son of James Brathwaite & Katherine his wife (18 Mar 1666) — a different Brathwaite family.

---

## Agnes Brathwaite — 4 June 1669

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0018` (p. 18, births 1669)

Agnes daughter of George Brathwaite & Agnes his wife of Hawrey was borne there the fourth day of the fourth month Anno Domini 1669

**Also on this page:** John Holme (son of George Holme, 1669), Mary Dixon, and other Swarthmoor families.

---

## Dorothy Brathwaite — 10 February 1671

**Image:** `TNA/RG6/1085/0/0019` (p. 34, births 1671–1672)

Dorothy daughter of George Brathwaite & Agnes his wife of Hawrey was borne... the fourth day of the twelfth month Anno Domini 1671

**Also on this page:**
- **Abigail Benson** — Abigail daughter of **William Benson & Margaret** his wife borne... the [?] day of the second month Anno Domini 1671. (This is a *different* Benson family from Agnes's father Barnard Benson.)
- **Agnes Brathwaite** (1672) — Agnes daughter of **James Brathwaite and Maybell** his wife, born the second day of the second month 1672. (Different Brathwaite family.)
