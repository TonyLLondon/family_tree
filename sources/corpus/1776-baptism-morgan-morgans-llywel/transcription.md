## Notes

- This is the probable baptism of [Morgan Morgans](../../people/morgan-morgans-llywel.md) (I659), who appears as the father of William Morgan in the 1795 Llywel baptism record. The patronymic "Morgan Morgans" means "Morgan son of Morgan" — and this record shows the father's name as simply **Morgan**, consistent with the patronymic naming system.
- Morgan Morgans would have been 18–19 when his son William was baptized in September 1795. Young for a father, but not uncommon in 18th-century rural Wales.
- **Rachel** — the mother. Nothing further is known about her.
- **Morgan** — the father. This creates a three-generation patronymic chain: Morgan → Morgan Morgans → William Morgan.
