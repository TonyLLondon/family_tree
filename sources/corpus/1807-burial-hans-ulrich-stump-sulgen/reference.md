# Burial — Hans Ulrich Stump, 18 December 1807, Sulgen

FamilySearch indexed record from the Swiss church registers. This is the burial record of Marc François Stump's paternal grandfather.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Hs Ulrich Stump |
| **Born** | 1767 |
| **Buried** | 18 December 1807 |
| **Place** | Sulgen, Thurgau, Switzerland |
| **Wife** | A Margreth Hagen (Anna Margaretha Hagg) |

A duplicate entry exists: [66JN-HGN4](https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:66JN-HGN4) with the wife's surname spelled "Haggen."

Hans Ulrich died at roughly forty, leaving his son Jakob (born 1800) as a seven-year-old. The burial place at Sulgen is consistent with the Stump family's presence in the Erlen–Sulgen Reformed parishes across several generations.
