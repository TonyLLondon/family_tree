# Marriage register — Benjamin Chenevard × Christine Nerfin, 9 March 1720

FamilySearch indexed family register entry from the Reformed church registers at Céligny, Geneva. This record identifies the parents of both Benjamin Chenevard and Christine Nerfin, who are the parents of Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard (wife of Jean François Louis Montagnon).

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Husband** | Benjamin Chenevard |
| **Husband's father** | Antoine Chenevard |
| **Wife** | Christine Nerfin |
| **Wife's father** | Humbert Nerfin |
| **Date** | 9 March 1720 |
| **Place** | Céligny, Geneva, Switzerland |

A duplicate indexed entry (6NQL-VP2D) renders the surname as "Nerpin" rather than "Nerfin."

Benjamin Chenevard was born c. 1688 (from his burial record) and was the son of Antoine Chenevard. Christine Nerfin was the daughter of Humbert Nerfin. The couple had children baptised at Céligny including Jean François (1721, died as infant) and Jaqueline Susanne (1732).
