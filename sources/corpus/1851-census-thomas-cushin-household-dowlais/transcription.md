Thomas has moved from Trevethin to **Dowlais** and married **Anne** (née Mainwaring) from **Llansamlet** — she is Welsh, not Irish. He works as a **filler** (charging blast furnaces with ore and coke). Daughter Mary was born at **Abersychan** (~1847), confirming Thomas was still near Pontypool when she was conceived. Son Thomas Jr born at Dowlais ~1851. Three Cork lodgers share the house — the Irish network persists.
