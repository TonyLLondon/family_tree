Mary is a widow — [David Cushen](../../people/david-cushen.md) died between 1901 and 1911. Son David T (31) is himself a widower; the 7-year-old Elizabeth Cushen is his daughter. Catherine (née Cushen) married William Richards, a **loco fireman** — the same trade as **James Lynch**, who married Catherine's sister Lily. The two brothers-in-law likely worked together on the Dowlais ironworks railways.

Lily (Elizabeth Lilian) is absent — she was at [4 Cwm Canal, Dowlais](../../sources/corpus/1911-census-james-elizabeth-lynch-cwm-canal-dowlais/) with husband James Lynch.
