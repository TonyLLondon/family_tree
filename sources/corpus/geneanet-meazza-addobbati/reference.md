# Giuseppe Meazza — Addobbati / Luxardo (Geneanet)

## Summary

**Giuseppe Meazza** (Geneanet user **gmeazza**) is an independent genealogical researcher who published a large public tree on Geneanet centred on the **Addobbati** surname in **Zadar (Zara), Dalmatia**, with extensive cross-links to allied families (**Luxardo**, **Lanfranchi**, **Plarino**, **Coltelli**, and related lines).

The tree is widely cited as a **finding aid**: it aggregates names, approximate dates, and proposed relationships across centuries—from early notarial-era Addobbati into **19th-century Habsburg Dalmatian** office-holding and nobility. It is **not primary evidence**. Baptisms, marriages, burials, civil registers, and archival inventories in this vault remain the standard for proof; Meazza’s tree is a **secondary source**: another researcher’s synthesis, useful for orientation, collateral discovery, and cross-checking—not a substitute for records.

**Access:** Public Geneanet tree; a free account is typically required for full navigation and detail.

**Offline capture:** A repo-held snapshot lives at [obrien-roche-url-offline-captures/13-geneanet-gmeazza-addobbati-pietro-paolo.html](../obrien-roche-url-offline-captures/13-geneanet-gmeazza-addobbati-pietro-paolo.html).
