# Family register — Jean François Louis Montagnon × Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard, 28 October 1761

FamilySearch indexed family register entry from the Reformed church registers at Céligny, Geneva. This is a critical record identifying both sets of parents for the Montagnon–Chenevard couple, who are Marc François Stump's maternal great-great-grandparents.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Husband** | Jean François Louis Montagnon |
| **Husband's father** | Jean Pierre Montagnon |
| **Husband's mother** | Jeanne Cusin |
| **Wife** | Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard |
| **Wife's father** | Benjamin Chenevard |
| **Wife's mother** | Christine Nerfin |
| **Date** | 28 October 1761 (variant entry: 21 November 1761) |
| **Place** | Céligny, Geneva, Switzerland |

Multiple indexed entries exist for this family register (ARKs 6NQL-FNTW, 6NQ2-K2PD, 6NQL-FNRT, 6NQL-Z3Y5, 6NQL-FNRY, 6NQL-2YDB, 6NQL-27K7, 6NQL-FNT9, 6NQL-2Y64), representing different pages or transcription passes of the same register. They consistently name:

- **Groom's parents:** Jean Pierre Montagnon + Jeanne Cusin
- **Bride's parents:** Benjamin Chenevard + Christine Nerfin

One variant (6NQL-Z3Y5) misspells the surname as "Mon**r**agnon." The duplicate entries reinforce the reliability of the parental identifications.

**Parentage correction:** An earlier research pass attributed Jean Louis Montagnon's parents to Jean Michel Montagnon + Catherine Vougaz based on a burial record (6NQ2-TV9P, d. 1817). That burial is for a different "Jean Louis Montagnon" (b. 1743, son of Jean Michel) who married Jeanne Françoise Vougaz in 1780 (6NQG-NVVV). Our ancestor Jean François Louis Montagnon (son of Jean Pierre, married Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard) was probably born ~1737 and died 4 February 1786 at Aubonne (6NQL-3F71).
