# Death of Thomas Roche, Esq.

*Leinster Express*, 22 December 1855

---

December 15, after a short illness, in his **87th year**, **Thomas Roche, Esq.**, elder brother of **William Roche, Esq., M.P.**, deceased, and surviving partner of the late Bank of **Thomas and William Roche**, of Limerick.

---

**Notes:**

- Confirms Thomas Roche (treeId I341) died **15 December 1855**, aged 86 (87th year), born ~1769.
- "Elder brother of William Roche, Esq., M.P., deceased" — William MP had predeceased Thomas.
- **"Surviving partner of the late Bank of Thomas and William Roche, of Limerick"** — a major new detail: Thomas and William had operated a **bank** together in Limerick. This elevates the family from merchants/solicitors to bankers — a crucial piece of the Limerick Catholic elite picture.
