## Notes

- Annie Fensom (I291, b. 29 Jan 1893) is **not with her parents** at this census. At 18 she is already living independently, boarding on the second floor of 17 North Road with the Watts family.
- **Rose Elizabeth M Watts** is the same **Rose Elizabeth Maud Watts** who witnessed [Annie's marriage to Alfred Evans](../../../sources/corpus/st-davids-islington-marriage-evans-fensom-1912/transcription.md) at St David's Church on 25 December 1912 — just twenty months after this census. They were housemates, the same age, born in the same neighbourhood, and working side by side at the **Star Brush Factory** on Eden Grove, Holloway.
- The Star Brush connection runs through the family: Annie's workplace in 1911 is the same **Star Brush Company, Eden Grove, Holloway** where she would still be employed in the [1921 Census](../../../sources/corpus/1921-census-evans-bratton-street-islington/transcription.md), by then as a married woman with three children.
- Eden Grove itself is the Fensom family's street — Annie's father David William Fensom lived at no. 11 in 1891, and her grandparents David and Georgiana Fensom at no. 13 in 1901.
- The schedule is signed by **John Jas Watts** with the postal address "17 North Rd, Holloway, N."
- Annie's birthplace is recorded as **London, Holloway** — more specific than the "Islington" given in other records.
