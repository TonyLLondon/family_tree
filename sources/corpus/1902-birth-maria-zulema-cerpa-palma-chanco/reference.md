# Birth Registration — María Zulema Cerpa Palma, 5 January 1902, Chanco

FamilySearch indexed record from the Chilean civil registration. María Zulema is another child of Francisco Cerpa registered in Chanco. The mother is recorded as "Betsabe Palma de Cerpa" — likely the same person as Bersabé Villegas, with Palma possibly being her maternal surname or a transcription variant.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | María Zulema Cerpa Palma |
| **Birth** | 5 January 1902 |
| **Place** | Chanco, Cauquenes, Maule, Chile |
| **Father** | Francisco Cerpa |
| **Mother** | Betsabe Palma de Cerpa |
| **Gender** | Female |

The surname discrepancy (Villegas in 1890/1900 records, Palma in 1902) could reflect a double-barrelled maternal name (Villegas Palma), different informant knowledge, or two distinct women. The 1902 date falls between the last Villegas child (1900) and the first González child, making it plausible this is still the first wife.
