## Significance

Elisabet is a sibling of François Montagnon (bap. 22 Dec 1667), both children of **Jean Montagnon**. This corroborates the existence of Jean Montagnon as a patriarch at Aubonne in the late 1660s–1670s. The image ARK (3:1:3Q9M-C39V-MS3P) is adjacent to François's baptism image (MS39), confirming they come from the same register volume.
