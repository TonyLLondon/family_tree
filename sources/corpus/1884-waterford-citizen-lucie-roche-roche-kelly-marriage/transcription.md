# Roche-Kelly and Roche — Marriage

*Waterford Citizen*, September 1884

---

**MARRIAGE.**

**ROCHE-KELLY AND ROCHE** — On the 8th September, at the Church of St. Kevin, Dublin, **Edward Roche-Kelly**, J.P., Johnstown House, co. Cork, to **Lucie Mary**, daughter of **William Roche**, 72, Harcourt-street, **Crown Solicitor** for Limerick.

---

**Notes:**

- **Lucie Mary Roche** is another daughter of William Roche (treeId I137).
- Groom Edward Roche-Kelly was a Justice of the Peace, Johnstown House, co. Cork.
- Again at St. Kevin's Church, Dublin, consistent with the family's parish.

**Also reported in:** *Limerick Chronicle*, 9 Sep 1884.
