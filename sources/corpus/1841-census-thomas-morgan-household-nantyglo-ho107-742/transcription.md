## Notes

- **Thomas Morgan** (head, b. ~1801 as per 1841 rounding) is identified as the father of [Keziah Morgan](../../people/keziah-morgan.md). He married Charlotte Davies at Aberystruth on 5 Jul 1823; Keziah was born 7 Mar 1824. Charlotte died ~1824–1825 and Thomas married **Mary** (his wife in 1841) as his second wife.
- **Mary Morgan** (wife, b. ~1806 per 1841; b. ~1803 per 1851 census) — second wife. Born "Cadirkstone, Glamorganshire" per the 1851 census.
- [Keziah Morgan](../../people/keziah-morgan.md) (b. 1824) is absent — at 17 he had likely left home to work in the mines.
- **Ann** (~1826) — eldest child of Thomas × Mary; gone from the household by 1851.
- **William** and **Jane** (~1839) — twins.
