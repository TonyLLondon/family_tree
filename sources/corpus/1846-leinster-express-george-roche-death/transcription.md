# Death of George Roche, Esq., of Granagh Castle

*Leinster Express*, 7 March 1846

---

**DEATHS.**

At his seat, near Tramore, county Waterford, **George Roche, Esq., of Granagh Castle, Kilkenny**, aged **86**, eldest brother of **Thomas Roche, Esq.**, and **Wm. Roche, Esq.**, present High Sheriff of Limerick.

---

**Notes:**

- George Roche was a son of [Stephen John Roche](../../../people/stephen-john-roche.md) from his first marriage (to Margaret Meade). Burke's 1833 lists him as successor to his brother John and the current chief of the house, seated at Granagh Castle, Kilkennyshire.
- Died aged 86 (~born 1760) at his seat near Tramore, Co. Waterford.
- **William Roche** here described as "present High Sheriff of Limerick" — a different detail from the earlier "M.P." designation; William may have held both offices at different times, or this is a variant report.
- Thomas and William identified as his brothers (half-brothers: from Stephen's second marriage to Sarah O'Bryen).
