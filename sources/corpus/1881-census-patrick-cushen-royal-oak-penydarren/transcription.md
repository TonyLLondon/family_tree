Patrick Cushen (b. ~1811, Ireland) is the senior Cushen patriarch. A widower at 70, living with his daughter Anne (Walsh) and son-in-law Patrick Walsh at the Royal Oak Inn. Patrick was an **overman** — a supervisory role in the ironworks — and probably a brother of [Thomas Cushen](../../people/thomas-cushen.md) (b. 1821, Cork).
