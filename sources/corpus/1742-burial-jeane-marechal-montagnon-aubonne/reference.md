## Significance

Confirms the marriage of **Jeane Marechal** (b. 1665) to **François Montagnon** (= Jean François Montagnon, b. 1666, d. 1732). She survived her husband by ten years. The indexed surname "Marechal Montagnon" follows the Vaudois convention of recording a married woman with both maiden and married surnames.

Her burial comes from the same Aubonne register volume as her husband's burial (15 Sep 1732, image 3:1:3Q9M-CS1X-P9YT-Y).
