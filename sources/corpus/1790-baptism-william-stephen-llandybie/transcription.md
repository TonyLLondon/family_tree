## Significance

William Stephen later married **Margaret Hopkins** at Llanarthne on 18 Nov 1814 (of Llandebie — i.e. the same Llandybie parish), confirming this baptism belongs to the right man. He subsequently married **Rachel Morley** at Llanarthne on 27 Jan 1826. His daughter [Rachel Stephen](../../people/rachel-james.md), baptised 9 Oct 1825 at Llanarthne (mother recorded as "Mary"), was the second wife of [Lewis Lewis](../../people/lewis-lewis.md) and mother of [David John Lewis](../../people/david-john-lewis-1857.md).

This record extends the Stephen line one generation further back to **Thomas Stephen** and **Jane** of Llandybie, Carmarthenshire, active in the late 18th century.
