# Baptism — Anna Margaretha Freyenmuth, 15 September 1743, Wigoltingen

FamilySearch indexed record from the Swiss baptism registers. This is the baptism of Marc François Stump's great-grandmother (mother of Anna Margaretha Hagg's father Hans Conrad Haag's wife).

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Anna Margaretha Freyenmuth (Freÿenmüth) |
| **Christened** | 15 September 1743 |
| **Place** | Wigoltingen (Wagerswil, Engwang), Thurgau, Switzerland |
| **Father** | Hr. Hs. Jacob Freyenmuth (Hans Jacob Freyenmuth) |
| **Mother** | Fr. Anna Marg Keller (Anna Margaretha Keller) |

The GEDCOM gives the christening date as 15 October 1743 — one month off from the indexed record's 15 September 1743. The discrepancy is likely a transcription error in the GEDCOM; the indexed records from two independent FamilySearch collections both give September.
