## Significance

William Lewis's first marriage. He had a son **William Lewis** (bapt. 25 May 1783 at Briton Ferry, mother **Mary** — matching this wife). Mary David appears to have died between 1783 and ~1793, after which William married **Catherine Rees**. Their son [David Lewis](../../people/lewis-lewis.md) was baptised 7 January 1794 at Briton Ferry — establishing the line down to Lewis Lewis (1813).

Both this marriage and the 1812 marriage of David Lewis × Ann Davies use the same Briton Ferry register (P/70/CW/8), confirming continuity of the Lewis family at this parish across three generations.
