# Baptism — Jeanne Bénigne Montagnon, 15 July 1805

FamilySearch indexed record from the Swiss church registers. This is the baptism record of Jeanne Bénigne Montagnon, wife of Hans Jacob Stump (b. 1800) and mother of Marc François Stump.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Jeanne Bénigne Montagnon |
| **Baptised** | 15 July 1805 |
| **Place** | Switzerland (Canton de Vaud) |
| **Father** | Louis Montagnon |
| **Mother** | Jeanne Françoise Dumartheray |

This record corrects the GEDCOM export, which placed Jeanne's birth at "1800" in "France." She was actually born in 1805 in the Canton de Vaud, the French-speaking region of western Switzerland — not France proper. The Montagnon family was concentrated in the Aubonne area near Lac Léman, and they were Reformed Protestants, which explains how a French-speaker ended up in the Reformed parish of Erlen, Thurgau.
