# Medical Appointment

*Limerick Reporter*, 17 October 1890

---

**Dr. Antony Roche**, **72 Harcourt st.**, **Dublin**, has been appointed Professor of Medical Jurisprudence and Hygiene in the Catholic University Medical School. **Dr. Roche** is the son of our old and esteemed townsman, **Wm. Roche**, Crown Solicitor.

*(FindMyPast search snippet; ~45 words.)*

---

**Notes:**

- **Dr Antony Roche** is a brother of **Catherine Mary Roche**. His address at **72 Harcourt Street** is the family home/firm.
- The *Limerick Reporter* calling **William** "our old and esteemed townsman" confirms William's Limerick origins despite his Dublin practice.
