# Baptism — Henri Samuel Montagnon, 12 October 1764

FamilySearch indexed record from the Reformed church registers at Aubonne, Vaud. This is a child's baptism record that confirms the Montagnon–Chenevard couple at Aubonne.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Henri Samuel Montagnon |
| **Baptised** | 12 October 1764 |
| **Place** | Aubonne, Vaud, Switzerland |
| **Father** | Jean François Louis Montagnon |
| **Mother** | Suzanne Chenevard |

This record, along with other children's baptisms at Aubonne (1767–1776) and one at Céligny (1762), confirms Jean François Louis Montagnon and Suzanne Chenevard as a couple with children across both parishes. The father's full name "Jean François Louis" appears here, consistent with the marriage register (6NQL-FNTW) — his mother "Suzanne Chenevard" is the same "Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard" from the register.
