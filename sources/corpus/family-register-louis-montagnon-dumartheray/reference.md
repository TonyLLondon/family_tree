# Family register — Louis Montagnon × Jeanne Françoise Dumartheray

FamilySearch indexed family register entry from the Canton de Vaud church records. This record identifies the parents and grandparents of Jeanne Bénigne Montagnon on Marc François Stump's maternal side.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Husband** | Louis Montagnon |
| **Husband's parents** | Jean Louis Montagnon + Susanne Cheneveau |
| **Wife** | Jeanne Françoise Dumartheray |
| **Wife's parents** | Jean Gabriel Nicolas Dumartheray + Elisabeth Borrel |
| **Place** | Vaud, Switzerland (Aubonne area) |

This single record identifies both sets of great-grandparents on Marc's maternal line:

- **Paternal (Montagnon):** Jean Louis Montagnon and Susanne Cheneveau
- **Maternal (Dumartheray):** Jean Gabriel Nicolas Dumartheray and Elisabeth Borrel

The Montagnon family was concentrated in the Aubonne–Penthalaz–Rolle area of the Canton de Vaud, on the northern shore of Lac Léman. The Dumartheray surname is also from the Vaud region. Both families were Reformed Protestants, which connects to the Reformed parish of Erlen, Thurgau where daughter Jeanne Bénigne married Jakob Stump in 1832.
