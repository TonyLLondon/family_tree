## Notes

### Three-generation household — grandmother as head

The head of this household is **Catherine Enright** (the grandmother), not her married daughter Catherine O'Shea (née Enright, I119). The grandmother — identified as the informant on [Mary O'Shea's 1893 birth certificate](../../../media/docs/Ireland%20birth%20certificate%20Mary%20O%27Shea%201893-02-19%20Monkstown%20parents%20Cornelius%20O%27Shea%20Catherine%20Enright%20copy%201967.jpg) — was already living with or near the family at Castle Terrace, Monkstown from at least 1893. She is a **Widow** and speaks **Irish and English**, placing her birth in a generation and community where Irish was still a living vernacular.

Age 69 (born ~1832) is consistent with the 1911 census, which records her age as 79.

### Ellen Enright — the aunt

**Ellen Enright**, age 33, is the grandmother's daughter and therefore sister (or half-sister) of Catherine O'Shea (I119). She is unmarried and working as a **Dressmaker** — a skilled trade common among unmarried Cork women. The name "Ellie Enright" appears as a witness on [Cornelius and Catherine's 1892 marriage record](../../../people/cornelius-oshea.md), confirming the family connection. Ellen is absent from the 1911 household.

### Catherine O'Shea (née Enright, I119) — "Daughter" and "Married"

Catherine is listed as **"Daughter"** of the head — she is the grandmother's daughter, married to [Cornelius O'Shea](../../../people/cornelius-oshea.md). Age 30 (born ~1871) is consistent with the 1911 census (age 40) and the tree export birth year of 1871. The Ancestry profile's alternate year of 1865 does not match either census return. Her occupation is **Housekeeper** — she is managing this household while her husband is absent.

### Cornelius O'Shea — absent

[Cornelius O'Shea](../../../people/cornelius-oshea.md) (I118) is not present in this household. On the night of 31 March 1901, he was either lodging elsewhere (foreshadowing his 1911 separation into 4 James Street, Cork City) or possibly still recorded nearby in a separate schedule. His 1901 census return remains an open question.

### Mary O'Shea (I13) — age 8, Scholar

**Mary O'Shea** (I13), born 19 February 1893 at Monkstown. Age 8 is correct — she turned 8 five weeks before census night. She is at school, consistent with the [Monkstown National School photograph (c. 1900)](../../../media/docs/Monkstown%20National%20School%20c1900%20including%20May%20O%27Shea.jpg) in which she appears aged about seven.

### Madeline O'Shea — age 4

**Madeline "Maddy" O'Shea** (b. 1897 per person page), age 3 or 4. Too young for school or occupation.

### Teresa Rose O'Shea — absent

**Teresa Rose O'Shea** (b. 1894), who would have been about 7, does not appear in this household. She appears in the 1911 census at age 16 as a Scholar, so she survived childhood. Her absence in 1901 might mean she was staying with another relative (possibly with Cornelius, or with paternal O'Shea relatives in Inchigeelagh).

### Catherine Enright's birth year

The 1911 census gives her age as 79 (born ~1832). The 1901 age of 69 (born ~1832) is perfectly consistent.
