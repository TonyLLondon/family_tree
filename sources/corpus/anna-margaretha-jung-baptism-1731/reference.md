## Significance

This baptism record documents the birth of **Anna Margaretha Jung** (I147, 1731-1807), who became the wife of **Hans Konrad Stump** and mother of [Hans Ulrich Stump](../../../people/hans-ulrich-stump.md).

### **Name Variations:**
The record shows "Anna Margreth Jung," consistent with the "Anna Margaretha Jung" found in later marriage and burial records. Both forms were common spelling variations in 18th-century Swiss German parish records.

### **Family Context:**
Born into a Jung family established in Sulgen, Thurgau. Her marriage to Hans Konrad Stump in 1766 united two established Swiss families in the same parish.
