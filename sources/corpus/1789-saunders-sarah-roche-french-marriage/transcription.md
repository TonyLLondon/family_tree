# Saunders's News-Letter — Marriage of Miss Roche to Francis French

**Date:** 17 August 1789
**Newspaper:** Saunders's News-Letter
**FMP:** [article link](https://www.findmypast.co.uk/search-newspapers/article/BL/0001057/17890817/006)

## Transcription (partial, from OCR)

> MARRIED — Francis French, of Portacarrow, in the county Galway, Esq. to Miss Roche, daughter **Stephen Roche John, Esq.**

## Notes

Confirms Burke's 1833 entry: "Sarah, m. to Francis French, esq. of Portcarran, in the county of Galway." The bride's father is identified as **Stephen Roche John, Esq.** — i.e. [Stephen John Roche](../../people/stephen-john-roche.md) (1724–1804), the direct ancestor's father.
