## Notes

### Timing

The marriage took place on 22 December 1815. Their first known child, [John Griffiths](../../people/david-griffiths-llywel.md), was christened at Llywel just 6 weeks later on 4 February 1816, strongly suggesting John was born before the marriage. This was a common pattern in rural Wales.

### Margaret's maiden name: DAVIES

This record confirms Margaret's maiden name as **Davies**. She was born ~1786 in Cayo (Cynwyl Gaeo), Carmarthenshire, according to the 1851 census. The Davies surname opens a research path into Carmarthenshire parish records for her parents.

### Church of England ceremony

David and Margaret married in the established Church of England parish of Llywel, even though they later baptised children at the **Saron Independent Chapel** (Congregationalist) in the same parish. This pattern — CofE marriage, Non-Conformist worship — was common in Wales before civil registration (1837).
