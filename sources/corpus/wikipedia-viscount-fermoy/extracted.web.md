# Viscount Fermoy

Appearance

**Viscount Fermoy** was a title in the Peerage of Ireland. The title had a number of creations. Holders of the title, including members of the Roche family of Blackwater Castle in Castletownroche in County Cork, included:[1][2]

- Maurice Roche (or De Rupe), sometimes known as "Maurice the mad",
[1]is recorded as the 1st (and sometimes 6th) Viscount Fermoy (c. 1550–1600) - David Roche, 2nd (or 7th) Viscount Fermoy (1573–1635)
[2][3] - Maurice Roche, 3rd (or 8th) Viscount Fermoy (1597–1670)
[2][4]
