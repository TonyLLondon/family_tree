## Research Methodology Success

This extension demonstrates the effectiveness of **iterative parent-searching via death records**:

1. **Death/burial records** consistently show parent names
2. **Civil registration** (post-1880) provides detailed family information
3. **Catholic church records** extend back to colonial period
4. **Baptism records** capture multi-generational family structure

The same methodology successfully extended **CERPA** and **PÉREZ** lines, confirming its reliability for Chilean genealogical research.
