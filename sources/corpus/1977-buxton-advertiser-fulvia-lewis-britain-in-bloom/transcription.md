# 1977 — Buxton Advertiser — "Blooming"

**Newspaper:** Buxton Advertiser, 7 July 1977, page 2
**Reference:** BL/0005178/19770707/018

## Extract (relevant passage)

> …other members of the business community have made an outstanding effort. A trophy given by Mrs. Fulvia Lewis — wife of "Keep Britain Tidy" Director David Lewis — will be presented to the "Britain in Bloom" finalist most active in litter prevention.

## Notes

Confirms the trophy was given by Mrs Fulvia Lewis and identifies David Lewis as Director of Keep Britain Tidy. By 1977 the trophy was an established annual award within the Britain in Bloom competition.
