## Notes

Margaret Griffiths was the eldest child of [Thomas Griffiths](../../people/thomas-griffiths.md) and [Anne Morgan](../../people/anne-morgan.md). She appeared on the [1861 census](../1861-census-griffiths-household-aberdare-rg09-4068/transcription.md) aged 6, a scholar, living at Clive Place, Cwmdare, Aberdare. She died at age 8 and was buried at the parish church of St Fagan's.

Her residence is given as **"Clive St, Mile St"** — confirming the family was still at the same Clive Street address.

The name Margaret was reused for a later daughter, **Margaret A Griffiths** (b. ~1866), who appears on the 1871 and 1881 censuses.

Two additional indexes confirm the same event:
- **National Burial Index:** `BMD/NBI/09123530` — Margaret Griffiths, 1863, Aberdare.
- **Glamorgan Burial Index:** `GBPRS/D/17234734/1` — Margaret Griffiths, 1863, Aberdare St Fagans.
