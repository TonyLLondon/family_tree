## Notes

### Identity

This is the household of **Alfred Evans**, father of [William Evans](../../people/william-evans-i289.md) (I289). William appears here as a four-year-old, the youngest of the children listed.

The children's birthplaces trace the family's movements: Jessie born in St Luke's (~1849), then Alfred Jr and Arthur born in Islington (1853, 1855), followed by William (1857). By 1861 the family had settled in the Anglers Gardens area of Islington East.

### Catherine's trade

Catherine is recorded as doing **Needle Work** — domestic sewing or piecework, a common occupation for wives in artisan households.
