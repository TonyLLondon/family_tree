## Significance

This is almost certainly the baptism of **Jean François Montagnon** (buried 15 Sep 1732 at Aubonne, recorded as born 1666). The one-year discrepancy between the burial age (giving 1666) and the baptism date (giving late 1667) is typical of age-at-death estimates in early modern Swiss Reformed registers.

This record establishes **Jean Montagnon** as the father of Jean François — pushing the Montagnon line back one further generation. A sister, Elisabet Montagnon, was baptised at the same parish on 7 Nov 1670 with the same father listed.
