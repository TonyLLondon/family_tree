## Index conflict — mother’s identity

The **1818 marriage** cluster for her daughter’s marriage to Ignacio (see **fs-6rq6-zsqw-ignacio-isabel-marriage-1818**) indexes the bride’s mother as **María Estrada**. This burial indexes **Maria Mora**. Until the two films are read side by side, treat this as an **index or extraction discrepancy**, not as proof of two different women.

**Compound-name possibility:** Other entries in the same marriage cluster use forms such as “Maria Isabel Mora Estrada,” which may reflect **Mora** and **Estrada** as distinct surname pieces; the burial indexer may have dropped “Estrada” or conflated lines.
