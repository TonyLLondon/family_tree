| Sarah | Glasscock | Wife | Married | F | 42 | — | Bethnal Green, Middlesex |

| **Eliza** | **Thorbourn** | **Daughter** | **Widow** | **F** | **22** | **Winder** | **Bethnal Green, Middlesex** |
| Elizabeth | Thorbourn | Daughter | — | F | 3 | — | Shoreditch, Middlesex |

Eliza is listed under her married name **Thorbourn** — she was already widowed at 22. Her daughter Elizabeth Thorbourn (3) was born at Shoreditch ~1848. Eliza's first husband (Mr. Thorbourn) died before 1851. She would marry [George Knecht](../../people/george-knecht.md) five years later.

Son James is the **James George Glasscock** baptised on the same day as Eliza (15 Jan 1832) at Bethnal Green. He was a cabinet maker — the same trade George Knecht would later practise.

James the father was a **painter**, born at Bethnal Green ~1792. He was baptised at Stepney on 4 Jan 1795, son of **John (Jno.) Glasscock** and **Sarah**.
