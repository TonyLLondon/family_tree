# Y.P. Societies Open New Session

## Carmel (Trecynon) tea

*Aberdare Leader*, 8 October 1949

---

CARMEL (Trecynon) Y.P.S. opened their winter session with a tea and social evening on Tuesday week, and took the advantage of the occasion to bid farewell and bon voyage to Mrs. Rachel M. Frost (here from Australia), who had been a worshipper at the church during her stay.

After an excellent meat tea, a miscellaneous concert was held under the presidency of Mr. T. Marchant Harris. He expressed the society's regret at the impending departure of Mrs. Frost, who during her visit had endeared herself to everyone. It was to be hoped that she would, in due course, make a return visit.

Responding, Mrs. Frost said she had been overwhelmed with the welcome and hospitality she had received during her stay. She would return cherishing the memories of the happy time she had spent among the very friendly people of "Sweet Berdar."

Mrs. E. R. Walters and Mr. Griffith Wilson also spoke.

Musical items, etc., were given as follows: solo **Master Keith Lewis** accompanied by **Mr Samuel Lewis**; Messrs. E. J. Williams and Mr. Llewelyn Jones; recitation, Mrs. Eirwen Richards; duet, Mrs. Hettie Phillips and Mrs. Annie Jones.

The society offered its congratulations to Mr. David Wilson on reaching his 80th birthday. Bardic verses for the occasion were composed and read by Mr. David Llewelyn Jones.

Mr. E. J. Williams also sang some verses of his own composition.

Responding, Mr. Wilson thanked the society for their good wishes. Accompanist was Mr. David Llewelyn Jones (who, incidentally, also played at the children's harvest festival on Sunday week).

At the tables during the evening were: Mrs. J. T. Davies and Mrs. H. Phillips, Mrs. M. E. Jones and Miss A. Powell, Miss P. Davies, Mrs. E. J. Williams and Mrs. — Jones; cutters: **Mrs. Samuel Lewis**, Mrs. E. A. Thomas, Mrs. Phoebe Evans, Mrs. L. George, Mrs. Griffith Wilson and Mrs. Janet Roberts; tea brewers: Mr. Edwin Davies and Mr. Llewelyn Jones. Several other members gave valuable assistance.

The officers for the session are: Chairman, Mrs. Annie Jones; secretary, Mr. Llewelyn Jones; treasurer, Miss Phoebe Davies.
