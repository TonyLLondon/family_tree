# Marriage register — Pietro Plarinò & Margherita Canova, 19 October 1732, Zadar

Marriage of **Sig.r Pietro Plarinò** to **Sig.ra Margherita**, daughter of the late **Giovanni Maria Canova**, in **Zara** on **19 October 1732**. Officiated by **Don Pietro Cortese**, with dispensation from the three customary banns by **Archbishop Vincenzo Zmajević** (by written authorization). The ceremony took place in the couple's home (*"in Casa di loro habitatione"*).

## Marriage contract vs church wedding

The parish-register **church wedding** was on 19 October 1732. A separate **notarial marriage contract** of the same couple, dated **14 February 1732**, is preserved as **DAZD HR-DAZD-342 sig. 45** — the oldest document in the Addobbati family fonds. The 8-month interval between contract and wedding is consistent with the period's customary financial and dotal arrangements made well in advance of the ceremony itself.
