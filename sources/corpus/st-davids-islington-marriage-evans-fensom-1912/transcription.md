### Ceremony and witnesses

> Married in the Parish Church according to the Rites and Ceremonies of the Established Church.

**Signatures:**

- Groom: **Alfred Evans**
- Bride: **Annie Fensom**
- Witness: **David Fensom** (bride's father)
- Witness: **Rose Elizabeth Maud Watts**

**Notes:**

- Both bride and groom shared the same address — 24 Wellington Road — suggesting they were already living together or in adjacent households before the wedding.
- Alfred was born **2 February 1893** (per the GEDCOM export and corroborated by the age of 19 in this register — he would not turn 20 until February 1913).
- David William Fensom witnessed his daughter's marriage; his trade as **plumber** adds to the picture of Islington working trades.
- William Evans's trade is recorded here as **horse dealer** — contrasting with the **brass trimmer / brass finisher** he gave on census returns.
