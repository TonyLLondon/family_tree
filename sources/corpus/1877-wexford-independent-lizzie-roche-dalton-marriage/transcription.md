# Marriage — D'Alton and Roche

*Wexford Independent*, 6 June 1877

---

June 2, at the **Church of St. Kevin**, **Dublin**, **Michael D'Alton**, Esq., Woodpark, County Clare, to **Lizzie Mary**, daughter of **William Roche**, Esq., **72, Harcourt-street**, Crown Solicitor for **Limerick**.
