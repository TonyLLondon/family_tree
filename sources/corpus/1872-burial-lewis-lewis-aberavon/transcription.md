# Burial of Lewis Lewis, Aberavon, 18 December 1872

**Register:** Burials in the Parish of Aberavon in the County of Glamorgan in the Year 1872  
**Reference:** P/68/CW/12, Page 228, No. 1820  
**Date of Burial:** 18 December 1872

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Name | Lewis Lewis |
| Abode | Port Talbot |
| When buried | December 18th |
| Age | 60 |
| By whom the Ceremony was performed | D. Parry, Curate |

Lewis Lewis was buried at Aberavon (St Mary) aged 60, having lived at Port Talbot. The GRO death index records him in Q4 1872, Neath district, Vol 11A p. 413, age 60 (birth year given as 1812). His baptism at Briton Ferry was 14 March 1813, so he was 59 turning 60.
