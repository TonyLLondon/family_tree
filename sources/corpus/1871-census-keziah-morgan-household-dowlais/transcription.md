**Mary Morgan** (17) is [David Cushen's](../../people/david-cushen.md) future wife — they married Q1 1876. This household is on **page 200** of RG 10/5397; Thomas Cushing's household (with 15-year-old David) is on **page 201**. The two families lived next door to each other on Pond Street, Dowlais. David married the girl from the neighbouring house.

Keziah Morgan (b. ~1827) came from **Blaina, Monmouthshire** — a coal and ironworks town in the Western Valleys. His wife Jane (b. ~1828) was from Dowlais. Six children, five daughters and one son.
