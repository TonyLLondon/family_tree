# Marriage register — Pietro Paolo Addobbati & Gerarda Rovero Brizzi, 26 October 1845, Zadar

Marriage in the **Collegiate Parish Church of S. Simeone (Sv. Šime)**, Zara, on **26 October 1845**, officiated by **D. Dom. Morovich, Amministratore**. Banns published **13, 20, and 27 July 1845**. The bride, just twenty years old, was below the Austrian civil majority of twenty-four; **Praetor's consent was granted 14 July 1845**, two days after her twentieth birthday.
