# Marriage register — Francesco Lanfranchi & Francesca Doriceo, 4 June 1707, Zadar

Marriage on **4 June 1707** in the Metropolitan parish of **Sv. Stošija**, Zara. The Curate **Don Giovanni Battista Cardini** officiated under licence of **Archbishop Vittorio Priuli**. **Three banns published, no impediment**.

## Doriceo name puzzle

The 1715 baptism of the couple's daughter Margherita names the mother as **Margaritta Doriceo** — whereas this 1707 marriage names her as **Francesca Doriceo**, daughter of the late Giovanni Doriceo. The two possibilities:

1. **Same person, double name.** "Francesca Margaritta" or "Margaritta Francesca" was a common Venetian-Italian double-name pattern; either name could be used in different official records.
2. **Two different Doriceo women.** Possibly sisters; Francesco might have remarried between 1707 and 1715.

The first possibility is more likely given (a) the absence of a recorded second marriage of Francesco between 1707 and 1715, and (b) the daughter Santa Lanfranchi's 1707 baptism (six months after this marriage) naming a mother whose surname reading is uncertain — see `research/image-based-corrections.md` §28 and the [Margherita Lanfranchi](../../people/margherita-lanfranchi.md) page.
