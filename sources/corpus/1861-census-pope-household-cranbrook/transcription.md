## Notes

- **George Pope** (I296) is again a **master shoemaker** — consistently self-employed across both the 1861 and 1871 censuses. Age 36 gives birth year ~1825.
- **Sarah Ann Pope** (I297, née **Quaife**, per GRO marriage index Q4 1847) — her full name is now confirmed as **Sarah Ann**, the "A." in the 1871 census standing for Ann. Age 32 gives birth year ~1829, one year later than the 1871 census estimate (~1828). Both are consistent with a birth around 1828–1829.
- **Ellen M Pope** (~1848) — an older daughter not present in the 1871 census. By 1871 she would be ~23 and had likely married and left home. She is the eldest known child, born about 6 years before George H. This indicates George and Sarah Ann married around 1847 or earlier.
- **George H Pope** (~1854) and **Frank W Pope** (~1859) — middle initials H and W now recorded. Both match the 1871 census.
- Arthur (~1864), Minnie (~1865), and Gertrude (~1868) have not yet been born.
- All five family members born in Cranbrook — consistent with the deeply rooted local family seen in 1871.
