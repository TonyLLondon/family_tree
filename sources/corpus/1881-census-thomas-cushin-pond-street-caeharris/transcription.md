**Address: 8 Pond Street, Caeharris.** The same Pond Street where [David Cushen](../../people/david-cushen.md) later lived at no. 15 (1901) and widow Mary at no. 25 (1911). The Cushens were on Pond Street for at least 30 years.

Thomas Sr (60, widower) is explicitly **Cork, Cork, Ireland**. Mary Collins (33, Abersychan) is his eldest daughter Mary — born at Abersychan ~1847, she married a Collins and had son James in Middlesbrough (~1871). She is listed as "Other" rather than "Daughter" because the household head is Thomas Jr.

Next door at **7 Pond Street** (schedule 122): William Lewis (63, Miner, Glamorgan) with wife Ellen (62, Glamorgan) and son John Lewis (21, Miner).
