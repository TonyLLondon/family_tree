# Marriage register — Luigi Addobbati & Maddalena Plarinò, 4 April 1766, Zadar

Marriage on **4 April 1766** in the parish of **Sv. Stošija**, Zara, **in the bride's house** with dispensation from banns. Officiant: **Don Simeone Tatovich**. Both witnesses are physicians of Zara — **Dr. Giacomo Danieli** and **Dr. Antonio Danieli** — a marker of the social standing the household carried in the city.
