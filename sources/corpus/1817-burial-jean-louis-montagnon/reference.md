**Correction:** This burial is for a *different* Jean Louis Montagnon than our direct ancestor. This Jean Louis (b. 1743, son of Jean Michel Montagnon + Catherine Vougaz) married **Jeanne Françoise Vougaz** (daughter of Isac Vougaz + Susanne Gaillard) on 7 April 1780 ([6NQG-NVVV](https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:6NQG-NVVV)).

Our ancestor is **Jean François Louis Montagnon** (b. ~1737, son of **Jean Pierre Montagnon** + **Jeanne Cusin**), who married **Jaqueline Suzanne Chenevard** in October 1761 at Céligny and was buried **4 February 1786** at Aubonne ([6NQL-3F71](https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:6NQL-3F71)). The two were contemporaries at Aubonne but are distinct individuals. The confusion arises because both share the name "Jean Louis Montagnon" and both lived in Aubonne.
