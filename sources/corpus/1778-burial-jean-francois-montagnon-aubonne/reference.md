## Significance

This is a **different** Jean François Montagnon from our direct ancestor Jean François (b. 1666, d. 1732). Born 1694 and living to age 84, he was likely a son of Jean François b. 1666 × Jeane Marechal — making him a brother of Jean Pierre (b. 1692) and Jean Michel (b. 1699). All three were born within the right timeframe and all lived at Aubonne.

This record is not in the direct ancestral line but is important context: it shows the household of Jean François (b. 1666) produced multiple sons who remained at Aubonne, strengthening the case that Jean Pierre was also his son.
