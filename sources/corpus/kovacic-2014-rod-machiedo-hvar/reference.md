## Lauro Machiedo × Magdalena Novak

- **Magdalena** “Mandina” Novak “Bonaparte,” b. 1855, d. Zadar/Arbanasi 1920.
- Children named: **Jerko**, **Juraj-Zorko**, **Ružica**, **Kreuza**.

## Juraj-Zorko Machiedo × Graziella Nakic d’Osljak

- **Juraj-Zorko** 1879 – Zagreb 1963 — mining engineer (studied Graz; worked for Dalmatian government, then private).
- **Graziella Nakic d’Osljak** — Zadar 1883 – Trieste 1964; family described as Italian-leaning; *predikat* from the estate / island **Ošljak** (off Preko opposite Zadar).
- **Children (Kovačić):**
  - **Maksim** — Zadar 1907 – Florence 1917.
  - **Sergije** / Sergio / Serđo — Zadar 1910 – Belgrade 1991; law doctorate; partisan; post-war diplomat and UN; spouse Ljubica Mandić; daughters Olga, Vera (details in article).
  - **Tatjana-Tanja** — Zadar 1912; **later Zerauschek** (spelling in article).
  - **Isabella “Cinci”** (Činči) — Zadar 1915; later **Capanna**; both daughters said to have died in Italy with descendants.

## Dr. Jerko Machiedo (1877–1962)

Article gives **24 November 1877 – 10 February 1962**, medicine Graz 1901, municipal physician Hvar, Dalmatian Diet, deputy president of Dalmatian provincial government Zadar 1910, later Šibenik hospital, return to Hvar, wartime treating partisans — full narrative continues for many pages after the Juraj-Zorko block.
