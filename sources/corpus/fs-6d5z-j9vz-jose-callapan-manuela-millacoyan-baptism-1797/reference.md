## Indigenous Surname Analysis

### José CALLAPAN
**Mapudungun Etymology**:
- **Calla** = "valley, clearing, open space"
- **Pan** = locative suffix "place of/belonging to"  
- **Meaning**: "Man from the valley clearing"

**Record Distribution**: 255+ entries in Chilean church records (1724-1897)  
**Geographic Concentration**: Talca, Maule, Santiago regions - **core Picunche territory**

### Manuela MILLACOYAN  
**Mapudungun Etymology**:
- **Milla** = "gold/precious metal" OR "forest clearing/tree"
- **Coyan** = "gathering place/meeting ground"
- **Meaning**: "Woman from the precious gathering place" OR "Woman from the forest meeting place"

**Cultural Significance**: **Pure Mapuche compound surname** following traditional indigenous naming patterns linking individuals to ancestral territories and ceremonial sites.
