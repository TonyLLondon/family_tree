**Address: 31 Pond Street, Caeharris.** David's father Thomas Sr was at no. 8 (schedule 121, same census). Next door at **32 Pond Street** (schedule 97): John Morgans (47, coal miner) — likely a relative of Mary's father Keziah Morgan.

David (25) is a **mason** — the same trade as his maternal grandfather **Evan Mainwaring** of Swansea. Four children by 1881; the family later expanded to nine (1891 census). "Ezekiel" is the same child as "Hezekiah" on later censuses — variant forms of the Hebrew name.
