# Death Registration — Laureano Cerpa Orellana, 15 April 1895, Chanco

FamilySearch indexed record from the Chilean civil registration. Laureano was born c. 1815 and died in Chanco aged roughly 80. His parents — Francisco Cerpa and Pastora Orellana — represent an earlier generation of the Cerpa family in Chanco, predating the Francisco Cerpa who married Bersabé Villegas and later Ana González. This earlier Francisco Cerpa may be the great-grandfather (or great-great-grandfather) of Miguel Luis Cerpa González.

The name Francisco Cerpa recurs across at least two generations in Chanco civil records. This Laureano's father Francisco (active c. 1810s) is distinct from the Francisco Cerpa who fathered Abraham (1890) and married Bersabé Villegas, though both were registered in the same commune.
