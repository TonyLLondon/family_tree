**Correction:** Jean Michel Montagnon is the father of a *different* Jean Louis Montagnon (b. 1743, d. 1817) who married Jeanne Françoise Vougaz in 1780 ([6NQG-NVVV](https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:6NQG-NVVV)), not our direct ancestor. Our ancestor Jean François Louis Montagnon (b. ~1737, d. 1786) was the son of **Jean Pierre Montagnon** (b. 1692, d. 1737) + **Jeanne Cusin**, confirmed by family register entries at Céligny ([6NQL-FNTW](https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:6NQL-FNTW) et al.).

Jean Michel and Jean Pierre Montagnon were likely brothers or cousins in the Aubonne Montagnon clan, but Jean Michel is not a direct ancestor in this tree.
