This is [Thomas Griffiths](../../people/thomas-griffiths.md) (I422), who later migrated to the Aberdare coalfield and married [Anne Morgan](../../people/anne-morgan.md). Every subsequent census gives his birthplace as "Trecastle, Breconshire" or "Trecastle, Brecknockshire" — Trecastle is the village within Llywel parish.

A FamilySearch index entry (`R_733089160`, Wales Births & Baptisms 1541–1907, batch C08987-1) independently confirms: father **David Griffiths**, mother **Margaret**, baptism place **Savon Independent, Llywel, Brecon**.

---

## Notes

The surname is spelled **Griffith** (without S) in the 1828 entry and **Griffiths** in the 1830 entry. Both are common renderings of the same patronymic surname in Breconshire.

The register names the mother only as "Margaret" — no maiden name is given in Non-Conformist registers of this period. A Church of England marriage record for Llywel parish might supply it.
