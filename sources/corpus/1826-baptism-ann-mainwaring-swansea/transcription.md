[Ann Mainwaring](../../people/ann-mainwaring.md) married [Thomas Cushen](../../people/thomas-cushen.md) ~1847. Her father **Evan Mainwaring** was a **mason** — the same trade her son [David Cushen](../../people/david-cushen.md) later practised at Dowlais. The masonry craft crossed three generations: Evan → (Ann) → David.
