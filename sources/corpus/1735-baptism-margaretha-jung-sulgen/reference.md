# Baptism — Margreth Jung, 24 July 1735, Sulgen

FamilySearch indexed record from the Swiss baptism registers. This is the baptism of Anna Margaretha Jung, wife of Hans Konrad Stump (I146) and Marc François Stump's paternal great-grandmother.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Margreth Jung |
| **Christened** | 24 July 1735 |
| **Place** | Sulgen, Thurgau, Switzerland |
| **Father** | Hanss Jacob Jung (Hans Jacob Jung) |
| **Mother** | Maria Keller |

This record identifies the wife of Hans Jacob Jung (I150) as **Maria Keller** — previously unknown from the GEDCOM, which had I150 as a single-parent stub in union F75.

A duplicate record exists: [68VP-D2B1](https://www.familysearch.org/ark:/61903/1:1:68VP-D2B1) (collection 4138674, "Hss Jacob Jung" + "Maria Keller").

Anna Margaretha Jung first married Hans Ulrich Altwigg (children in Sulgen 1758–1760), then married Hans Konrad Stump on 19 June 1766 (children 1767–1770+). She was buried 18 December 1807 at Sulgen — the same day as her son Hans Ulrich Stump.
