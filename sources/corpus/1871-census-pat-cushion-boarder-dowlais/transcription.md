Patrick Cushen (60) is now a boarder in his son-in-law **Pat Welsh/Walsh's** household. Ann Welsh (21, Monmouthshire) is Patrick's daughter Ann (b. ~1851, Trevethin) — she has married young Pat Walsh. Their first child Mary Ann (b. ~1871) is an infant. Catherine (17) is still with her father. By 1881, this household becomes the **Royal Oak Inn** at 24 George Street, Penydarren, with Patrick (now 70, overman) still present as father-in-law.
