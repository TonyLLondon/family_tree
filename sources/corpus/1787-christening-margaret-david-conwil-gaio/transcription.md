## Identification

This is the **probable** baptism of Margaret Davies (I655), who married David Griffiths of Llywel on 22 December 1815.

Evidence for the identification:

- **Parish match:** Conwil-gaio is an alternate rendering of Cynwyl Gaeo / Caio / Cayo — exactly the birthplace Margaret gave on the 1851 census ("Cayo, Carmarthenshire").
- **Date match:** Birth ~1787 is within 1 year of the census-derived estimate of ~1786 (age 65 in 1851). Normal census-age discrepancy.
- **Name match:** "Margaret David" is the Welsh patronymic form of "Margaret daughter of David" = Margaret Davies. The fixed surname "Davies" crystallised from this patronymic.
- **Uniqueness:** She is the only Margaret David/Davies christened at Conwil-gaio parish in the indexed records for the 1780s–1790s window.

**Father:** "David Conwil" — the element "Conwil" is a locative identifier (of Cynwyl), not a fixed surname. In the emerging fixed-surname system, this man would be **David Davies** (son of some earlier David). Mother's name is not given in the index.

**Status:** Probable but not independently confirmed. Confirmation would require the original Cynwyl Gaeo parish register entry (not currently available online) or a corroborating record linking this Margaret to David Griffiths of Llywel.
