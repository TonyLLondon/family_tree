# Baptism — Jaqueline Susanne Chenevard, 26 October 1732

FamilySearch indexed record from the Reformed church registers of Céligny, Geneva. This is the baptism record of Susanne Chenevard (née Chenevard), who married Jean François Louis Montagnon. She is Marc François Stump's maternal great-great-grandmother.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | Jaqueline Susanne Chenevard |
| **Baptised** | 26 October 1732 |
| **Place** | Céligny, Geneva, Switzerland |
| **Father** | Benjamin Chenevard |
| **Mother** | Christiene Narphin (Christine Nerfin) |

Céligny is a Geneva-canton enclave within Vaud, on the north shore of Lac Léman between Nyon and Coppet. The Chenevard family had roots in Jorat-Mézières (Vaud) and Céligny (Geneva). Susanne's name appears in later records with multiple spellings: Cheneveau, Chenevard, Chanevaz.
