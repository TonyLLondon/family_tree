# Savushun — English translation excerpt (Dr. Schtomp / false teeth)

Facial-pages **366–367** from the **Ghanoonparvar** English translation of *Savushun* (headers on scan: «366 Savushun» / «Simin Daneshvar 367»). **Zari** taunts **Khan Kaka** (Abolqasem Khan) by listing his self-interested plans, including ordering **false teeth from Dr. Schtomp**.

The Persian original renders the proverbial dentist under spellings such as **اشتمپ** / **اشتومپ**; this translation uses **“Dr. Schtomp.”**

## Evidence line (citable)

Quoted in [transcription.en.md](./transcription.en.md):
