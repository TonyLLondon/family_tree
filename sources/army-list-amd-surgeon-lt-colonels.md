## Entry for William O'Byrne White

> White, William O'Byrne (hon. Brig.-Surg.) (Tralee) — First Commission: **31 Mar 1865** — Date of Retirement: **25 Apr 1885**

- Qualifications (from half-pay register): **LRCP Ed., LRCSI**
- Post-retirement title: Honorary Brigade-Surgeon (= honorary Lieutenant-Colonel grade)
- Residence at time of listing: **Tralee** (County Kerry, Ireland)

## Contextual note

The list is populated with many Irish names — O'Brien, O'Reilly, MacSwiney, McGann, Scanlan, Morgan — consistent with the disproportionate Irish Catholic share of AMD officers documented in secondary literature. Henry Joseph O'Brien (Cork), who also entered in 1865, appears on the same page with an identical retirement rank.
