# 1921 Census — Coolbear household, Southend-on-Sea

**Archibald Percy Coolbear** (29, chimney sweep / carpet beater / chair caner, self-employed) and **Rosina** née Martin (30, born Orpington, Kent) with four children at Southend-on-Sea, Essex. Also present: Rosina's brother Edward Martin (31, stoker) and three visitors. Archibald signed the form himself.
