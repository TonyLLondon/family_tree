# 1871 Census — Aberavon, Lewis Lewis household

Census of England & Wales, 2 April 1871. **Lewis Lewis** (58, Porter, born Briton Ferry) as head at Aberavon with wife **Rachel** (41, born Carmarthen, Carmarthenshire) and nine children, all born at Aberavon. The eldest is **David Lewis** (13, Scholar) — [David John Lewis (1857)](../people/david-john-lewis-1857.md), baptised at Aberavon the same year.

Lewis Lewis's occupation as Porter places the family in the coastal economy around Port Talbot and Briton Ferry, not the inland coalfield. David's later migration to Aberdare represents a generational shift from dockside/railway labour to deep mining.

**Reference:** RG 10/5424, schedule 460, Enumeration District 8, Sub-district Margam.
