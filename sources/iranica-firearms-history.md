## Key passages

### Georgian artillerymen (early Qajar)

> "Around 1800 the artillery corps consisted of some 840 men and most of the cannoneers were **Georgians**."

### Tehran arsenal founded 1838

> "The defeat at Herat in 1838 prompted Moḥammad Shah's grand vizier to engage in military reform which resulted in the establishment of the **arsenal of Tehran**."

### French specialists 1858

> "A contract of 1858 again included the sending of **French military specialists**. The newly established Dar al-fonūn had a department of military sciences, where artillery skills were taught."

### Late 19th century — Austrian reform

> "Austrians… attempting to reorganize the arsenal from an institution run for private gain into a professional one."
