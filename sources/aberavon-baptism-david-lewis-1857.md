# Baptism of David Lewis — Aberavon, 1857

FamilySearch index entry (Wales, Births and Baptisms, 1541-1907, batch C02533-2). **David Lewis** christened **11 August 1857** at **Aberavon** (Aberafon), Glamorgan. Father: **Lewis Lewis**. Mother: **Rachael** (maiden name not given in index; confirmed as **Stephen** from the Llanarthne baptism register and GRO MarriageFinder).

No register image is available on FamilySearch. The indexed data independently confirms [Lewis Lewis](../people/lewis-lewis.md) as David's father, matching the [1871 census](1871-census-aberavon-lewis-lewis.md) which places the family at Aberavon.

The baptism location — Aberavon, on the coast near Port Talbot — establishes that David was born there, not at Aberdare as he later reported on census returns.
