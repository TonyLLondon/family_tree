# 1921 Census — Evans household, 4 Bratton Street, Islington

Census schedule for the household of **[Alfred Evans](../people/alfred-evans-i101.md)** and **[Annie Evans née Fensom](../people/annie-fensom-i291.md)** at **4 Bratton Street, Islington**, enumerated **19 June 1921**. Five persons: Alfred (head, 28, costermonger, own account), Annie (wife, 28, brush maker, Star Brush Company), and three children — **Alfred Charles** (8), **[Joseph David](../people/joseph-evans-i99.md)** (6), and **Annie** (4).
