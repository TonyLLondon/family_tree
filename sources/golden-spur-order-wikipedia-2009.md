# Order of the Golden Spur — Wikipedia compilation (2009)

Fifteen-page PDF (Word export, dated 9 Dec 2009) compiling Italian-language Wikipedia and web sources on the **Ordine della Milizia Aurata o dello Speron d'Oro**. Includes the full Italian Wikipedia article, entries from the Corpo della Nobiltà Italiana website, Giorgio Aldrighetti's overview, and a Google Books bibliography of historical references. Collected as family research background for the Addobbati **Knight of the Golden Spur** honour (1786).
