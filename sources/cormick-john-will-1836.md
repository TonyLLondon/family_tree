# Will of Dr John Cormick (1836)

**Court:** Prerogative Court of Canterbury  
**Reference:** PROB 11/1865/205  
**Date proved:** 6 August 1836  
**Testator:** Dr John Cormick, late Surgeon on the Madras Establishment at Mearneh of Khorasan, Persia

Dr John Cormick was physician to Crown Prince Abbas Mirza at Tabriz. He was a colleague and predecessor of Edward Burgess in the British consular community at Tabriz. His will was proved in London six months after his death in Persia.

The will is handwritten in formal legal script and extremely difficult to read. Partial transcription and high-resolution images are in the corpus bundle. Key names visible include executors/witnesses **James Cooper** and **Becher**.

**Digital access:** [TNA Discovery catalogue record](https://discovery.nationalarchives.gov.uk/details/r/D282237) (image viewer available online)
