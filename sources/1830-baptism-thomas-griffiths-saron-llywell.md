# Non-Conformist Baptisms — Thomas Griffiths, Saron Independent, Llywell (1828 & 1830)

Two entries in the birth-and-baptism register of **Saron Independent Chapel**, Llywell, Breconshire (TNA RG 4/1725): a Thomas Griffith born 7 July 1828 (presumably died in infancy) and [Thomas Griffiths](../people/thomas-griffiths.md) born **16 June 1830**, baptised 18 July 1830. Father: [David Griffiths](../people/david-griffiths-llywel.md); Mother: [Margaret](../people/margaret-griffiths-mother.md).
