# Maeda 2019 — Lives of the Enikolopians

Scholarly chapter tracing the Enikolopian/Enikolopiant family across Georgia and Qajar Iran. Chapter 6 of the Brill volume *The Persianate World: Rethinking a Shared Sphere* (eds. Amanat & Ashraf). Not open access; held in hard copy by Tony.

## Key evidence for Saginian line

Per the Wikipedia article on Solayman Khan Saham al-Dowleh (which cites this chapter at pp. 189–195):

- **David Saginean and Zaal Saginean** are identified as Georgian brothers who fled Russia with Solayman Khan in early 1811, motivated by fear of forced relocation and religious conversion.
- They traveled Tbilisi → Yerevan → Akhalkalaki → Gyumri → **Tabriz**, where they joined the army of Crown Prince **Abbas Mirza**.
- David Saginean = **Daoud Khan Saginian**, later head of the Isfahan army under the Qajars.
