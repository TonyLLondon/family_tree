# Luxardo — Company history (luxardo.it)

**Kind:** web
**Corpus:** [luxardo-history](corpus/luxardo-history/)
**URL:** <https://www.luxardo.it/history/>

Official company history of the Luxardo maraschino distillery, founded 1821 in Zara (Zadar) by Girolamo Luxardo, consular representative of the Kingdom of Sardinia. Covers: 1817 arrival in Zara; Maria Canevari's perfection of rosolio maraschino; 1821 founding and Austrian imperial "Privilege"; 1913 Michelangelo Luxardo's harbour-front distillery (largest in the Austro-Hungarian Empire); post-WWI Zara as Italian territory and Luxardo as Italy's most important distillery; WWII bombing destruction; murder of Nicolò II and Bianca Luxardo by Yugoslav partisans; 1947 refounding in Torreglia (Padova).

**People:** [Antonio Zerauschek](../people/antonio-zerauschek.md) (contemporary in interwar Zara; Ausonia chocolate factory, possible Luxardo supplier).
