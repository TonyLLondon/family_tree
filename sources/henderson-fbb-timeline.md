# Henderson — FBB Timeline (1854–1938)

Chronological life outline for **Fanny Burgess Bottin** compiled from family papers by the Henderson branch. Single-sheet Excel workbook with year + event entries from Fanny's birth in Tehran (9 Jun 1854) through her death there in November 1938.

## Key facts sourced from this document

- Birth date: **9 June 1854**, Tehran.
- Marriage: **19 April 1884** to Julien Bottin, Tehran.
- Five children born Tehran: Henriette (1888), Alice (1889), Edward (1891), René (1892), George (1895).
- 1862–65 in **Urmia** with American Nestorian Missionaries.
- 1880 England trip (Anna's Leicester interview); 1881 return via Bedford.
- 1901–03 Shepherd's Bush, London (matches 1901 census).
- 1906 Paris / Melun.
- Divorced from Julien by **1919**.
- Last letter from Tehran **1929**.
