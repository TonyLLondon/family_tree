# Calypso cigarette packet database (zigsam.at)

Austrian cigarette packet collector's database entry showing a surviving **Calypso** hinged-box pack, c. 1940, producer **Manifattura Zaratina Sigarette S.A.** (Croatia / Italian Zara). Later unrelated Calypso packs by Pritchard & Burton (Switzerland, 1960s) and Philip Morris (USA/Germany, 1970s) are also listed.
