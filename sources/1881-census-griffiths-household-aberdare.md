# 1881 Census — 11 Clive Street, Aberdare (Griffiths household)

Enumerator's book for the 1881 Census of England & Wales, **3 April 1881**. Ten persons at **11 Clive Street, Aberdare**: [Thomas Griffiths](../people/thomas-griffiths.md) as head, now a colliery labourer; wife [Ann](../people/anne-morgan.md) (52); daughters Eliza (21, tinworker), [Catherine](../people/catherine-griffiths.md) (18, tin worker), Margaret A (15, tin worker); son David (12, scholar). Extended kin: nephew David Morgan (16, coal miner), niece Margaret Griffiths (16, brick worker), mother-in-law **Sarah Morgan** (75, widow, born **Builth**, Brecknockshire), cousin John Morgan (34, coal miner, born Llanfair, Carmarthenshire).
