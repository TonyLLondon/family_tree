# Kovačić (2014) — *Rod Machiedo sa Hvara*

## Summary

Archival and press-based genealogy of the **Machiedo** family of **Hvar** and their role in Dalmatian public life from the 19th century into the mid‑20th. Core published pedigree for **[Juraj-Zorko Machiedo](../people/juraj-zorko-machiedo.md)** × **[Graziella Nakic d’Osljak](../people/graziella-nakic-d-osljak.md)** and their children, including **[Tatiana Machiedo](../people/tatiana-machiedo.md)** (*Tatjana-Tanja*, later Zerauschek) and **[Sergije Makiedo](../people/sergije-makiedo.md)**; situates **Dr. Jerko Machiedo** as Juraj-Zorko’s brother (Tatiana’s paternal uncle).
