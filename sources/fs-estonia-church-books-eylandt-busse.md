# Estonia, Church Books — Eylandt / Busse / Erbe / Stump records

**Collection:** [Estonia, Church Books and Synagogue Registers, 1590–1941](https://www.familysearch.org/search/collection/1932350) (FamilySearch collection 1932350).

Digitised Estonian Lutheran parish registers indexed by FamilySearch. Searched **3 April 2026** for the Eylandt / Eyland / Eiland, Busse, Erbe, and Stump surnames. Yielded indexed entries for seven generations of the continuous Olga Erbe → Eylandt → Busse → Bohm direct line, plus the Erbe and Stump families in Tallinn.
