# Estonia, Church Books -- Eylandt / Busse / Erbe / Stump records

**Collection:** Estonia, Church Books and Synagogue Registers, 1590-1941 (FamilySearch collection 1932350).

Digitised Estonian Lutheran parish registers indexed by FamilySearch. Searched **3 April 2026** for the Eylandt / Eyland / Eiland, Busse, Erbe, Heimberger, and Stump surnames. Yielded indexed entries for seven generations of the continuous Olga Erbe -> Eylandt -> Busse -> Bohm direct line, plus the Erbe-Heimberger marriage and children, and the Stump families in Tallinn.
