# 1881 Census — Fensom household, 8 Milton Place, Islington

The full Fensom family at 8 Milton Place, Lower Holloway: **David Fensom** (46, house decorator, born Luton, Hertfordshire) with wife **Georgiana** (45) and five children — Georgiana E (19, general labourer, unemployed), David W (16, plumber), Annie (11), Ellen (6), and Fanny (4). David William was already plumbing at sixteen.
