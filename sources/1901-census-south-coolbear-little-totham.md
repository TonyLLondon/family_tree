# 1901 Census — South/Coolbear household, Little Totham

**Reuben South** (48, farm dayman, born Tolleshunt Knights) and **Alma** née Newcomb (47, born Tiptree) — Alma's second marriage after George Gridley Coolbear's death in 1892. Three Coolbear step-sons remain: Harold (19, farm worker), Ernest (11), and **Archibald** (9). Reuben and Alma have a son together: Henry South (6, born Tollesbury).
