# 1861 Census — Clive Place, Aberdare (Griffiths household)

Enumerator's book for the 1861 Census of England & Wales, **7 April 1861**. Five persons at **Clive Place, Cwmdare, Aberdare**: [Thomas Griffiths](../people/thomas-griffiths.md) as head and ironworks labourer; wife [Ann](../people/anne-morgan.md); two daughters — Margaret (6, scholar) and Elizabeth (1); plus a boarder Thomas Thomas (29, blocklayer, born Llywel). Thomas's birthplace is given as **Llywel**, matching his baptismal record.
