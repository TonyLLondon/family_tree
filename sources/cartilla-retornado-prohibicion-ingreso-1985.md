# Cartilla del Retornado Nº 3 — Nómina de Personas con Prohibición de Ingreso (1985)

Published November 1985 by the **Comité Pro-Retorno de Exiliados** with the **Vicaría de la Solidaridad**. Contains the "Sexto listado" (6th edition, dated 15 July 1985) of Chilean citizens banned from re-entering Chile under the Pinochet military government, listing 4,360 names compiled by the Policía de Investigaciones de Chile (Director General Fernando Paredes Pizarro). A 7th list update (17 September 1985) reduced the figure to 3,878.

**Helia del Carmen Cerpa Pérez** appears as **"Cerpa Pérez, Helia del Carmen"** on page 12 of the booklet, confirming she was formally prohibited from returning to Chile as of July 1985 — twelve years after her detention and exile.
