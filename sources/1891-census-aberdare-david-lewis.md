# 1891 Census — Glan Road, Aberdare (David Lewis household)

Scan of the enumerator's book page from the **1891 Census of England & Wales**, taken **5 April 1891**. Seven persons at **Glan Road, Aberdare**: [David Lewis (1857)](../people/david-john-lewis-1857.md) as head and coal miner; wife [Catherine](../people/catherine-griffiths.md); three young children — [Mary Ann](../people/mary-ann-lewis.md) (5, scholar), [Samuel](../people/samuel-lewis.md) (3), and infant Thomas; plus two Gillias boarders from Hereford, both labourers underground.

This is the **first census** after the 1884 marriage and the earliest snapshot of the household. **Thomas Lewis** (I336, b. 1891) appears as a newborn — the only census on which he is recorded. He died before the 1901 census.
