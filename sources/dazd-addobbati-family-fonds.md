# DAZD HR-DAZD-342 — Obitelj Addobbati (Family Fonds)

**Archive:** Državni arhiv u Zadru (DAZD), Zadar, Croatia.

**Fonds reference:** HR-DAZD-342 *Obitelj Addobbati* · 1732–1930 · 1 box (10 cm) · 86 items · 7 series.

**Remote inventory PDF:** [dazd.hr — HR-DAZD-342 Addobbati](https://www.dazd.hr/attachment/preview/5c657cc51c881/dazd-342-addobbati.pdf)

The full Croatian-language inventory (with an English-side companion) and all sixteen page facsimiles of the official DAZD finding aid are reproduced below.

## Fonds summary

The DAZD Addobbati family fonds is the single most important archival collection for this family. Languages: Italian and Latin. **Not digitised** as of inventory date; physical access at DAZD required.

### Series breakdown

| Series | Signatures | Content |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| I. Identity documents | sig. 1–5 | Baptism certificates, documents of the imperial-royal administration |
| II. Genealogy | sig. 6–25 | Genealogical documents including the **1745 Bergamo testimonial** (sig. 41 in the analytical list), genealogical tables, nobility documentation, the **1786 Golden Spur diploma** |
| III. Property documents | sig. 26–44 | Deeds, land holdings, property transactions |
| IV. Legal documents | sig. 45–60 | Court records, correspondence on property and inheritance |
| V. Correspondence | sig. 61–72 | Personal and official correspondence |
| VI. Financial documents | sig. 73–81 | Accounts, receipts |
| VII. Miscellaneous | sig. 82–86 | Obituary June 1926, printed matter, miscellaneous |

### Key documents

- **sig. 19** — "The magnificent Nin municipality confirms all titles, rights and privileges to brothers Pietro and Vincenzo Addobbati and their successors" (1 July 1804) — formal Nin noble-council confirmation.
- **1745 Bergamo testimonial** — attestation by the Bergamo magistracy of six-generation lineage from Pietro de Adobatis (1495), coat of arms, and *cives originarios* status.
- **1786 Golden Spur diploma** — papal knighthood and lateran count dignity conferred on Dr. Petar Addobbati by Bishop Ivan Dominik Stratico, 16 October 1786.

## Use in this research

Primary archive for all phases of the Addobbati story from the 1733 Zara arrival through to the Habsburg period. Documents the Bergamo origins, Nin noble-council status, legal practice, and the 1920s end of the main family line in Zara.
