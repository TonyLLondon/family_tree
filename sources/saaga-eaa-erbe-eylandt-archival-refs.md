# Estonian National Archives (Saaga) — Erbe / Eylandt archival references

**Repository:** [Estonian National Archives / Rahvusarhiiv — Saaga viewer](https://www.ra.ee/dgs/explorer.php)

Specific archival shelf marks cited by Geni profile managers **Elle Kiiker** and **Andrey Masing** on the Erbe / Eylandt family profiles. Each reference points to a digitised page in the Saaga viewer with highlight coordinates identifying the relevant entry on the scan. Images are publicly accessible but the JavaScript viewer cannot be scraped.

## Key records

- **Hermann Eberhard Erbe** birth/baptism, Rakvere, 3 Sep 1814 — `EAA.3057.2.3:290` (parents: Georg Sigismund × Caroline Sophie)
- **Hermann × Emilie** marriage, Tallinn, 27 Mar 1839 — `TLA.237.1.34:5` / `TLA.237.2.6:32`
- **Hermann** Personalbuch, Dom parish 1864–1901 — `TLA.237.2.9:25`
- **Emilie Ida Eylandt** birth/baptism, Tallinn, 20 Mar 1812 — `TLA.31.1.34:171` (parents: Johann Jakob × Marie Elisabeth)
- **Johann Jakob Eylandt** in 1795 Kirbla family list — `EAA.1864.2.V-67:227` (listed under father Carl Gustav Eyland)
- **Johann Jakob × Maria Elisabeth** marriage, Viljandi, 27 Dec 1799 — `EAA.3149.2.7:6`
- **Georg Sigismund Erbe** Pärnu parish entry — `EAA.1273.1.388:19`
- **Caroline Sophie Heimberger** death, Riga 1850 — LVVA book 7556, slide 105 #39
