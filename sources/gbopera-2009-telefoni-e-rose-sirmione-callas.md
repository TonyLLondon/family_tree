# Incom — "Telefoni e rose per la villa della Callas"

Reportage by **Savino Mariani**, published in the weekly *Incom*, **10 May 1958**. Reproduced with original photographs on [GBOPERA (November 2009)](https://www.gbopera.it/2009/11/telefoni-e-rose-a-sirmione/).

Names the full ownership chain of Villa Giannantoni, Via Caio Valerio Catullo 7, Sirmione:

1. **Giannantoni** — Lombard industrialist, built the villa ~1928
2. **Antonio Zerauschek** — "industriale zaratino, ora trasferitosi in quel di Firenze"
3. **Angelo Marinoni** — electric-oven manufacturer, Verona (immediate post-war)
4. **Comm. Battista Meneghini** — Veronese brick/tile industrialist, bought December 1957 for ~60 million lire, gift for his wife **Maria Callas**

Describes Callas's April 1958 visit directing restoration: telephones in every room, hundreds of rose varieties, travertine path to San Pietro in Mavino. Details include the villa's cadastral registration (3 floors, 21 rooms, 780 m²; park 7,552 m²).
