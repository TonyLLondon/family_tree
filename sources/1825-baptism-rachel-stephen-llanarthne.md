# Baptism of Rachel Stephen, Llanarthne, 1825

Parish register entry for the baptism of **Rachel**, daughter of **William** and **Mary Stephen**, at **Llanarthne**, Carmarthenshire, on **9 October 1825** (p. 81, No. 648). William Stephen was a farmer.

Rachel later married [Lewis Lewis](../people/lewis-lewis.md) of Aberavon in Q4 1856 (GRO, Neath district).
