# Marriage — David Griffiths × Margaret Davies, Llywell, 22 Dec 1815

**Summary:** David Griffiths married Margaret Davies at Llywell parish church (CofE), 22 December 1815. Residence: Llywell. Margaret's maiden name was **Davies**; she came from Cayo, Carmarthenshire.

**Archive:** Llywel parish register, held at the National Library of Wales, available on FamilySearch microfilm. Image restricted to FamilySearch Centers.
