Census schedule for the household of **[William Evans](../people/william-evans-i289.md)** and **[Dorothea Evans née Knecht](../people/dorothea-knecht.md)** in **Clerkenwell**, enumerated **5 April 1891**. Six persons: William (head, 33, brass dresser), Dorothea (wife, 30), and four children — D. (daughter, 12), W. (son, 9), K. (daughter, 3), B. (son, 1). All born London.
