# 1891 Census — Coolbear household, St Peter, Maldon

**George G Coolbear** (46, general dealer, born Hazeleigh) and **Alma** née Newcomb (37, born **Tolleshunt Knights, Essex**) with seven children at St Peter, Maldon. George's last census — he died in 1892, the year his youngest son Archibald Percy was born. Five new children appear since 1881; the three eldest from 1881 have left home.
