# 1851 Census — Morgan household, Coedefaur, Llanfairarybryn

**HO107/2470**, folio 395, page 3, schedule 6 — Coedefaur, Hamlet of Rhandir Ganol, Llanfairarybryn, Llandovery, Carmarthenshire.

**William Morgan** (head, 55, farmer of 263 acres) with wife **Sarah** (49), sons **David** (19) and **William Jr** (16, druggist apprentice), and William's widowed mother **Anne Morgan** (84). Three servants: John Jones (19), Hannah Davies (27), David Davies (63).

**Key finding:** Anne Morgan is explicitly listed as "Mother" to the head, confirming she is William's mother and a direct ancestor.
