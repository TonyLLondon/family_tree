# Certi Ricordi — Zerauschek-Addobbati family scrapbook

A 165-page family history scrapbook compiled by [Fulvia Zerauschek Lewis](../people/fulvia-ottilia-antonia-zerauschek.md) in the 1990s, titled **"Zerauschek - Addobbati: Certi Ricordi"** (Certain Memories). Combines handwritten Italian narrative, family photographs, postcards, business documents, and printed historical extracts into a curated visual family history of the Zerauschek-Addobbati world in [Zara](../topics/zara-italy-dalmatia.md).

The album opens with a Caesar epigraph — *"Meglio essere il primo al proprio paese... che il secondo a Roma!"* — and proceeds through the Addobbati lineage from 1495 Bergamo (via Sabalich's *Guida archeologica di Zara*), [Antonio Zerauschek](../people/antonio-zerauschek.md)'s business ventures ([Ausonia chocolate factory](../sources/zadarskitjednik-ausonia-tvornica-cokolade.md), cigarette manufacturing), life in interwar Zara, the Dalmatian exile, and later life in Hove.

**Companion source:** Fulvia's 1996 English-language memoir to PML — [famhist-nonna-memoir-1996](../sources/corpus/famhist-nonna-memoir-1996/extracted.pdf.md).
