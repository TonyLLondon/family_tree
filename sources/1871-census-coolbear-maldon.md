# 1871 Census — George G Coolbear, lodger, Wantz Road, Maldon

**George G Coolbear** (26, bill poster, born Hazeleigh, Essex) lodging alone at an inn on Wantz Road, St Peter, Maldon. Unmarried, no family present. This contradicts a prior-marriage theory for the two eldest children in the 1881 household.
