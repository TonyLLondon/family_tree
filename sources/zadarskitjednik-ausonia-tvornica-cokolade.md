# Zadarski tjednik — Ausonia chocolate factory photo-feuilleton (2024)

Croatian photo-feuilleton on the Voštarnica (Ceraria) quarter of Zadar. After the 1928 bridge over the Jazine inlet connected the old centre to the suburbs, the Zerauschek family built the **Fabbrica Cioccolato e Cacao Ausonia** and modern residential buildings in the area. The factory won numerous quality awards. The article calls it Zadar's "sweetest factory" — overshadowed in memory by the city's famous Maraschino liqueur industry.
