## Summary

Civil birth registration providing **initial discovery evidence** for:
- **Parents:** Francisco Cerpa + Bersabé Villegas
- **Birth:** 22 June 1890, Chanco

This record served as the **starting point** for the major CERPA lineage extension, establishing Francisco Cerpa as documented father and enabling systematic parent-tracing back to colonial ancestor Justo Cerpa (~1775).
