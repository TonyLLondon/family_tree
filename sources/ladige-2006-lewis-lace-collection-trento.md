# L'Adige (2006) — Lewis lace collection sold to Province of Trento

Two articles by Luisa Patruno in *L'Adige* (Trento), 28 December 2006, reporting the Province of Trento's purchase of 600 antique laces from the Lewis family for €120,000 — destined for the planned Museo del Tessuto at Palazzo Taddei, Ala. The collection, inherited from [Fulvia Zerauschek Lewis](../people/fulvia-ottilia-antonia-zerauschek.md), was sold in two lots of ~300 pieces each by sons [Ivor Anthony Samuel Lewis](../people/ivor-anthony-samuel-lewis.md) (born Florence, resident Bolsena, professor) and Owen Michael Lewis (lawyer, Twickenham). Appraised by Paolo Peri and Doretta Davanzo Poli; declared among the most important lace collections in Europe, with pieces previously auctioned at Sotheby's in London.
