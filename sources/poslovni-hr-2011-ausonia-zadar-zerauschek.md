# Poslovni dnevnik (2011) — Ausonia / Zerauschek chocolate history

Croatian business newspaper article (21 Dec 2011) about a new Zadar chocolatier, which provides the most explicit biographical description of Antonio Zerauschek found so far: **"trgovac, bankar, veleposjednik i tvorničar"** (trader, banker, large landowner, and factory owner). His Ausonia chocolate was exported to Italy and Western Europe from **1929**. The factory was closed by the incoming Yugoslav authorities after WWII. Also notes that the Luxardo liqueur firm (est. 1821 in Zadar) probably used Zerauschek's products for bonbon and chocolate production.
