# Obituary notices — Samuel Lewis (d. 7 Aug 1967)

Three newspaper clippings from South Wales papers, August 1967.

**English death notice** — died 7 August 1967 at Maesyffynnon, Aberaman, formerly of 32 Penydarren Street, Aberdare. Husband of Elizabeth Lily, father of David John and Brynmor Keith. Funeral Friday 11 August, 11 a.m. at Glyntaff Crematorium.

**Welsh chapel tribute** (Carmel, Trecynon) — by Mr D. Llewellyn Jones. Samuel was a deacon (Blaenor) for 35 years and served as corresponding secretary of the church.

**Aberdare Leader, 18 Aug 1967, p. 8** — "Church secretary for 30 years." Full obituary: aged 80; officiated by Rev. Glyndwr Richards (former Carmel pastor); Carmel Calvinistic Methodist Church, Trecynon; school superintendent 10 years, deacon from 1932, church secretary ~30 years; district manager at Scottish Legal Life Assurance Society until retirement 1954; Independent Order of Oddfellows, Lily of the Valley Lodge; Maesyffynnon was a care home (16-month residence); brother Tom Lewis; daughter-in-law Jeanette Lewis; named nephews and nieces.
