# 1901 Census — Glen Road, Aberdare (David John Lewis household)

Scan of the enumerator's book page from the **1901 Census of England & Wales**, taken **31 March 1901**. Nine persons at **Glen Road, Gadlys, Aberdare**: [David John Lewis (1857)](../people/david-john-lewis-1857.md) as head and coal miner; wife [Catherine](../people/catherine-griffiths.md); five children including [Samuel Lewis](../people/samuel-lewis.md) (age 13, already a coal miner) and infant Rachel (age 0); plus two boarders, both coal miners.

Notable absences: **Thomas Lewis** (I336, b. 1891) does not appear — dead before age 10. **Rachel Lewis** (I421) is an infant here but absent from the 1911 household — dead before age 10. Both contribute to Catherine's 1911 fertility return of twelve births and seven deaths.
