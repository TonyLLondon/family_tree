# Zara (1920–43) — Italian cigarette packet collection

Web article cataloguing every private tobacco manufacturer in Italian Zara, with provenance detail on ownership, addresses, brand lists, and post-war confiscation. Key facts: **Manifattura Zaratina Sigarette** acquired by Antonio Zerauschek on 27 May 1928 from Niccolò Peristeridis; brands included Calypso, Calitea, Diadora, Flavia, Lampo, Radio, Raminga, Samos, Si. Post-WWII: factory confiscated by Yugoslav authorities; company attempted reconstitution in Florence; Tribunale di Firenze annulled the resolution and the company ceased to exist.
