# 1871 Census — Clive Street, Aberdare (Griffiths household)

Enumerator's book for the 1871 Census of England & Wales, **2 April 1871**. Six persons at **Clive Street, Cwmdare, Aberdare**: [Thomas Griffiths](../people/thomas-griffiths.md) as head and ironworks labourer; wife [Anne](../people/anne-morgan.md); four children — Elizabeth (11), [Catherine](../people/catherine-griffiths.md) (9), Margaret (5), David (2). Anne's birthplace is given as "Llan Ferbyn, Carmarthenshire".
