# 1891 Census — Martin household, Orpington

**Peter Martin** (48, chimney sweep, born Woolwich) and **Clara** (40) with seven sons and infant daughter **Rosina** at 1 Alfrons Cottages, Orpington, Kent. A chimney sweep family: Peter Sr, Peter Jr (20), and Arthur (13) are all sweeps. **Rosina Martin** — who later married Archibald Percy Coolbear (also a chimney sweep) — is an infant. **Edward Martin** (3) is the brother-in-law who appears in the 1921 Coolbear household.
