## Summary

Analyses the **1806 French census** of the Zara noble and citizen councils conducted at the onset of the Illyrian Provinces regime. Records the composition of the *Consiglio dei cittadini* (citizen council) in that year, family by family.

**Addobbati entries:** Six Addobbati are listed among active members of the Zara citizen council in 1806:

- Anđelo (Angelo)
- Ante (Antonio)
- Dominik
- Petar
- Salustio
- Vicko (Vincenzo)

This is the densest simultaneous documentary presence of the family in the civic record.

## Use in this research

Confirms the Addobbati as an established and numerous *cittadini* family at the moment of the French takeover. The six-member simultaneous listing demonstrates that the family was not diminished by the transition from Venice to France; they were embedded across the citizen council.
