# 1911 Census — Fensom household, 9 Hope Street, Lower Holloway

**David William Fensom** (46, plumber) and **Minnie Flora Pope** (45, born **Cranbrook, Kent**) with eight of their eleven children at 9 Hope Street, Lower Holloway. The three eldest (David Jr, Minnie Jr, Annie) have left home. Fred (20) is a painter; four new daughters have arrived since 1901. David William signed the form as "Fensom" — the "Fenson" spelling is purely an indexing error.
