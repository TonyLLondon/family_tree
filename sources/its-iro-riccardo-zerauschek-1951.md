# ITS / IRO — Riccardo Zerauschek displaced persons file (Trieste, 1951)

Seven-image Arolsen Archives (International Tracing Service) file. ITS Nr. **S-1920**. IRO Application for Assistance + interview report + filing card.

**Subject:** [Riccardo Zerauschek](../people/riccardo-zerauschek.md) (b. 17 Jun 1896, Zara), with wife **Tatiana née Machiedo** (b. 16 Mar 1912, Zara), children **Nora** (b. 14 Apr 1933), **Renato** (b. 5 Oct 1934), **Luciano** (b. 3 Jun 1939). Father: [Antonio Zerauschek (senior)](../people/antonio-zerauschek-senior.md) (deceased). Mother: [Anna Herenda](../people/anna-herenda.md).

Key content: wartime escape from Zara to Lošinj Grande and Silba; employment as singer with Opera at Split and Zagreb; exit to Trieste (April 1947) on pretext of continuing singing studies; confirms brother ([Antonio Zerauschek](../people/antonio-zerauschek.md)) held Ford, Firestone, Zenith agencies; political statement opposing fascism, Nazism, and communism.
