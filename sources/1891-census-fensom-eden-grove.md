# 1891 Census — Fensom household, 11 Eden Grove, Islington

Household of **David William Fensom** (26, lime worker) and **Minnie** née Pope (25, born Cranbrook, Kent) with three young children — David (4), Minnie (2), and Fred (infant) — at 11 Eden Grove, Lower Holloway, Islington. The extended Fensom family would remain on this street: David's parents were at 13 Eden Grove by 1901.
