# Vera Obolensky — Russian émigré, Mahmoudieh guesthouse

Source cluster for **Vera Obolensky** (née Nemtchinova), Russian émigré who managed the **Mahmoudieh guesthouse** on **Dr. Étienne Stump**'s estate in 1920s Tehran, and later became Stump's wife.

## Key memoir source (not yet in repo)

**Georges Obolensky**, *Forever Russian: Memoirs of a Vagabond Prince* (AuthorHouse, 15 Feb 2013, 176 pp). First-hand memoir by Vera's son. Primary source for the guesthouse (40+ rooms, European business travelers), the flight from Russia, Prince Yuri's death, and Vera's marriage to Stump.
